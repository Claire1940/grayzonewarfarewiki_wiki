### Economy and Crafting Systems

Resourcefulness will be a cornerstone of survival in Lamang during 2026. The roadmap emphasizes gathering materials and refining crafting techniques. This suggests a more robust and essential crafting system, moving beyond simple item repairs to potentially allowing players to construct complex tools, customize weapons more extensively, or even build temporary shelters. The economy will likely adapt to support this, with a greater emphasis on raw materials and specialized components.

For instance, the Update 0.4 already rebalanced vendor stock, making high-end items purchasable with unlimited stock and adjusting prices. This sets a precedent for a dynamic economy where players might need to choose between buying ready-made gear or investing in crafting for potentially superior or custom alternatives.

### Enhanced Tactical Depth

Stealth mechanics are explicitly mentioned as an area for mastery in 2026. This implies significant improvements to how players can move undetected, utilize cover, and execute silent takedowns. Coupled with the existing dynamic weather and day/night cycles, stealth will offer a crucial advantage for those who prefer a more calculated approach to engagements.

Furthermore, the "Rising Tensions" theme suggests that the power struggle between factions will be more deeply integrated into gameplay. Players' actions could directly influence the territorial control, resource availability, and even the narrative direction for their faction, making every mission a contribution to a larger, ongoing conflict.
