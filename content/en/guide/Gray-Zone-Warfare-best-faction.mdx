<Section icon={Users} title="Understanding Gray Zone Warfare Factions">
In Gray Zone Warfare, selecting your Private Military Company (PMC) faction is one of the first and most critical decisions you'll make. This choice dictates your initial spawn location, influences early task routes, and can significantly impact your overall experience on Lamang Island. Each of the three PMCs – Crimson Shield International, Mithras Security Systems, and Lamang Recovery Initiative – offers a distinct starting point and strategic advantages.

The game's progression is heavily tied to your faction, particularly regarding initial tasks and vendor access. While the core gameplay loop remains consistent, the geographical advantages and challenges presented by each faction's starting town can lead to vastly different early-game experiences.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Faction Choice">
