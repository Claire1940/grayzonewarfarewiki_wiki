## Finding the MCX Nomad Blueprint

The centerpiece of the 0.4 update is the MCX Ghost Nomad, an LVAW-style build chambered in .300 Blackout. This integrally suppressed short-barrel beast is perfect for stealth operations, though it requires specific ammunition to shine. To unlock this weapon for purchase at Banshee, you must locate a specific red "Guns and Ammo" magazine hidden within the dangerous Midnight Sapphire region.

### Midnight Sapphire Hotel Location
The blueprint is located within the main hotel complex at Midnight Sapphire. This area is heavily guarded by elite AI wearing Tier 3+ armor and carrying AK-12s. Approach with extreme caution, preferably at night using white phosphorus night vision goggles.

| Floor | Specific Room/Area | Item Description |
|---|---|---|
| **2nd or 3rd Floor** | Main Lobby / Bar Area | Red "Guns and Ammo" Magazine |
| **Furniture** | Circular Coffee Table | Located near the center lounge |
| **Requirement** | Banshee Level 4 | Must turn in the magazine to unlock |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The AI in the hotel are no joke. They utilize grenades and high-penetration ammo. Ensure your squad is geared with at least Class 4 plates before attempting this retrieval.

## Navigating the Gray Zone Warfare Blueprint Map
While "blueprint" often refers to weapon unlocks, the **gray zone warfare blueprint map** also encompasses the strategic points of interest (POIs) where these items and high-value loot spawn. In 2026, the map has been categorized into Minor COPs (single star) and Major COPs (triple star).

### The Secret Vendor: Vulture
One of the most significant additions is Vulture, a secret trader who does not appear on your standard UI. Vulture rotates between two of the seven Major COPs every week. To track his current location, players often use community tools like `whereisvulture.com`.

Vulture is notoriously difficult to level up because he buys items for lower values than other traders. However, reaching Level 4 with him grants access to a massive **108-slot safe**, which is invaluable for storing high-value items like watches and jewelry without cluttering your main inventory.

## Trader Reputation and Economy
In the 2026 update, you no longer rely solely on tasks to gain reputation. You can now earn rep by selling high-value items to specific traders. This is particularly useful for Artisan, Turncoat, and Banshee, who typically have fewer missions than Gunny or Handshake.

| Action | Reputation Gain | Strategy |

| **Complete Main Story Quests** | Variable | Best for unlocking Banshee and Gunny early |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling with a specific trader's missions, head to high-loot areas like the Sawmill or Hunter's Paradise. A single successful raid with a full backpack of high-tier attachments can grant more reputation than two or three early-game tasks.

## Tactical Combat Adjustments for 2026
Recent changes to ballistics have made headshots feel inconsistent for many players. This is often due to the "height over bore" mechanic, where the bullet exits the barrel lower than the optic's line of sight.

### Aiming and Ballistics
If you are using an Elcan or similar scope zeroed to 100m, your shots may land low at 50m. Furthermore, the head hitbox is tightly constrained; hitting the neck often counts as a thorax shot, which may not be fatal if the spine isn't hit.

**Recommended Combat Tweaks:**
1.  **Aim for the Top of the Helmet:** At short to medium ranges, aiming for the very top of an enemy's head ensures that even if the bullet drops slightly, it still connects with the cranial hitbox.
2.  **Use Longer Barrels:** Increasing your barrel length improves muzzle velocity, resulting in a flatter trajectory and more consistent hits on the **gray zone warfare blueprint map**.
3.  **Silence Headsets (Key O):** When riding the helicopter, press 'O' to dampen the engine noise. This allows you to maintain communication with your squad without being deafened.

## Helicopter Logistics
The helicopter system has seen significant improvements. You no longer need to "pre-book" slots as aggressively, as birds can now enter from the sides of the map.

*   **Calling the Bird:** Call your extraction when you are within 100m of the LZ. If you call it from further away, the timer increases significantly (up to 2 minutes and 30 seconds).
*   **Expedited Takeoff:** Once seated, hold the **L key** to signal the pilot to take off immediately. This is vital when the LZ is hot.
*   **Invulnerability:** You are invulnerable while seated on a moving helicopter and for 5 seconds after disembarking to prevent LZ camping.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the MCX Nomad blueprint magazine?**

A: It is a red "Guns and Ammo" magazine located on a circular coffee table in the lobby/bar area of the Midnight Sapphire hotel, usually on the 2nd or 3rd floor.

**Q: How do I unlock the secret vendor Vulture on the gray zone warfare blueprint map?**

A: Vulture does not need to be unlocked via a quest, but he only appears at two specific Major COPs (triple-star locations) which rotate every week. You must physically travel to these locations to trade with him.

**Q: Why are my headshots not killing AI in one hit?**

A: This is likely due to a combination of height-over-bore issues and the tight head hitbox. Aim slightly higher than the center of the head, preferably at the top of the helmet, to compensate for the bullet's trajectory.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get reputation with Banshee?**

A: Beyond missions, the fastest way is to sell high-value loot to him. Every $10,000 in sales grants 100 reputation points. Focus on looting high-tier attachments and electronics from Midnight Sapphire or Tiger Bay.
