## Harvest Moon Quest Objectives Overview

The Harvest Moon task is primarily an intelligence-gathering mission. You are tasked with locating four specific poppy fields and interacting with documents or folders left behind by the local drug manufacturing operations. These locations are spread across the southern regions of the map, often placing you in the crosshairs of aggressive AI patrols.

| Objective | General Location | Item to Find | Key Landmark |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **First Field** | South of Lima 2 | Paper on a table | Near YBO2 |
| **Second Field** | South of Lima 2 | Paper in a house | Left-side structure |
| **Third Field** | South of Ghost 3 | Yellow folder | Near Ground Zero |
| **Fourth Field** | South of Ground Zero | Document on crate | Green military crate |

> **Warning:** The areas surrounding these poppy fields are frequently patrolled by AI with high-tier weaponry. Ensure you are wearing at least Tier 3 body armor and carrying sufficient ammunition before attempting this run.

## Detailed Walkthrough: All Document Locations

To complete the **grayzone warfare harvest moon quest**, you must visit four distinct points of interest. While the map markers provide a general area, the actual documents are often tucked away inside buildings or on small pieces of furniture that are easy to overlook during a firefight.

### 1. The Southern Poppy Field (Lima 2)
Your first stop is located in the southernmost region of the active operations zone. Head south from the Lima 2 landing zone toward the coordinates near YBO2. You are looking for a poppy field situated in a valley. In the center of this field, you will find a makeshift wooden table. The first document is a simple piece of paper sitting directly on top of this table. Press your interact key (default 'F') to read and secure the intel.

### 2. The Left House Search
Still within the vicinity of the first poppy field south of Lima 2, look for a cluster of small buildings. There are two primary structures here. You need to enter the house on the left side (when facing north). Inside, search the main room for another piece of paper. This document provides further context on the harvest operations and is required to progress the quest.

### 3. The Ghost 3 Yellow Folder
The third objective takes you toward the dangerous outskirts of Ground Zero. Navigate south from the Ghost 3 landing zone. This area is characterized by denser vegetation and more verticality. You are looking for a yellow folder located on a table near a small outpost. This folder is a "high-value" intelligence item, so ensure your surroundings are clear before standing still to interact with it.

### 4. The Ground Zero Green Crate
The final piece of the puzzle is located directly south of the Ground Zero landmark. This is arguably the most dangerous part of the **grayzone warfare harvest moon quest** due to the high concentration of elite AI. Search for a green military crate sitting near the edge of the poppy plantation. The document is resting on top of this crate. Once you read this, your objective list should update, signaling that you are ready for extraction.

## Unlocking the Secret Vendor: Vulture

While completing tasks like the **grayzone warfare harvest moon quest**, you will naturally accumulate high-value loot and military equipment. In the 0.4 update, players can now interact with a secret vendor named **Vulture**. Unlike standard vendors who stay at your base, Vulture is a scavenger who moves between "Major COPs" (Command Outposts) on a weekly rotation.

### Finding Vulture's Location
Vulture can be found at Major COPs, which are identified on your map by three black dots rather than the standard single dot. His rotation includes the following locations:
*   Harrison
*   Titan
*   Westmore
*   Stalwart
*   Crusader
*   Bronco
*   Nomad

To access Vulture, your faction must currently control the COP where he is located. If the computer terminal at the COP is yellow, the area is captured and Vulture will be available for trading.

### Why Level Up Vulture?
Vulture sells scavenged gear from fallen PMCs, which often includes high-tier weapons and attachments that you might not have unlocked through Handshake or Gunny yet. However, the primary reason to reach Level 3 and Level 4 reputation with him is to purchase **Safes**.

| Safe Type | Level Required | Storage Size | Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Small Safe** | Level 3 | 48 Slots | Stores money and jewelry |
| **Large Safe** | Level 4 | 108 Slots | Massive storage for high-value loot |

> **Tip:** Vulture does not accept standard weapons for reputation. To level him up, you must sell him "Military Equipment" and high-tier valuables found in areas like Midnight Sapphire or during the **grayzone warfare harvest moon quest**.

## Tactical Recommendations for Success

Completing the **grayzone warfare harvest moon quest** in 2026 requires more than just knowing the locations. The AI in Gray Zone Warfare has been significantly tuned to be more reactive to sound and light.

1.  **Suppression is Key:** Use a suppressor on your primary weapon (preferably a 5.56 or 7.62 platform). Unsuppressed shots will draw every AI within a 300-meter radius to your position.
2.  **Night Operations:** If you have access to NVGs, attempting this quest at night can be safer, as AI detection ranges are slightly reduced. However, be wary of flashlights giving away your position.
3.  **Inventory Management:** Since you will likely find high-value "Military Equipment" while searching the poppy fields, ensure you have at least a medium-sized backpack to carry items back for Vulture.
4.  **Extraction Planning:** Don't wait until you are out of water or ammo to head to an LZ. The trek from Ground Zero to the nearest extraction point is long and filled with potential ambush spots.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the second document in the grayzone warfare harvest moon quest?**

A: The second document is located in a small house south of Lima 2. When you see the cluster of two buildings near the poppy field, enter the house on the left. The paper is usually on a small table or desk inside.

**Q: Can I complete the Harvest Moon quest in PvE mode?**

A: Yes, the quest objectives and document locations are identical in both PvP and PvE modes. In PvE, you only need to worry about the AI patrols, whereas in PvP, other factions may be camping the document locations.

**Q: What should I sell to Vulture to increase my reputation quickly?**

A: Vulture prefers high-value "Military Equipment" and rare loot items. Selling him items like gold watches, expensive electronics, or specific military-grade hardware found in high-tier zones is the fastest way to reach Level 4.

**Q: Why can't I see Vulture at the Command Outpost (COP)?**

A: There are two possibilities: either your faction does not currently own that COP (check if the terminal is yellow), or Vulture has rotated to a different Major COP for the week. Check the other "three-dot" locations on your map.
