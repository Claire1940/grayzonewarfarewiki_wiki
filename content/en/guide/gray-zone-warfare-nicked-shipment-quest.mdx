## Quest Overview and Objectives

The "Nicked Shipment" quest is typically issued by Gunny, the primary weapons and ammunition vendor. He’s lost a shipment of supplies near the local gas station in your faction's starting town. Your job is to locate the specific vehicle, confirm the contents, and report back.

| Objective | Location | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Locate the Shipment** | Local Gas Station | Find the white delivery truck |
| **Search the Cargo** | Truck Interior | Interact with the shipment |
| **Extract Safely** | Nearest LZ | Survive the return trip to Base |

> **💡 Tip:** Before heading out, ensure you have accepted the task in your "Tasks" menu. If you forget to click "Accept," you won't be able to interact with the objective in the field.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### 1. Reaching the Gas Station
Deploy to the Landing Zone (LZ) closest to your faction's starter town. Once you land, move toward the gas station. Be cautious, as the gas station is a high-traffic area for both AI and enemy PMCs.

### 2. Clearing the Area
AI guards often patrol the perimeter of the pumps and the convenience store. Use the surrounding walls and vehicles for cover. If you are using the M870, wait for the AI to get within 15–20 meters for maximum effectiveness. Avoid long-range duels unless you brought a scoped secondary weapon.

### 3. Locating the White Truck
The shipment is located in the back of a white delivery truck parked near the gas station. 
*   Approach the rear of the truck.
*   Look for the prompt to "Search" or "Inspect" the crates.
*   Once the UI updates your task status, you are ready to leave.

### 4. Healing and Maintenance
If you took damage during the fight, use your medical kit immediately. The 2026 medical system requires specific items for different wounds:

| Wound Type | Required Medical Item |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Heavy Bleeding** | **CT (Tourniquet)** |
| **Medium/Light Wounds** | **Bandage** |
| **Fractures** | **Splint** |
| **Organ Damage** | **Surikit (Surgery Kit)** |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore organ damage. If your lungs or stomach are "yellow" or "red" in the health menu, your stamina recovery will be severely penalized, making extraction difficult.

## Unlocking the Secret Vendor: Vulture

Completing quests like the **gray zone warfare nicked shipment quest** increases your reputation with Gunny, but it also paves the way for discovering "Vulture." Vulture is a secret vendor added in the 0.4 update who specializes in reselling gear scavenged from dead PMCs.

Vulture doesn't stay in one place; he rotates weekly between "Major COPs" (Capture Points) marked with three black dots on your map. Popular locations include **Harrison** and **Titan**. To access his inventory, your faction must currently control the COP where he is stationed.

### Why Level Up Vulture?
While Gunny provides your standard ammunition, Vulture offers high-value storage solutions once you reach higher loyalty tiers:
*   **Level 3:** Unlocks the Small Safe.
*   **Level 4:** Unlocks the Large Safe (108 slots for money and high-value loot).

To level him up, you must sell him specific military equipment or high-value items found in locations like Midnight Sapphire.

## Advanced Tactics for the Nicked Shipment Quest

If you are struggling with the **gray zone warfare nicked shipment quest** in a high-population PvP server, consider these advanced strategies:

1.  **Night Operations:** Use the cover of darkness. AI detection ranges are slightly reduced, and players without Night Vision Goggles (NVGs) will have a harder time spotting you near the truck.
2.  **The Flank:** Most players approach the gas station from the main road. Try circling around through the woods or back alleys to catch the AI (and players) off guard.
3.  **Suppressors:** Even a basic suppressor on a handgun or rifle can prevent the entire town's AI from swarming your position once you fire your first shot.
4.  **Looting Keys:** While at the gas station, check the pockets of every AI you kill. They often carry keys for nearby houses or storage rooms that contain high-value loot or are required for subsequent quests.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the truck for the gray zone warfare nicked shipment quest?**

A: The truck is a white delivery vehicle located at the gas station in your faction's starting town (Nam Thaven, Khoa, or Mithras). It is usually parked near the side or rear of the main building.

**Q: Can I complete this quest in a squad?**

A: Yes. As long as every squad member has the quest accepted, everyone can interact with the truck to receive credit. However, you must all extract safely to turn in the mission.

**Q: What happens if I die after finding the shipment?**

A: If the objective was to "Locate" or "Inspect," the progress usually saves. However, if there was a physical item you needed to pick up, you will lose it upon death and must return to the gas station to retrieve it again.

**Q: Is the M870 really the best gun for this mission?**

A: In the 2026 version 0.4 meta, the M870 is the most cost-effective tool for starting towns. It saves you money on expensive 5.56mm ammo while providing reliable stopping power against unarmored AI.
