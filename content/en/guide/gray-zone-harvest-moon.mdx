Navigating the treacherous jungles of Lamang requires more than just a steady aim; it demands keen investigative skills and strategic planning. One of the most challenging mid-game missions you will encounter is the **gray zone harvest moon** task, assigned by the vendor Banshee. This mission sends players deep into enemy territory to uncover the illicit poppy production operations fueling the local conflict. Successfully completing **gray zone harvest moon** requires visiting four distinct hidden poppy fields scattered across the map, each guarded by hostile AI and containing critical intelligence documents.

In this guide, we will break down the exact coordinates for every objective, provide tactical advice for surviving the encounters, and detail the specific items you need to interact with to progress. Whether you are playing solo or with a squad, understanding the layout of these fields is essential for a swift extraction.

## Task Overview and Objectives

Before heading out, ensure you have accepted the mission from Banshee. The primary goal is to investigate the "Hidden Poppy Fields" and report your findings. This isn't just a simple scouting mission; you must physically locate and read specific documents at each site to trigger the task updates.

| Objective | Requirement | Location Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Field 1** | Read "Labor Allocation Request" | East of F.A.L.N.G. Airfield |
| **Investigate Field 2** | Read "Weekly Shipment Note" | South of Falcon Area |
| **Investigate Field 3** | Read "Protective Oversight Agreement" | Near Ground Zero / Ghost 3 |
| **Investigate Field 4** | Read "Harvest Discipline Notice" | South of Lima 2 / YBL-1 |

> **Warning:** Several of these locations are high-traffic areas for both AI and rival PMCs. Expect resistance, especially at Field 3, where AI density is notably higher.

## Detailed Location Guide for Gray Zone Harvest Moon

To complete the **gray zone harvest moon** mission efficiently, it is recommended to tackle these locations in a logical circuit based on your faction's closest Landing Zones (LZs). Below are the precise details for each hidden field.

### 1. Hidden Poppy Field #1 (Coordinates 192 154)
Located to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and southeast of the Nomad area, this field is relatively open. You are looking for a small table situated near the center of the poppy rows. On this table, you will find a piece of paper titled "Labor Allocation Request." Press your interact key (default 'F') to read it and update your quest log.

### 2. Hidden Poppy Field #2 (Coordinates 178 152)
This field is situated south of the Falcon area. Unlike the first field, the intel here is located inside a structure. Look for an open-sided building or shed near the crops. Inside, you will find the "Weekly Shipment Note." This area occasionally features locked rooms; if you have found keys in the vicinity previously, check the nearby doors for high-tier medical supplies or weapon parts.

### 3. Hidden Poppy Field #3 (Coordinates 172 132)
This is often considered the most difficult stage of the **gray zone harvest moon** task due to the distance and the AI presence. Located south of Ghost 3 and near the Ground Zero perimeter, you should expect to engage at least 6 to 10 armed insurgents. The document, a green folder labeled "Protective Oversight Agreement," is located on a crate within an open structure.

### 4. Hidden Poppy Field #4 (Coordinates 148 117)
The final field is located at the far southern end of the map, south of the Lima 2 LZ and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. You will find a residential-style house adjacent to the poppy fields. Walk up to the front porch; at the end of the porch, there is a book titled "Harvest Discipline Notice." Reading this will complete the investigation phase of the mission.

## Tactical Recommendations for Success

When attempting the **gray zone harvest moon** objectives, your loadout should balance mobility with protection. Because you will be traveling through dense foliage and open fields, a suppressed weapon is highly recommended to avoid drawing the entire map's AI to your position.

*   **Suppression is Key:** Use a suppressor on your primary rifle (M4A1 or AK-74 variant) to pick off guards at the poppy fields without alerting nearby patrols.
*   **Long-Range Optics:** Fields 1 and 3 offer long sightlines. A 4x or 6x optic will allow you to clear the area before moving in to read the documents.
*   **Stamina Management:** The walk to Field 3 (172 132) is a "haul," as many players describe it. Ensure you have adequate water and food, and keep your weight in the "Green" or "Yellow" zone to maintain stamina.
*   **Check for Loot:** As mentioned by veteran players, Fields 2 and 4 often have structures with locked doors. These rooms frequently contain rare loot that can make the long trip much more profitable.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing in a squad, have one player pull security on the perimeter while another interacts with the documents. The animations for reading can leave you vulnerable for several seconds.

## Completing the Task

Once all four documents have been read and your task list shows all objectives as "Completed," you must return to your base camp. Open your vendor menu, select Banshee, and hand in the quest. The rewards for **gray zone harvest moon** typically include a significant amount of reputation with Banshee, currency, and occasionally high-quality armor or ammunition.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order for the Gray Zone Harvest Moon task?**

A: No, you can visit the four poppy fields in any order that is convenient for your flight path or current position on the map. However, many players find it easiest to start at the northernmost field and work their way south.

**Q: What happens if I die after reading a document but before reaching the next field?**

A: Your progress for the documents you have already read is saved. You do not need to revisit fields you have already successfully investigated, but you must survive long enough for the "Task Updated" notification to appear on your screen.

**Q: Are there specific keys required for the Gray Zone Harvest Moon mission?**

A: No specific keys are required to complete the main objectives of investigating the fields. However, having local keys for the sheds and houses near the poppy fields can provide access to bonus loot and supplies.

**Q: Why isn't the document appearing at the third poppy field (172 132)?**

A: Ensure you are looking at the correct crate. The "Protective Oversight Agreement" is a green folder that can sometimes be obscured by other environmental assets or shadows. If it still doesn't appear, try clearing the AI in the immediate vicinity, as sometimes proximity to combat can interfere with interaction prompts.
