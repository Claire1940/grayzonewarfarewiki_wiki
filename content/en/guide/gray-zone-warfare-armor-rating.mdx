### The NIJ Ballistic Hierarchy

In Gray Zone Warfare, ballistic ratings scale from Level IIA up to Level III+ [4q-5eqAPGJQ, 3245764171]. While Level IV armor exists in the real-world NIJ standard, it is not yet implemented in the game [4q-5eqAPGJQ, 3245764171]. 

The modifiers attached to these numbers are critical:
- **The Plus (+) Modifier**: Indicates the armor is upgraded, offering protection greater than the base level but slightly less than the next tier up [4q-5eqAPGJQ, 3245764171].
- **The A Modifier**: Indicates a downgrade. An "A" rating is always worse than its standard counterpart in the same tier (e.g., IIIA is inferior to III) [4q-5eqAPGJQ, 3245764171].

| NIJ Level | Relative Protection | Ballistic Capability | Game Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| IIA | Very Low | Stops low-velocity 9mm and .40 S&W | Common low-tier |
| II | Low | Standard 9mm and .357 Magnum | Basic security |
| IIIA | Moderate | High-velocity 9mm and .44 Magnum | Mid-tier pistol rated |
| IIIA+ | Moderate-High | Upgraded pistol protection | Helmet-tier maximum |
| III | High | Stops 7.62x39mm and 7.62x51mm FMJ | Standard rifle rated |
| III+ | Very High | Advanced rifle protection | Current peak meta |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ballistic Warning">
