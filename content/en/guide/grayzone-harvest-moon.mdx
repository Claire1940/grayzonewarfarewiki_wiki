## Task Overview: Harvest Moon

Before heading out, ensure you have accepted the quest from Banshee. The primary objective is to "Locate and investigate all hidden poppy fields and report your findings." Unlike simple "kill and collect" missions, this task is an investigation. You don't necessarily need to extract items, but you must interact with (read/translate) specific documents found at each site to trigger the task update.

| Objective | Location | Key Interaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Investigate Field 1** | 192 154 | Read Labor Allocation Request |
| **Investigate Field 2** | 178 152 | Read Weekly Shipment Note |
| **Investigate Field 3** | 172 132 | Translate Protective Oversight Agreement |
| **Investigate Field 4** | 148 117 | Translate Harvest Discipline Notice |

> **💡 Tip:** This task involves significant travel across the map. It is highly recommended to bring at least three bottles of water and two food items, as the "haul" between fields 2 and 3 can be exhausting for your character’s hydration levels.

## Detailed Poppy Field Locations

The **grayzone harvest moon** mission is unique because it spans a large geographical area, moving from the eastern airfield sector all the way to the southern YBL-1 corridor. Following the locations in a logical order will save you significant time and fuel.

### Field 1: The Airfield Outskirts (192 154)
The first poppy field is situated to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and southeast of the Nomad landing zone. This area is relatively open, making you a target for long-range AI patrols.

1.  Navigate to coordinates **192 154**.
2.  Look for a small cluster of makeshift structures and tables in the center of the flowers.
3.  On a wooden table, you will find a piece of paper titled **Labor Allocation Request**.
4.  Interact with the paper to update your task progress.

### Field 2: South of Falcon (178 152)
The second location is located just south of the Falcon area (F.A.L.N.G.). If you are coming from Field 1, this is a short westward trek.

1.  Move to coordinates **178 152**.
2.  Locate the open-sided building (a shed-like structure) near the edge of the field.
3.  Inside, look for the **Weekly Shipment Note**.
4.  Read the note to trigger the second update for the **grayzone harvest moon** quest.

## Crossing Lamang: Reaching the Southern Fields

The transition from the northern airfield sector to the southern fields is where many players struggle. Field 3 is located near Ground Zero, which is often a "hot" zone for both high-level AI and rival PMCs.

### Field 3: Ground Zero Perimeter (172 132)
This is arguably the most dangerous part of the **grayzone harvest moon** task. The AI density here is significantly higher than at the first two fields. Expect to encounter groups of 5-8 hostiles guarding the perimeter.

1.  Approach coordinates **172 132** with caution.
2.  Clear the AI before attempting to interact with the objective.
3.  Locate a green folder titled **Protective Oversight Agreement** resting on a crate or table in the open area.
4.  Select "Translate" and then "Close" to ensure the task registers.

### Field 4: The Southern House (148 117)
The final field is located far south, below the Lima 2 landing zone and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. This area is heavily forested, so watch your flanks for bush-wookie AI.

1.  Head to coordinates **148 117**.
2.  Find the residential-style house situated near the poppy crop.
3.  Walk onto the front porch. At the far end, you will find a book titled **Harvest Discipline Notice**.
4.  Translate the document to complete the final objective of the mission.

## Tactical Preparation for Harvest Moon

Completing the **grayzone harvest moon** task in 2026 requires more than just knowing where to go; you need the right gear to survive the journey. Since you are visiting four different locations, your weight and stamina management are crucial.

### Recommended Loadout
*   **Weapon:** A suppressed assault rifle (like the M4A1 or AK-47) is vital. Stealth allows you to pick off guards at Field 3 without alerting the entire Ground Zero sector.
*   **Optics:** Use a 1-4x or 1-6x variable scope. The poppy fields are wide open, but the approaches are often through dense jungle.
*   **Armor:** Level III soft armor with plates is sufficient for the local insurgents, but don't skimp if you expect PMC interference.
*   **Storage:** You don't need much backpack space for quest items, so prioritize a rig with plenty of ammunition and medical supplies.

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to rush Field 3 (172 132) without clearing the surrounding bushes. The AI in this sector often uses high-caliber SKS rifles that can penetrate basic armor from a distance.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to find the documents in a specific order for the grayzone harvest moon task?**

A: No, you can visit the four poppy fields in any order. However, the most efficient path is starting at the Airfield (Field 1) and working your way south toward YBL-1 (Field 4).

**Q: Can I complete this task in a squad?**

A: Yes. As long as each squad member interacts with the documents (reading or translating them), everyone will receive credit for the investigation. It is actually safer to do this in a squad, especially for the Ground Zero location.

**Q: What happens if I die after finding two of the documents?**

A: Your progress is saved per document. If you have already updated your task for Field 1 and Field 2, you only need to reach Field 3 and Field 4 in your next raid. You do not need to revisit the first two locations.

**Q: Are there any keys required for the grayzone harvest moon mission?**

A: No keys are required to access the four main investigation documents. However, some poppy fields have nearby locked rooms that contain high-value loot, so bringing a "Master Key" or specific area keys can make the trip more profitable.

**Q: Why isn't the task updating when I stand in the poppy field?**

A: Simply being in the field is not enough. You must find the specific physical item (paper, folder, or book) and interact with it (usually by pressing 'F' to read or translate). Ensure the "Task Updated" notification appears on your UI before moving to the next coordinate.
