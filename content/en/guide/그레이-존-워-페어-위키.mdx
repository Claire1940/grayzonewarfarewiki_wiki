The primary reason veteran players seek out Vulture is for his high-level rewards. ## Tactical Strategy for Farming Vulture XP

To reach Level 4 with Vulture quickly, you need a consistent farming route. The most effective method in 2026 involves "Midnight Sapphire" runs. The guards in this area drop military equipment that Vulture accepts. 

1.  **Infiltration**: Enter Midnight Sapphire from the northern ridge to avoid the main gate bottlenecks.
2.  **Clearing**: Focus on the elite guards patrolling the villa and the hotel. They have a higher chance of carrying the "Military Equipment" items Vulture craves.
3.  **Extraction**: Use the nearest LZ once your backpack is full of 1x1 and 1x2 high-value items. 
4.  **The Sale**: Travel to the current Major Cop where Vulture is located. Capture it if necessary, then dump your entire haul into his "Sell" tab.

By repeating this process, you can gain approximately 700 to 1,000 reputation points per successful raid, depending on the rarity of the loot found.
