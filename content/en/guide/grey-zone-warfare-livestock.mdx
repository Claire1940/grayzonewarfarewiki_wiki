Navigating the dangerous jungles and industrial ruins of Lamang requires more than just a steady aim; it requires keen investigative skills. The **grey zone warfare livestock** task, assigned by the vendor Banshee, is a multi-stage objective introduced in the 0.4 update that challenges players to infiltrate a car repair facility to recover sensitive documents. Whether you are playing for the Lamang Recovery Initiative, Mithras Security Force, or Crimson Shield International, completing the **grey zone warfare livestock** mission is essential for progressing through Banshee's high-tier equipment unlocks.

This guide provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the operation, including the exact locations of the hidden key code, the locked office, and tactical advice for surviving the hostile AI presence in the area. Following these steps will ensure you spend less time wandering and more time extracting with the intel.

## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out into the field, it is vital to understand what Banshee expects from you. This task is not a simple "search and destroy" mission; it involves environmental interaction and puzzle-solving. You will be operating in a zone typically populated by mid-to-high-tier AI combatants, so coming prepared with the right gear is non-negotiable.

> **Warning:** The area surrounding the car repair shop is often patrolled by multiple AI squads. Ensure you have at least two spare magazines and basic medical supplies before attempting this task.

## Step 1: Locating the Cages and Shack

The first phase of the **grey zone warfare livestock** mission requires you to identify the primary site of interest. Your destination is a small wooden shack situated near a series of animal cages. This location is found in the southwest corner of the car repair shop complex.

Upon arriving at the marked location on your map, look for the peripheral fence of the repair shop. Instead of entering through the main gate, circle around to the bottom-left (southwest) corner. You will see a small, weathered wooden shack and several cages that appear to have been used for livestock—hence the mission name.

Walking up to these cages or entering the shack usually triggers the first sub-task completion. However, the door you actually need to open is locked behind a digital keypad, necessitating a trip to a nearby residential structure to find the combination.

## Step 2: Finding the Car Office Code

You cannot brute-force the office door; you must find the physical note containing the code. From the wooden shack and cages, you need to head north. Look for a residential house located just outside the immediate industrial circle.

1.  Exit the repair shop area and move North.
2.  Enter the house (it typically has a television in the main room).
3.  Search near the television set for a small note.
4.  Interact with the note to reveal the code.

While the code has been reported as **2896** by some operators, codes in Gray Zone Warfare can occasionally be randomized or changed in seasonal patches. Always interact with the note to ensure the objective updates in your task log.

## Step 3: Entering the Office and Retrieving Intel

With the code in hand, return to the car repair shop area where you first found the cages. The office is located within the same building structure, but the entrance is often tucked away at the back. Look for a door with a digital padlock.

Interact with the padlock and enter the code you discovered in the northern house. Once the door swings open, enter the office and look for a desk or a filing cabinet. The document, labeled as "Car Office Intel," will be sitting there. Pick it up and immediately secure it in your "Safe Container" if you have space. This ensures that even if you are KIA (Killed in Action) during extraction, you will not lose the mission item.

| Location Component | Description | Key Interaction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Northern House** | Residential building with a TV | **Find Note/Code** |
| **Southwest Shack** | Wooden structure near cages | **Initial Objective** |
| **Car Office** | Locked room at the back of the shop | **Enter Code & Loot Intel** |

## Tactical Advice for the Livestock Task

The **grey zone warfare livestock** mission takes place in an area with long sightlines and several elevated positions where AI snipers can spawn. To successfully navigate this zone in 2026, follow these tactical recommendations:

*   **Suppressors are Mandatory:** The car repair shop is a "hot" zone. Using an unsuppressed weapon will draw every AI within 200 meters to your position, making the search for the code significantly more difficult.
*   **Check the Roofs:** Before approaching the northern house, glass the rooftops of the surrounding industrial buildings. AI often sit on the edges with SKS or AK-series rifles.
*   **Clear the Interior:** When entering the house for the code, do not rush. AI can sometimes spawn inside or right behind the house. Use a flashbang if you suspect a "closet spawn."

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing in a squad, have one player stay near the office while the other two retrieve the code. This prevents the AI from respawning in the office area while you are away.

## Completing the Mission

Once you have the intel in your inventory, your final objective is to extract safely and hand the documents over to Banshee. Open your map and locate the nearest Landing Zone (LZ). Be cautious, as LZs near mission hubs are frequent targets for "LZ campers" or wandering AI patrols.

Once back at Base Camp, open the "Vendors" menu, select Banshee, and navigate to the "Tasks" tab. Click on "Livestock" and select "Hand Over" to complete the mission.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the code for the grey zone warfare livestock task the same for everyone?**

A: While many players report the code as 2896, Gray Zone Warfare occasionally randomizes codes per server or per player instance in newer updates. It is always recommended to check the note in the northern house to ensure the code is active for your specific mission state.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the intel?**

A: If the intel is in your standard backpack or rig, you will drop it upon death and must return to your body to retrieve it. However, if you place the intel in your Safe Container (Gamma/Epsilon/etc.), it will remain with you even if you fail to extract.

**Q: Do I need a specific key to enter the house with the code?**

A: No, the house containing the code is typically unlocked. You only need the digital code for the final office door where the intel is located.

**Q: Can I complete this task in a squad?**

A: Yes, but only one person needs to find the code for the whole squad to use it. However, every player must physically pick up the intel document (or wait for it to respawn) to complete their individual task requirements unless the mission allows for shared progression.
