<Section icon={Swords} title="Ultralight Weapon Builds and the Lever Effect">

Weapon weight specifically impacts your arm stamina, which is consumed when aiming down sights (ADS) or holding your breath for a shot. In Gray Zone Warfare, weapon length is just as critical as overall weight due to the "Lever Effect" [707005192]. Long barrels act as a heavy lever, shifting the center of mass away from your body and draining arm stamina rapidly. 

Shortening your barrel brings the center of mass closer to your body, reducing arm strain and weapon sway. Keeping a short-barreled build allows for up to 40 percent longer ADS holds, preventing the "Sore Arms" status effect that makes weapon sway uncontrollable [707005192].

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Manage Inventory and Stash Space Efficiency">

Stash management is a common headache for players, but a few simple organization techniques can free up massive amounts of space. The most effective method is stripping your stored weapons of their pistol grips and magazines [5oLIku9ObWY]. 

A fully assembled rifle like the MK18 takes up a 7x2 grid in your inventory. By removing the magazine and the pistol grip, the weapon becomes non-functional and shrinks to a 7x1 grid [5oLIku9ObWY]. The removed grip takes up 1 slot, and the magazine takes up 2 slots, meaning you save a total of 4 inventory slots per weapon by storing these components separately [5oLIku9ObWY].

| Item / Action | Original Size | Optimized Size | Grid Slots Saved |
| **MK18 (Fully Assembled)** | 7x2 grid | 7x1 grid | **4 slots saved** |
| **EMT Container** | 2x2 grid | 3x3 storage | **5 slots gained** |
| **Bags (No Stacking)** | Individual | Storage containers | **Variable** |
