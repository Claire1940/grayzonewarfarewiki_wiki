The Harvest Moon quest typically originates from one of your primary vendors, such as Handshake or Gunny, depending on your faction's specific progression path. The core objective involves investigating a local farm or storage facility to uncover evidence of illicit activities or to secure vital supplies that have been left behind during the initial evacuation.

Before heading out, ensure your kit is prepared for short-to-mid-range engagements. The starter towns are often crawling with local insurgency AI who, while less geared than the professionals in Tiger Bay, can still end your run with a well-placed shot.

## Step-by-Step Guide to the Harvest Moon Quest

To successfully complete the **gray zone warfare harvest moon quest**, follow these tactical steps. The 0.4 update has introduced new map icons that provide a rough area of operation, making it easier to find your way without constantly tabbing out to third-party maps.

### 1. Accept the Task and Prep Your Map
Check your messages at the Forward Operating Base (FOB). Once the quest is active, open your map. You will notice the new UI improvements from the Spearhead update, which highlight the quest area in a subtle yellow or blue circle. This area is usually located on the outskirts of your faction’s starting town.

### 2. Infiltration
Approach the farmstead from the high ground if possible. Use the new bamboo biomes and dense foliage added in 0.4 to mask your approach. The lighting and ambient sounds have been overhauled, so listen for the sound of idling vehicles or shouting guards to pinpoint enemy positions before they see you.

### 3. Locating the Item
The "Harvest Moon" item—usually a ledger or a specific container—is typically found in the main office or the largest barn on the property. 
*   **Search the desks:** Look for interactable objects highlighted by the UI.
*   **Check the back rooms:** Sometimes the item is tucked away in a locker or on a shelf behind crates.

### 4. Extraction
Once the item is in your inventory, do not rush to the nearest Landing Zone (LZ). With the 0.4 update, player stats and KD are now tracked more accurately, and losing your gear—or the quest item—can be a significant setback. Move cautiously back toward the extraction point, utilizing cover and checking your corners.

| Objective Phase | Key Action | Tactical Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Infiltration** | Stealth approach | Use the new bamboo forests for concealment. |
| **Search** | Identify the Ledger | Clear the building floor-by-floor before looting. |
| **Secure** | Item Retrieval | Place the item in your "Safe" container if it fits. |

## Progression: From Harvest Moon to the Secret Vendor

Completing early tasks like the **gray zone warfare harvest moon quest** is the first step toward the "endgame" of Lamang. As you build reputation with Handshake and Lab Rat, you will eventually unlock the ability to interact with the secret vendor known as **Vulture**.

### Who is Vulture?
Vulture is a scavenger vendor who moves between "Major COPs" (Command Posts) on a weekly rotation. These locations are marked with three black dots on your map, such as Harrison, Titan, or Westmore. 

### Why Level Up for Vulture?
Unlike standard vendors, Vulture sells items scavenged from fallen PMCs, including fully repaired high-tier weapons like the M14 or specialized armor. More importantly, at max level, Vulture sells **Large Safes**, which provide a massive 108-slot container for storing money and high-value loot.

| Vendor Level | Reward / Item | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Scavenged PMC Gear | Access to high-tier guns early. |
| **Level 3** | Small Safe | Secure storage for currency. |
| **Level 4** | **Large Safe** | 108-slot high-value loot storage. |

To level up Vulture, you must sell him specific "Military Equipment" found in high-tier areas like Midnight Sapphire. However, you cannot reach this stage without first proving your worth through early missions like the **gray zone warfare harvest moon quest**.

The combat in 0.4 is significantly more visceral. When you shoot an enemy, they react based on the severity and location of the impact. This means your choice of ammunition and weapon during the **gray zone warfare harvest moon quest** matters more than ever.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the ledger for the Gray Zone Warfare Harvest Moon quest?**

A: The ledger is typically located in the main farm office or a central warehouse in the designated quest area. In version 0.4, look for the yellow quest area on your map for a more precise search radius.

**Q: Did the 0.4 update change the rewards for early quests?**

A: While the base XP and currency rewards are similar, the 0.4 update added "Intel Items" as potential spawns in quest areas. These can unlock unique weapon skins or engravings that were previously unavailable.

**Q: How do I find the secret vendor Vulture after completing my early quests?**

A: Vulture rotates weekly between Major COPs like Harrison and Titan. You must capture the Command Post (the computer must turn yellow) to access his shop. He is essential for purchasing the Large Safe at higher levels.

**Q: Can I complete the Harvest Moon quest in PvE mode?**

A: Yes, all quests, including the **gray zone warfare harvest moon quest**, can be completed in both PvP and PvE modes. The objectives and item locations remain identical across both server types.
