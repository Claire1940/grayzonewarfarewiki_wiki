Navigating the treacherous terrain of Lamang Island requires more than just a steady aim; it demands keen investigative skills and a deep understanding of the local infrastructure. In the **harvest moon gray zone** task, players are commissioned by the vendor Banshee to uncover the illicit operations fueling the local conflict. This mission is a multi-stage investigation that takes you across several high-risk sectors, specifically targeting hidden poppy fields that serve as the backbone of the region's narcotics trade.

To successfully complete the **harvest moon gray zone** objective, you must locate and investigate four distinct poppy fields, each containing critical intelligence documents. These fields are often guarded by hostile AI, making tactical planning and environmental awareness essential for survival. Whether you are operating as a solo mercenary or as part of a squad, this guide provides the exact coordinates, document locations, and tactical advice needed to report your findings to Banshee and secure your rewards in 2026.

## Harvest Moon Task Overview

The Harvest Moon task is primarily an intelligence-gathering mission. Banshee is interested in the logistics and oversight of the poppy production in the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield area and the southern reaches of the map. Unlike simple "kill and clear" missions, your success here depends on your ability to interact with specific items—folders, notes, and ledgers—tucked away in various structures within the fields.

| Task Attribute | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vendor** | Banshee |
| **Location** | F.A.L.N.G. Airfield / YBL-1 / Lima 2 |
| **Objectives** | Investigate 4 Hidden Poppy Fields |
| **Primary Threats** | Armed Insurgents (AI), Enemy PMCs |
| **Required Intel** | Labor Request, Shipment Note, Oversight Agreement, Discipline Notice |

> **Warning:** Several of these poppy fields are located in open areas with minimal cover. Always scout the perimeter with a long-range optic before moving in to interact with the mission items.

## Preparation and Tactical Requirements

Before heading out to the coordinates, ensure your loadout is optimized for both mid-range engagements and close-quarters building clearing. The poppy fields are often surrounded by dense foliage, but the interaction points are usually inside small shacks or houses.

### Recommended Loadout for 2026
*   **Weapon:** An assault rifle with a 1-4x or 1-6x variable optic is ideal.
*   **Ammo:** Carry at least three spare magazines of armor-piercing (AP) rounds, as some AI guards may wear basic ballistic vests.
*   **Medical:** Bring a minimum of two tourniquets and several bandages. The walk between these fields can be long, and you don't want to bleed out far from an LZ.
*   **Tactical Gear:** A recon drone (if available in your current build) can help spot AI clusters in the fields before you commit to an approach.

## Hidden Poppy Field 1: The Airfield Perimeter

The first location is situated to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield, roughly southeast of the Nomad landing zone. This area is relatively flat, making you a target for any snipers perched near the airfield hangers.

**Coordinates: 192, 154**

Follow these steps to secure the intel:
1.  Approach the field from the north to maintain a height advantage.
2.  Locate the small wooden table situated near the center of the harvesting area.
3.  On this table, you will find a document titled **"Labor Allocation Request."**
4.  Interact with the paper (default key 'F') to read it and update your task progress.

## Hidden Poppy Field 2: South of Falcon

The second field is located directly south of the F.A.L.N.G. (Falcon) facility. This area is more cluttered than the first, with several open-air structures that can hide hostile AI.

**Coordinates: 178, 152**

Follow these steps:
1.  Look for an open-sided building or a shed near the edge of the poppy rows.
2.  Inside this structure, search for a **"Weekly Shipment Note."**
3.  Reading this note will reveal the logistical side of the operation and trigger the next stage of the Harvest Moon task.

## Hidden Poppy Field 3: The Ground Zero Approach

The third location is a significant hike for most factions. It is situated at coordinates **172, 132**, which lies south of Ghost 3 and near the Ground Zero sector. This area is notorious for high AI density.

**Coordinates: 172, 132**

Tactical Approach:
*   Expect to engage at least 7-10 AI combatants in this vicinity.
*   Move toward the green folder located on a crate or table in the open-sided structure.
*   The document is the **"Protective Oversight Agreement."** You must "Translate" and "Close" the document interface for the task to register as updated.

> **Tip:** If you are playing as the Mithras faction, your nearest LZ provides a slightly shorter walk, but the path is still heavily patrolled. Move through the treeline rather than the open road.

## Hidden Poppy Field 4: The Southern Outpost

The final field is located at the far southern edge of the operational map, below the Lima 2 extraction point and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. This is often the most dangerous leg of the journey due to the proximity of other high-level tasks.

**Coordinates: 148, 117**

Follow these steps:
1.  Locate the residential house near the poppy field.
2.  Approach the front porch of the house.
3.  On the porch, search for a book or ledger titled **"Harvest Discipline Notice."**
4.  Translate the document to complete the investigation phase of the mission.

| Poppy Field | Coordinates | Document Name | Location Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## Completing the Task

Once all four documents have been read and translated, your objective list will update to "Report your findings to Banshee." You do not need to extract with physical items; the intel is recorded digitally upon interaction.

Return to your base of operations via the nearest Landing Zone (LZ). Open the vendor menu, select Banshee, and hand in the task. Completion usually rewards players with a significant amount of Reputation, Experience, and currency, along with specialized equipment that Banshee offers to his trusted operatives.

## Advanced Tips for Solo Players

Completing the **harvest moon gray zone** task alone is challenging but rewarding. Since you lack squad support, stealth is your best friend.
*   **Time of Day:** If the server allows, attempt this mission during low-light conditions. AI detection ranges are slightly reduced, allowing you to slip into the poppy fields, read the notes, and slip out without engaging the entire garrison.
*   **Suppressors:** A suppressor is mandatory. If you fire an unsuppressed shot at Field 3, you will likely draw AI from the surrounding Ground Zero perimeter.
*   **LZ Management:** Don't always go for the closest LZ. Sometimes landing further away and walking in through the woods prevents "LZ camping" by enemy PMCs who know players are hunting for quest objectives.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to find the poppy fields in a specific order for the Harvest Moon task?**

A: While the task lists them, you can generally visit the fields in any order that suits your tactical route. However, the task UI often updates sequentially, so it is recommended to follow the order from North to South (Airfield to YBL) to ensure the tracking doesn't glitch.

**Q: Are there any locked rooms in the Harvest Moon poppy fields?**

A: Yes, two of the poppy field locations feature small buildings with locked rooms. These rooms often contain high-tier loot, including weapon parts and rare medical supplies. While not required for the **harvest moon gray zone** task itself, bringing a set of local keys can make the trip much more profitable.

**Q: What happens if I die after reading three of the documents?**

A: In Gray Zone Warfare, task progress for "investigate" objectives is usually saved as you go. If you have successfully interacted with and translated three documents, you should only need to return for the fourth one. Always check your task menu after respawning to verify which objectives remain active.

**Q: Why isn't the document appearing at the coordinates?**

A: Double-check your coordinates. Some fields are quite large, and the "investigation point" is a specific small prop (like a folder or book). If it is definitely not there, try switching servers, as occasionally quest items can bug out in high-population instances.
