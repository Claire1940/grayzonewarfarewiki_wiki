## How to Access the Secret Vendor: Vulture

While most vendors are accessible from your Base Camp menu, **Vulture** is a unique "in-person" trader added in the Spearhead update. Even though he appears in your vendor tab, he remains locked until you physically visit the Combat Outpost where he is currently stationed. Vulture is essential for players using **greyzone warfare blueprints** because he is the primary source for purchasing specific weapons like the AK-308 and MP7A2.

### Vulture's Rotation Schedule
Vulture does not stay in one place. He operates on a weekly rotation, moving every **Monday at 8:00 AM EST**. He typically alternates between two specific Combat Outposts.

| Current Cycle | Primary Location | Secondary Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Week A** | Winchester | Titan |
| **Week B** | Lima | Echo |

To unlock him, simply fly to the active LZ, walk into the fortified area of the Combat Outpost, and open your vendor menu. Once "discovered" for the week, you can trade with him just like any other NPC, provided you remain at that outpost.

> **Warning:** Vulture's inventory is level-gated. While you can visit him at Level 1, his best items (like the suppressed AK-308) require higher reputation levels which are earned through his specific questline.

## Tactical Breakdown: High-Risk Blueprints

### The Midnight Sapphire Double-Run
Midnight Sapphire is one of the most dangerous zones in Gray Zone Warfare. To collect the MCX and M700 blueprints, you should approach from the hotel's southern entrance. The MCX is located on a table in the main lobby—expect heavy resistance from elite AI guards here. Once secured, do not extract immediately. Head to the stairwell and reach the roof. The M700 manual is located at the far eastern edge of the roof.

### The Fort Nar Infiltration
Fort Nar houses two of the most sought-after **greyzone warfare blueprints**: the DDM4 and the AK-308. 
1. **DDM4:** Located in the Barracks. You will need to clear approximately 10-15 enemies. The manual is on a table at the end of the top-floor hallway.
2. **AK-308:** Located in the Jail building. This is a central structure within the fort. You must kick down a specific door in the side hallway to find the manual on a shelf.

### The Tiger Bay Mall Slog (SVD)
The SVD blueprint is arguably the hardest to obtain due to the sheer density of AI in Tiger Bay. The "Pum Galaxy" bookstore is located on the ground floor. You must fight your way past the Lantel store and into the back office of the bookstore. Because Tiger Bay is a high-traffic PvP area in 2026, it is highly recommended to attempt this run with a full squad.

## Strategy for Early Game Progression
If you are a new player in 2026, focus on the **AKMn** and **M14 Gordy Special** blueprints first. These are located in relatively low-threat areas compared to Fort Nar or Tiger Bay. The AKMn blueprint at the Occupied Cottage is particularly valuable as it provides a reliable 7.62x39mm platform early on. 

Always check the weekly rotation for Vulture. Even if you don't have his specific blueprints yet, he often sells high-tier ammunition and 20-slot backpacks that are unavailable from Gunny or Artisan at lower levels.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I lose the greyzone warfare blueprints if I die before extracting?**

A: Yes. The blueprint manuals are physical items that stay in your inventory. If you are killed before reaching an LZ and extracting, the item will remain on your corpse and can be looted by other players or lost if you cannot return to your body.

**Q: Can I buy the blueprint weapons from any vendor?**

A: No. Most camo-skinned weapons are tied to Artisan. However, specific high-tier weapons like the AK-308 and MP7A2 can only be purchased from Vulture once you have completed the associated blueprint task.

**Q: Does Vulture's location change for every player?**

A: No, Vulture's location is global for the entire server and faction. If he is at Winchester for one player, he is there for everyone during that weekly rotation.

**Q: Are the blueprints purely cosmetic?**

A: While they provide a unique "camo" skin, many of these blueprints come pre-built with high-tier attachments (like suppressors or specific optics) that might otherwise be locked behind high vendor loyalty levels. This makes them "meta" shortcuts for savvy players.
