## Finding the Secret Vendor: Vulture

One of the most significant additions in the 0.4 cycle is the secret vendor known as Vulture. Unlike standard vendors like Gunny or Lab Rat, Vulture does not stay at your base. He is a scavenger who roams the major Checkpoints (C.O.P.s) around the map. To unlock his services, you must physically locate him at a captured C.O.P.

Vulture operates on a weekly rotation, appearing at two major C.O.P.s at any given time. These locations are marked on your map with three black dots rather than the standard single dot. Once your faction captures the location (the terminal turns yellow), Vulture becomes accessible in your vendor menu.

| Major C.O.P. Location | Region | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| **Harrison** | Central | Frequent rotation spot, high AI density |
| **Titan** | Eastern | Easier to hold for most factions |
| **Stalwart** | Northern | Contains PMC Weapon Crates |
| **Westmore** | Western | Contains PMC Armor Crates |
| **Crusader** | Southern | Near high-tier loot zones |

> **💡 Tip:** To save time, check community hubs or specialized bots to find Vulture's current weekly location before flying out.

## Weapon Customization and Performance Blueprints

Creating effective **grayzone warfare blueprints** for your firearms involves balancing two primary stats: Recoil Control and Ergonomics. While many players instinctively gravitate toward maximum recoil reduction, the 2026 meta favors high Ergonomics (Ergo). High Ergo builds allow for snappier aim-down-sights (ADS) speeds and significantly reduced arm stamina drain, which is vital for holding angles during long engagements.

### Ergonomics vs. Recoil Control
In testing, a build with 60% Ergonomics and only 12% Recoil Control often outperforms a "heavy" build with 50% Recoil Control but negative Ergonomics. The snappier response time allows you to acquire targets faster, which is often the deciding factor in a PMC firefight.

| Build Focus | ADS Speed | Stamina Drain | Control Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **High Ergonomics** | **Instant** | **Very Low** | Snappy/Reactive |
| **High Recoil Control** | Slow | High | Sustained Fire |
| **Balanced Blueprint** | Moderate | Moderate | Versatile |

To build your own **grayzone warfare blueprints** at a vendor, simply click on the base weapon once. The vendor's inventory will highlight all compatible parts in yellow. This visual aid makes it easy to identify which barrels, grips, and sights will fit your specific frame without trial and error.

## Advanced Stimulant Blueprints for 2026

The 0.4 update introduced a powerful "blueprint" for physical performance using Lab Rat’s level 3 stimulants. By combining specific syringes, players can achieve temporary "super-soldier" status, which is particularly effective for crossing large distances or escaping ambush zones.

1.  **The Strict Stimulant:** Provides unlimited sprinting stamina. The downside is that it causes heart damage and intoxication.
2.  **The HPR 3S Syringe:** Automatically repairs organ damage over time.

By popping the HPR 3S *before* the Strict stimulant, the organ repair effect counters the heart damage caused by the unlimited stamina boost. This allows you to sprint across the map without stopping, while keeping your health stabilized.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not stack more than two Strict stimulants in a short window, or your character will likely enter a coma due to extreme intoxication.

## Storage Solutions: The Large Safe

As you progress through the **grayzone warfare blueprints** for your base, storage becomes a major bottleneck. Vulture is the primary source for the game's most efficient storage containers. Upon reaching Level 4 with Vulture, you can purchase the Large Safe.

*   **Large Safe Size:** 108 slots.
*   **Utility:** Can store currency, jewelry, and high-value military items.
*   **Organization:** Using a Large Safe is significantly more efficient than cluttering your stash with dozens of individual wallets.

To level Vulture up quickly, you must sell him specific military equipment found in high-tier POIs like Midnight Sapphire. While he pays less than other vendors for standard gear, the reputation gain is necessary to unlock these endgame containers.

## Ammo Penetration and Combat Effectiveness

Your **grayzone warfare blueprints** are only as good as the ammunition you load into them. In 2026, understanding armor classes and penetration values is non-negotiable for both PvE and PvP.

| Ammo Type | Penetration Power | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **M855 (5.56)** | Level 3+ | Standard AI and basic PMC kits |
| **M855A1 (5.56)** | **Level 3++** | High-tier PvP and armored bosses |
| **FMJ (5.56)** | Level 3 | Budget PvE (Low durability impact) |
| **7.62 PP** | **Level 3++** | AKM builds for heavy penetration |

For consistent success in PvE, aim for the "brim" of the helmet. Due to current scope alignment bugs, aiming directly at the center of the face can sometimes result in the bullet hitting the neck or chest. Aiming slightly higher ensures a clean one-tap through most Level 3 helmets.

## Economy and Looting Tips

To fund your expensive **grayzone warfare blueprints**, you need a reliable "Looting Blueprint." Locations like the Villa Louisa in Midnight Sapphire contain hidden safes behind paintings that many players overlook. 

Additionally, utilize [Gray Zone Warfare's official resources](https://www.grayzonewarfare.com/) or community tactical maps to locate PMC Weapon Boxes. These crates often contain fully modded M700s or high-end suppressors that can be sold or stripped for parts to use in your own builds.

*   **Fast Looting:** Use `Ctrl + Left Click` to instantly move items to your inventory.
*   **Weight Management:** If you are too heavy to jump over a wall, drop your backpack, vault the obstacle, and then reach back to grab it.
*   **Vendor Specialization:** Sell jewelry to Turncoat and electronics to Artisan to maximize your profit margins.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the latest grayzone warfare blueprints for weapon builds?**

A: The best way to find blueprints is by inspecting weapons at Gunny or Banshee. By clicking a weapon, the vendor will highlight all compatible parts. High-Ergo builds are currently the most recommended for 2026.

**Q: How do I unlock the Vulture vendor permanently?**

A: Vulture is never truly "permanent" in your base. You must visit him at a captured C.O.P. each week. Once you have visited him during his current rotation, he will appear in your vendor list as long as your faction maintains control of at least one of his active locations.

**Q: What is the best way to level up vendors quickly?**

A: Complete all available tasks first. If you run out of tasks, sell specialized loot to the corresponding vendor (e.g., military tech to Vulture, jewelry to Turncoat). High-value items from Midnight Sapphire are the most efficient for XP farming.

**Q: Does muzzle velocity affect armor penetration?**

A: Yes. In Grayzone Warfare, certain rounds like M855 require a barrel length of 18 inches or more to achieve the velocity needed to penetrate Level 3+ armor. Using a shorter 10-inch barrel will reduce the effective penetration of the same round.
