Navigating the dense jungles and contested territories of Lamang Island requires more than just a steady aim; it demands a keen eye for intelligence. One of the more complex reconnaissance missions players face is the **harvest moon grey zone** task, assigned by the vendor Banshee. This mission sends operators deep into the outskirts of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and surrounding southern sectors to uncover the truth behind illegal narcotics production. To successfully complete the **harvest moon grey zone** objective, you must locate and investigate four distinct hidden poppy fields, each containing sensitive documentation that proves the scale of the local insurgency's operations.

In this comprehensive guide, we will break down every coordinate, document location, and tactical consideration needed to survive these high-traffic areas. Whether you are flying in from a Mithras, Crimson, or Lamang Recovery Initiative base, these poppy fields are often swarming with well-armed AI, making preparation just as important as knowing where to land.

## Harvest Moon Task Overview

Before heading out into the field, it is essential to understand exactly what Banshee is looking for. This is not a simple "search and destroy" mission. Instead, it is an investigation task that requires you to interact with specific items—usually folders or notes—left behind at makeshift processing stations within the poppy fields.

| Objective | Requirement | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Field 1** | Read Labor Allocation Request | 192 154 (East of Airfield) |
| **Investigate Field 2** | Read Weekly Shipment Note | 178 152 (South of Falcon) |
| **Investigate Field 3** | Read Protective Oversight Agreement | 172 132 (Near Ground Zero) |
| **Investigate Field 4** | Read Harvest Discipline Notice | 149 117 (South of Lima 2) |

> **Warning:** These fields are often guarded by 5-10 AI combatants. Do not approach the center of the fields without clearing the perimeter first, as the lack of hard cover makes you an easy target.

## Mastering the Harvest Moon Grey Zone Task: Location 1

The first stop on your investigation is located to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield, roughly southeast of the Nomad landing zone. This area is characterized by rolling hills and rows of bright flowers that are easily visible from the air if you are paying attention during your insertion.

### Coordinates: 192 154
Upon arriving at these coordinates, look for a small cluster of makeshift structures and tables. In the center of this poppy field, you will find a wooden table with various agricultural tools. Sitting on the table is a piece of paper titled the **Labor Allocation Request**. 

Approach the table and press your interact key (default 'F') to read the document. You do not need to extract the item; simply reading it will trigger the task update in your UI. This field is relatively open, so keep a teammate on overwatch toward the Airfield side, as patrols frequently wander over from the hangars.

## Location 2: South of Falcon (F.A.L.N.G.)

The second hidden poppy field is situated south of the main Falcon (F.A.L.N.G.) area. This location is slightly more built-up than the first, featuring an open-sided building that serves as a temporary storage facility for the harvest.

### Coordinates: 178 152
As you approach the coordinates **178 152**, identify the open-walled structure. Inside, among crates and harvesting equipment, you will find the **Weekly Shipment Note**. This document provides evidence of the logistics chain involved in moving the product. 

Because of the proximity to the Falcon Airfield, the AI here may be better equipped than standard village rebels. Expect them to use basic tactical movement and potentially carry higher-tier 5.45mm or 7.62mm weapons. Ensure you have cleared the building and checked the surrounding tall grass before standing still to read the note.

## Location 3: The Ground Zero Vicinity

The third field is a significant distance from the first two, requiring a trek (or a new flight) toward the center of the map near Ground Zero. For Mithras players, this is a relatively short distance, but for other factions, it represents a "haul" through dangerous territory.

### Coordinates: 172 132
This field is located near the balloon crash site area. The AI presence here is notably higher, with some players reporting up to seven or eight guards patrolling the perimeter. 

Look for a green folder lying on a crate or table in the middle of the field. This document is the **Protective Oversight Agreement**. 
1. Move to the center of the poppy field.
2. Locate the table/crate with the yellow or green folder.
3. Select "Translate" and then "Close" to ensure the task updates.

> **Tip:** If you are playing solo, use a suppressed weapon to pick off the guards from the treeline before entering the field. The bright colors of the poppies make it very difficult to spot prone AI until you are right on top of them.

## Location 4: Southern Highlands (Lima 2)

The final piece of the puzzle for the **harvest moon grey zone** mission is located at the far southern edge of the operational map, south of the Lima 2 landing zone and near the YBL-1 bunker complex.

### Coordinates: 149 117
Unlike the previous locations, which focused on tables and crates in the middle of the fields, this objective is tied to a more permanent structure. Look for a small house overlooking the poppy rows. 

On the front porch of this house, you will find a book or ledger titled the **Harvest Discipline Notice**. Approach the porch, interact with the book, and translate the text. Once this is completed, your task list should show all four investigations as finished.

## Tactical Preparation and Gear Recommendations

Completing the **harvest moon grey zone** mission involves visiting four spread-out locations, which means you will be spending a lot of time in transit. Managing your stamina and hydration is just as important as your ammunition count.

### Recommended Loadout
*   **Weapon:** An assault rifle with a 4x optic (like the Spectre or ACOG) is ideal. The poppy fields offer long sightlines, allowing you to clear AI from a distance.
*   **Armor:** Level III or higher is recommended. While the rebels aren't always crack shots, they often hide in the poppies and can surprise you with a burst of fire.
*   **Supplies:** Carry at least two bottles of water and two MREs. If you plan to hit all four locations in one deployment, you will likely run low on energy.

### Document Checklist

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Weekly Shipment Note** | 178 152 | Note in an open building |
| **Protective Oversight Agreement** | 172 132 | Green folder on a crate |
| **Harvest Discipline Notice** | 149 117 | Book on a house porch |

## Returning to Base

Once you have investigated all four fields, you do not need to find any further items. Your primary goal is survival. Head to the nearest Extraction Point (LZ). Once you are safely back at base, open your menu, navigate to the Vendors tab, select Banshee, and hand in the task.

The rewards for this mission usually include a significant reputation boost with Banshee, which is vital for unlocking higher-tier medical supplies and specialized equipment. You will also receive a payout of currency and potentially some rare ammunition or gear pieces.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order for the harvest moon grey zone task?**

A: No, you can visit the four locations in any order that suits your flight path or current position on the map. However, most players find it efficient to start at the northernmost fields near the Airfield and work their way south.

**Q: What should I do if the document isn't appearing at the coordinates?**

A: Sometimes the "interact" prompt can be finicky. Ensure you are looking directly at the item (the folder, note, or book). If it truly isn't there, try switching servers or "re-logging," as the item might have glitched through the environment.

**Q: Are there any locked rooms in the harvest moon grey zone locations?**

A: While the task objectives are in open areas, some of the buildings near the poppy fields (especially near Falcon and the southern house) have locked rooms that require specific keys. these rooms often contain high-value loot, including rare weapons and attachments, so it is worth checking them if you have found any keys in the area.

**Q: Is it better to do this mission solo or in a squad?**

A: While solo play is possible, a squad is highly recommended for the **harvest moon grey zone** task. Having one player provide long-range cover while another interacts with the documents makes the process much safer, especially at the high-AI density site near Ground Zero.
