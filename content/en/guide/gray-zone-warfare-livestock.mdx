Navigating the dense jungles and war-torn villages of Lamang requires more than just a steady aim; it requires a keen eye for detail and the ability to track down vital intelligence. For players tackling the **gray zone warfare livestock** task, the challenge lies in uncovering a dark human trafficking operation hidden within the Spielberg region. This mission, typically assigned by the vendor Banshee following the Spearhead 0.4 update, takes you deep into the Nam Theun area, where you must investigate an abandoned car repair shop and retrieve incriminating documents.

Completing the **gray zone warfare livestock** mission is essential for increasing your reputation with Banshee and unlocking higher-tier equipment. The task is multi-staged, requiring you to find physical evidence of trafficking, locate a randomized access code in a nearby residential area, and finally breach a locked office to secure the evidence. This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough of the entire operation, including tactical advice for surviving the high-threat environment of Spielberg in 2026.

## Livestock Task Overview

Before heading out to the field, it is important to understand the objectives. The Livestock task is not a simple "search and destroy" mission. It involves reconnaissance and puzzle-solving elements that can be deadly if you are unprepared for the local militia presence.

| Objective | Location | Key Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Car Repair Shop** | Spielberg (Nam Theun) | Reach the cages at the back |
| **Locate Office Code** | Residential House (North) | Find the note near the TV |
| **Retrieve Documents** | Car Repair Shop Office | Enter the 4-digit keypad code |
| **Hand over Evidence** | Base Camp | Return to Banshee |

> **Warning:** The Spielberg area is known for high AI density. Expect multiple hostiles patrolling the car repair shop and the surrounding residential streets. Bring at least three spare magazines and adequate medical supplies.

## Phase 1: Locating the Car Repair Shop

Your first destination is the Spielberg area, specifically the abandoned car repair shop located near the Nam Theun region. The most efficient way to reach this location is by flying into **LZ November 1** or **LZ November 2**. From these landing zones, you will need to head toward the southern outskirts of the town.

The car repair shop is a distinct industrial building. To complete the first part of the **gray zone warfare livestock** task, you don't just need to find the building; you must find the evidence of human trafficking.

1.  Approach the car repair shop from the south or west to avoid being funneled into the main road.
2.  Look for a small wooden shack or an outdoor area containing iron cages.
3.  You must physically walk up to and touch these cages to trigger the "Investigate" objective completion.
4.  Once the sub-task updates, your next goal is to find the access code for the locked office inside the main building.

## Phase 2: Finding the Car Office Code

The office at the car repair shop is protected by a digital padlock. Unlike some static keys in the game, the code for the **gray zone warfare livestock** task is often found on a physical note in a separate location. You will need to travel slightly north of the repair shop into the residential sector of Spielberg.

The specific house you are looking for is located near the coordinates **182 127**. This house is recognizable by its proximity to a television set inside the main living area.

*   **Step 1:** Head north from the car repair shop along the main road.
*   **Step 2:** Locate the house at 182 127.
*   **Step 3:** Enter the building and check the table or stand directly in front of the TV.
*   **Step 4:** Interact with the note to read the code. 

> **Note:** The code is randomized for different players or sessions. Common codes reported include **2381** or **2896**, but you must verify the note in your specific instance to ensure the keypad accepts the input.

## Phase 3: Retrieving the Documents

With the code in hand, return to the car repair shop. The office is located toward the back of the main structure. You will see a door equipped with a electronic keypad.

| Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Approach Door** | Located at the rear/side of the car repair shop building. |
| **Input Code** | Use the 4-digit code found in the residential house. |
| **Locate Item** | The documents are usually sitting on a desk or shelf inside. |
| **Secure Item** | Place the documents in your **Safe Container** immediately. |

Once you have the documents, the **gray zone warfare livestock** task requires you to extract safely. Since Spielberg is a contested zone, the trek back to the LZ can be the most dangerous part of the mission. If you die before reaching the LZ, the documents will remain in your safe container, but you will still need to successfully exfiltrate to hand them over to Banshee.

## Tactical Recommendations for Spielberg

The AI in the Nam Theun/Spielberg area is significantly more aggressive than in the starting towns. They often wear soft armor and carry AK-series rifles, making them a threat even to mid-game players.

1.  **LZ Selection:** LZ November 2 offers a slightly more concealed approach through the treeline, whereas November 1 puts you closer to the main road where you are easily spotted.
2.  **Suppressors are Mandatory:** If you are playing solo, a suppressor is your best friend. Firing unsuppressed shots will draw AI from the entire block to your position.
3.  **Check the Rooftops:** Some buildings in Spielberg have flat roofs or balconies where AI snipers may spawn. Always scan vertically before crossing open streets.
4.  **Team Synergy:** If playing in a squad, have one player pull security at the 182 127 house while another grabs the code. The residential areas have many "L" corners where AI can hide.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Many players encounter difficulties during the **gray zone warfare livestock** quest due to the nature of the "investigate" trigger. If the objective does not tick over, try walking around the perimeter of the cages or standing inside them if the doors are open.

Another common issue is the code note not appearing. If the note is missing from the house at 182 127, ensure you have actually accepted the task from Banshee. In some cases, a server restart or switching to a different instance may be required if the world items fail to spawn correctly.

| Component | Potential Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cages** | Objective won't trigger | Get closer/prone near the cage floor. |
| **Code Note** | Note is not on the table | Check the floor or restart the session. |
| **Keypad** | Code "Invalid" | Re-read the note; codes are **unique** per instance. |
| **Extraction** | LZ is camped | Move to a secondary LZ further away. |

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the car repair shop for the gray zone warfare livestock task?**

A: The repair shop is located in the town of Spielberg, which is part of the Nam Theun region. It is a large industrial building on the southern side of the town, identifiable by the iron cages located behind or beside it.

**Q: Is the office code the same for everyone?**

A: No, the code for the car office is randomized. While some players have reported codes like 2381 or 2896, you must find the note in the house at coordinates 182 127 to get the correct code for your current mission instance.

**Q: What happens if I lose the livestock documents after picking them up?**

A: If you die and the documents were not in your safe container, you will need to return to the car repair shop office to retrieve them again. If you have already used the code, the door may remain unlocked, or you may need to re-enter the code.

**Q: Which vendor gives the Livestock task?**

A: The Livestock task is provided by Banshee. It is part of the 0.4 Spearhead update questline and focuses on uncovering the illegal activities of local criminal elements in Lamang.
