## The Tactical Blueprint for Precision: Overcoming Ballistic Issues

One of the most significant hurdles in the 0.4 update is the inconsistency of headshots against AI combatants. Many players assume "height over bore" is the culprit—where the bullet exits the barrel lower than the optic's line of sight—but 2026 testing reveals deeper issues with zeroing and hitboxes.

Currently, several scopes, including the popular Elcan, suffer from a bug where shots land lower than intended even when properly zeroed. Furthermore, the neck area is often tied to the thorax hitbox rather than the head, meaning a slightly low shot results in non-fatal damage because the spine is not currently modeled as a vital organ.

| Issue | Cause | Tactical Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| **Low Point of Impact** | Zeroing bugs in 0.4 | **Aim for the top of the helmet** or slightly above the head. |
| **Non-Fatal Neck Shots** | Thorax hitbox overlap | Prioritize high-velocity rounds and longer barrels for flatter trajectories. |
| **Zeroing Reset** | ADS/Un-ADS bug | Re-verify your distance settings frequently during long-range engagements. |

> **💡 Tip:** Using a longer barrel increases bullet velocity, which flattens the arc of your round and minimizes the impact of the current zeroing inconsistencies.

Vulture operates on a weekly rotation, appearing at only two of the seven major COPs at any given time. He is a "scavenger" trader, meaning he sells gear left behind by fallen PMCs on your server. This makes him an excellent source for high-tier weapons like the M14 or specialized armor if you haven't leveled up traditional traders like Banshee or Gunny.

## Essential Gear Blueprints: Binoculars and Scoping

Binoculars are a game-changer in the 2026 Spearhead update. Assigned to the "9" key, they allow for reconnaissance without consuming arm stamina. This is vital for scouting AI patrols and avoiding ambushes.

Banshee is the primary source for these optics. As you progress through his main story quests in the starter town, he will unlock higher tiers of binoculars.

1.  **Tier 1 Binoculars:** Standard zoom, wide field of view. Best for general scouting.
2.  **Tier 2 Binoculars:** High zoom. Can be difficult to use in dense jungle due to narrow FOV.
3.  **Tier 4 Rangefinder Binoculars:** Includes a built-in rangefinder, essential for calibrating long-range sniper shots over 400 meters.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check your surroundings before pulling out binoculars. While they don't drain stamina, they leave you vulnerable to flankers as you cannot quickly return fire while glassing.

### Helicopter Management
The helicopter system has been revamped for speed. You no longer need to "pre-book" flights as aggressively.
*   **Call Distance:** Call your heli when you are within 100 meters of the LZ to minimize wait time.
*   **The "L" Key:** Once seated, hold **L** to expedite takeoff. This prevents you from sitting idle while under fire.
*   **Invulnerability:** You are invulnerable while the helicopter is in flight and for 5 seconds after landing to prevent LZ camping.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the gray zone warfare blueprints for the secret vendor?**

A: The "blueprint" for finding Vulture involves checking Major COPs (triple-star icons) on a weekly rotation. He appears at two locations per week, such as Harrison or Titan, and requires you to capture the COP to access his shop.

**Q: How do I fix the headshot inconsistency in Update 0.4?**

A: Due to zeroing bugs, you should aim for the top of an enemy's helmet. Using longer barrels to increase bullet velocity also helps create a flatter trajectory, making your shots more predictable at medium ranges.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get reputation with Vulture?**

A: Sell high-value military equipment and loot. Every $10,000 worth of items sold grants 100 Rep. Farming $60,000 boss items from Midnight Sapphire is the most efficient method to reach Level 4 and unlock the Large Safe.

**Q: Can I rebind the "Quick Drop" hotkey?**

A: As of the 2026 Spearhead update, the drop action is hard-coded to the Delete key. You cannot currently rebind it in the settings, though you can use third-party mouse software to map a button to the Delete key.
