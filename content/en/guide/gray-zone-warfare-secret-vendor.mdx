## Who is Vulture? The Secret Vendor Explained

Vulture is not your typical military contractor. In the lore of the 2026 Lamang conflict, Vulture is a scavenger who roams the battlefield, collecting gear from fallen PMCs that other players have left behind. This makes his inventory highly unpredictable but incredibly valuable, especially on active servers where high-level players are frequently engaging in combat.

Because he scavenges from the dead, Vulture often sells fully repaired weapons and armor that you might not have unlocked through traditional vendor progression. For example, a Level 1 player could potentially purchase a fully modded M14 or high-tier ceramic plates if Vulture managed to recover them from a recent skirmish.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Vulture is locked by default in your vendor menu. You cannot interact with him or view his inventory until you physically locate him in the world and capture the Command Outpost (COP) where he is currently stationed.

## Finding the Gray Zone Warfare Secret Vendor

Vulture does not stay in one place for long. He follows a weekly rotation, moving between various Major COPs across the map. To find the **gray zone warfare secret vendor**, you must look for specific icons on your tactical map.

### The Weekly Rotation
Vulture is typically active at two Major COPs simultaneously each week. The rotation is global, meaning he will be in the same two spots for every player on every server (PvP or PvE) for that duration. If you are unsure where he is, the community often tracks his movement, or you can manually scout the Major COPs listed above.

## How to Unlock and Access Vulture
Simply arriving at a Major COP is not enough to trade with the **gray zone warfare secret vendor**. You must actively secure the area to "activate" him for your session.

1.  **Infiltrate the COP:** Approach the location with caution. Major COPs are heavily guarded by AI and often camped by enemy PMCs.
2.  **Clear the Area:** You must eliminate the guards within the immediate vicinity of the main terminal.
3.  **Check the Terminal:** Locate the main computer inside the outpost. If the screen is **Gray**, there are still enemies nearby. If the screen is **Yellow**, it is ready to be captured.
4.  **Capture the Point:** Interact with the terminal. Once the capture is complete, Vulture will appear in your vendor list, and you can begin trading.

> **💡 Tip:** Vulture's scavenged weapons are usually repaired to 95%–100% durability. This makes them much more reliable than the "battle-scarred" gear you might find on standard AI enemies.

## How to Level Up Vulture Fast
Unlike other vendors who accept almost any gear related to their profession, Vulture is incredibly picky. You cannot sell him standard rifles, basic armor, or medical supplies to increase your reputation. To level up the **gray zone warfare secret vendor**, you must provide him with specific high-value items.

### Military Equipment Farming
Vulture primarily accepts "Military Equipment" items. These are non-gear items found in high-security zones. 
*   **Farming Locations:** Areas like **Midnight Sapphire** and the **Airfield** are prime spots for these items.
*   **Guard Loot:** High-level guards often carry military-grade electronics or specialized tools that Vulture prizes.
*   **Boss Items:** The boss at Midnight Sapphire occasionally drops a rare military item worth approximately **60,000 credits**. Selling this to Vulture can grant nearly **600-700 reputation points** in a single transaction.

### Reputation Conversion Rate
In the current 2026 build, the reputation gain is roughly calculated at **1 Rep per 100 Credits** of value sold. Because Vulture pays roughly half of what other vendors (like Turncoat) might pay for these items, leveling him is a significant financial investment. However, the utility of the Large Safe far outweighs the lost credits for most late-game players.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Vulture’s location change every day?**

A: No, the **gray zone warfare secret vendor** moves on a weekly rotation. Once he is found at a specific Major COP, he will remain there for the rest of the week for all players on that server.

**Q: Can I sell my old guns to Vulture to level him up?**

A: No. Vulture generally does not buy standard weapons or armor from players. He only accepts specific high-value loot and "Military Equipment" items found in high-tier loot zones like Midnight Sapphire.

**Q: What happens if another faction captures the COP while I'm trading?**

A: If a rival faction takes control of the Major COP, you will lose access to Vulture's shop menu until your faction (or you) recaptures the terminal.

**Q: Is the Large Safe permanent?**

A: Yes, once purchased, the Large Safe remains in your stash. It provides 108 slots of storage and is currently the most efficient way to store currency and high-value items in the game.
