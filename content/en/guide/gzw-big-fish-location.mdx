When you arrive at the Cooler Shop, do not rush through the front entrance. The area is often patrolled by AI that can easily pin you down inside the narrow aisles. Instead, approach from the flank or the rear.

## The Journey to the Wooden Shack

Once the package is in your possession, the second half of the **gzw big fish location** challenge begins. You must transport the item to a wooden shack located on the outskirts of the immediate town area. This trek is the most dangerous part of the mission because carrying the quest item often limits your mobility or stamina regeneration.

The wooden shack is a small, unassuming structure. It is easy to miss if you are sprinting, so keep your eyes peeled for a weathered building that looks significantly more dilapidated than the surrounding brick structures.

### Drop-off Instructions
*   **Locate the Shack:** Follow the dirt path leading away from the industrial sector toward the coastal or rural zone.
*   **Enter the Structure:** The shack usually has a single door or a broken wall section you can enter through.
*   **Find the Placement Prompt:** Look for a ghosted silhouette of the box on a table or in a corner.
*   **Place the Item:** Once the "Place" prompt appears, hold the interact key until the progress bar completes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The wooden shack offers almost no ballistic protection. Do not linger inside once the item is placed. Exit immediately and find hard cover (stone or concrete) to check your map for the nearest Extraction Point (LZ).

## Tactical Considerations and AI Behavior

In the 2026 version of Gray Zone Warfare, AI behavior has been significantly tuned. Enemies near the **gzw big fish location** will now use grenades more effectively and attempt to flank players who stay in the Cooler Shop for too long.

After placing the item at the wooden shack, do not head directly to the closest LZ if it has been recently called in by another team. In 2026, "LZ camping" remains a threat. Instead:
1.  Move 200 meters away from the objective.
2.  Wait in concealment for 60 seconds to ensure you aren't being followed.
3.  Call in your transport to an alternative LZ if the primary one is hot.

## Troubleshooting the "Big Fish" Task

Sometimes, players arrive at the **gzw big fish location** only to find the item missing. This can happen for a few reasons in the current build:
*   **Instance Desync:** If you don't see the box, try restarting your game or switching servers.
*   **Prior Collection:** If a teammate picked it up, they must be the one to place it, or they must drop it for you.
*   **Quest Not Active:** Ensure you have actually "Accepted" the task from the vendor menu before deploying.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I complete the GZW Big Fish location task in a single deployment?**

A: Yes, it is highly recommended to do so. If you extract after picking up the box but before placing it, the box will remain in your inventory, but you risk losing it if you die in a subsequent raid.

**Q: What happens if I die while carrying the Big Fish box?**

A: If you die, the box will stay on your corpse. You must return to your body and loot it to continue the quest. If another player loots your body and takes the box, you may need to wait for the item to respawn in the Cooler Shop in a new raid instance.

**Q: Is the wooden shack guarded by elite AI?**

A: Usually, the shack itself is not heavily guarded, but the paths leading to it are often patrolled by 2-3 AI combatants. Always scout the **gzw big fish location** with binoculars before approaching the final drop-off point.

**Q: Does faction choice affect the location of the shop?**

A: The physical location of the Cooler Shop and the wooden shack remains the same for all factions, but your starting point and nearest LZ will differ based on whether you are Mithras, Crimson Shield, or Lamang Recovery Initiative.
