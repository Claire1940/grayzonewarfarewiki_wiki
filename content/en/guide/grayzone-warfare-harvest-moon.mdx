## Grayzone Warfare Harvest Moon: Mission Overview

The Harvest Moon task is primarily an investigation quest. Unlike "seek and destroy" missions, your goal here is to interact with specific intelligence items—usually folders, notes, or ledgers—located within makeshift structures near poppy plantations. Banshee is interested in the logistics of these operations, specifically the labor and oversight agreements that keep the fields running.

Before heading out, ensure you are equipped for mid-to-long-range engagements. The poppy fields are often situated in depressions or open clearings surrounded by dense foliage, making you vulnerable to AI snipers and patrolling squads.

### Task Objectives
*   Locate the first hidden poppy field and find the labor documents.
*   Investigate the second poppy field near the airfield perimeter.
*   Find the oversight agreement at the third hidden field.
*   Secure the final harvest notice at the southern field near Lima 2.
*   Report all findings to Banshee at base.

## Detailed Walkthrough of the Grayzone Warfare Harvest Moon Task

To efficiently complete the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** task, it is recommended to tackle these in a logical geographical order based on your nearest Landing Zone (LZ). Most players find it easiest to start near the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and work their way south toward the YBL-1 bunker area.

### Phase 1: The Airfield Perimeters (Fields 1 & 2)

The first two locations are situated in the northeastern quadrant of the playable area, relatively close to the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield. This area is high-traffic, both for AI and other players completing airfield-related tasks.

**Field #1 (192 154):**
Located southeast of the Nomad area, this field is tucked away in the hills. Look for a small cluster of wooden shacks. Inside the primary structure, you will find the **Labor Allocation Request** sitting on a table. Reading this paper will trigger the first task update.

**Field #2 (178 152):**
Moving west/southwest from the first field, you will encounter the second plantation. This area features an open-sided building. Approach the desk inside to find the **Weekly Shipment Note**. Be cautious here, as the open structure provides very little cover if AI patrols spot you from the surrounding treeline.

> **Warning:** The areas around the airfield are notorious for "bush-wookies" (players sniping from foliage). Always scout the perimeter of the poppy fields with a high-magnification optic before approaching the central buildings.

### Phase 3: The Ground Zero Approach (Field 3)

The third field is a significant trek to the southwest. Located at **172 132**, this site is south of the Ghost 3 LZ and sits on the periphery of the Ground Zero zone. Because of its proximity to high-tier loot areas, the AI presence here is significantly tougher.

*   **The Intel:** Look for a green or yellow folder titled **Protective Oversight Agreement**.
*   **The Location:** It is usually placed on a crate or a makeshift desk within the field's central hub.
*   **Tactical Tip:** If you are playing as the Mithras faction, you will have a slightly shorter overland route to this location, but the density of AI means you should still move slowly and clear the area house-by-house.

### Phase 4: The Southern Sector (Field 4)

The final piece of the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** puzzle lies in the far south, below the Lima 2 LZ and near the YBL-1 facility. The coordinates are approximately **148 117**.

This location is unique because the intel isn't hidden deep inside a warehouse but is often found on the exterior of a residential-style house. Look for a book or ledger on the front porch titled the **Harvest Discipline Notice**. Once you "Translate" or "Read" this item (by pressing 'F' by default), your task list should fully update, indicating you are ready to head home.

## Loot Opportunities and Locked Rooms

While the primary goal of the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** mission is intelligence gathering, these fields are also lucrative for gear. Several of the locations feature locked rooms that require specific keys.

| Location | Key Required (Potential) | Loot Type |

| **Southern Field** | Farmhouse Key | Weapon parts, ammunition, provisions |
| **Ground Zero Field** | Oversight Office Key | Rare folders, cash, electronics |

If you happen to find keys labeled for these sectors while looting AI, keep them. The locked rooms often contain medical supplies that are essential for long-term survival in the jungle.

## Survival Strategies for Lamang Island

Completing the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** task requires traveling across several kilometers of varied terrain. To ensure you don't lose your progress to a random patrol, follow these expert tips:

1.  **Stamina Management:** Do not sprint across the open poppy fields. If you are caught with zero stamina when an AI starts firing, you won't be able to reach cover quickly.
2.  **Suppressors are Mandatory:** Shooting unsuppressed in the jungle is a dinner bell for every AI within 300 meters. To finish the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** objectives quietly, use a suppressor on your primary rifle.
3.  **Check the Porches:** As seen in the southern field, intel isn't always on a desk. Check crates, porches, and even the beds of trucks parked near the fields.
4.  **Faction Coordination:** If possible, run this task with a squad. Having one player pull security on the perimeter while another investigates the documents makes the process significantly safer.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order for the Grayzone Warfare Harvest Moon task?**

A: No, you can investigate the fields in any order that suits your current position on the map. However, the task list in your UI will update sequentially as you find the documents. Most players find a North-to-South route most efficient.

**Q: What happens if I die after finding two of the documents?**

A: Progress in the **grayzone warfare harvest moon** task is persistent. If you have successfully interacted with a document and received the "Task Updated" notification, that objective remains completed even if you die before reaching the next field. You only need to visit the remaining locations.

**Q: Are the documents in the same place for every faction?**

A: Yes, the physical locations of the poppy fields and the specific documents (like the Labor Allocation Request) are identical regardless of whether you are Mithras, Crimson, or LRI. Only your starting LZ and potential encounter points with other players will differ.

**Q: Can I find the Harvest Moon documents without the quest being active?**

A: While the documents may physically appear in the world, you generally cannot interact with them or "read" them to progress the mission unless Banshee has officially assigned the task to you in your command menu.
