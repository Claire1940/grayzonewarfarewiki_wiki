## How to Unlock the MCX Nomad Blueprint

The MCX Nomad is one of the most sought-after builds in the 2026 meta. It is an LVAW (Low Visibility Assault Weapon) build, featuring an integrally suppressed short barrel chambered in .300 Blackout. While the subsonic ammunition used by this weapon isn't the fastest, it is incredibly quiet, making it the perfect tool for night raids or stealthy infiltration.

To unlock this blueprint for purchase from Banshee, you must locate a specific "Red Guns and Ammo" magazine hidden within the dangerous Midnight Sapphire resort.

### Location: Midnight Sapphire Hotel
Midnight Sapphire is a high-tier loot zone teeming with well-equipped AI. You should expect resistance from enemies wearing Level 3+ armor and carrying modern rifles like the AK-12.

1.  **Infiltration:** Approach from the **Argos** landing zone and move through the golf fields. 
2.  **The Golf Club:** Clear the outskirts of the golf club and move toward the main hotel entrance.
3.  **The Hotel Lobby:** Enter the main lobby. You will see a central staircase.
4.  **Finding the Magazine:** Head to the second or third floor (depending on your entry point). Look for the bar or lounge areas with circular coffee tables.
5.  **Extraction:** The item is a physical red magazine. Once you pick it up, you must extract alive.

| Component | Specification |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The AI at Midnight Sapphire are no joke. They frequently use grenades and have high accuracy. Do not attempt this run without at least Level 3A head protection and a high-capacity rig.

## The Secret Vendor: Vulture

In addition to world-drop **gzw blueprints**, the 0.4 update introduced a "Secret Vendor" named Vulture. Unlike standard traders who stay at your base, Vulture is a scavenger who moves between different Major COPs (Command Outposts) on a weekly rotation.

Vulture is essential because he sells high-value items that other vendors don't carry, including gear scavenged from dead PMCs and, most importantly, high-capacity storage safes.

### Finding Vulture's Location
Vulture can be found at any Major COP marked with three black dots on your map. He rotates every week, but he will be in the same two locations for every player on the server, regardless of whether you are playing PvP or PvE.

**Common Vulture Spawn Points:**
*   Harrison
*   Titan
*   Westmore
*   Stalwart
*   Crusader
*   Bronco
*   Spielberg
*   Winchester
*   Nomad

To see if Vulture is at a COP, you must first "capture" the area. Locate the main computer in the COP; if the screen is **Yellow**, the area is free to capture. If it is **Gray**, there are still enemies (AI or players) nearby that must be cleared.

## Vulture Loyalty Rewards and Safes

While Vulture's initial inventory consists of repaired PMC gear and large backpacks, his true value lies in his Loyalty Level rewards. These are essentially the "blueprints" for your stash management in 2026. 

| Loyalty Level | Unlockable Item | Storage Capacity | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1** | Scavenged PMC Gear | Variable | Cheap high-tier gear |
| **Level 2** | Large Backpacks | 30+ Slots | Early game looting |
| **Level 3** | **Small Safe** | 54 Slots | Money & High-value loot |
| **Level 4** | **Large Safe** | 108 Slots | Endgame currency storage |

> **💡 Tip:** You cannot sell weapons or armor to Vulture to increase your reputation. He only accepts specific high-value military equipment and "trash" loot like jewelry or electronics.

## Strategies for Leveling Vulture
Because Vulture doesn't offer traditional contracts, leveling him up requires a dedicated farming strategy. You need to sell him items to gain XP/Reputation.

1.  **Midnight Sapphire Boss Runs:** The boss at Midnight Sapphire often drops a military item worth approximately 60k. Selling this to Vulture (even at his reduced buy-back rates) provides a massive boost to your reputation.
2.  **Jewelry and Purses:** While raiding the hotel for **gzw blueprints**, keep an eye out for purses and jewelry. These small, high-value items are exactly what Vulture wants.
3.  **Military Equipment:** Items labeled as "Military Equipment" found on guards in high-tier zones are the most consistent way to grind Vulture XP.

## Advanced Blueprint Customization

Once you have unlocked the **gzw blueprints** for weapons like the MCX Nomad, you aren't stuck with the base configuration. The 0.4 update added over 500 new attachments, allowing for deep customization.

*   **Optics:** The Nomad comes with an Acro mount, but many players prefer a 1-6x LPVO for the varied engagement distances in the resort.
*   **Ammunition:** While the blueprint comes with AP rounds, you should stock up on .300 Blackout Supersonic if you plan on taking long-range fights, as the subsonic rounds have significant bullet drop.
*   **Grips:** Adding an "Unlock Rail" to the bottom of the MCX allows for the attachment of foregrips, significantly reducing the horizontal recoil of the quad-stack 60-round magazine.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the MCX magazine in the Midnight Sapphire hotel?**

A: It is usually located on the second floor in one of the bar or lobby lounge areas. Look specifically for a red magazine sitting on a circular coffee table. Note that AI frequently respawns in this hallway, so clear the area thoroughly before looting.

**Q: Does Vulture's location change every day?**

A: No, Vulture operates on a weekly rotation. Once you or the community finds him at a specific COP (like Harrison or Titan), he will remain there for the duration of that week for everyone on the server.

**Q: Can I get gzw blueprints in the PvE mode?**

A: Yes, all blueprints, including the MCX Nomad and Vulture's inventory, are available in both PvP and PvE modes. The locations and requirements remain identical across both game types.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the blueprint magazine?**

A: If you die before extracting, you will lose the magazine. You must successfully extract and turn the item in to Banshee to permanently unlock the blueprint for purchase. It is highly recommended to bring a teammate to cover your extraction.
