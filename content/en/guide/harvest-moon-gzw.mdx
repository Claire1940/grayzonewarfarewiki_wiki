The **harvest moon gzw** task is one of the more extensive reconnaissance missions assigned by the vendor Banshee. Unlike simple "kill and clear" objectives, this task requires players to navigate deep into the dangerous territories surrounding the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and the southern regions near YBL-1. Your primary objective is to locate and investigate four hidden poppy fields that are central to the local narcotics trade, reporting your findings back to Banshee to disrupt the enemy's supply chain.

Completing the **harvest moon gzw** mission is essential for players looking to increase their reputation with Banshee, which unlocks higher-tier equipment and specialized ammunition. Because these fields are scattered across a wide area, preparation is key. You will need to manage your weight, bring plenty of hydration, and be ready for mid-to-long-range engagements with AI patrols that guard these lucrative agricultural sites. In this guide, we provide the exact coordinates and interaction points for all four fields to ensure a smooth extraction.

## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out into the Lamang jungle, it is important to understand what Banshee expects. The task is divided into four distinct phases, each requiring you to find a specific piece of intel—usually a document or a folder—hidden within a poppy field. 

| Objective | Location Area | Key Item to Find |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Field 1** | East of F.A.L.N.G. Airfield | Labor Allocation Request |
| **Investigate Field 2** | South of F.A.L.N.G. Airfield | Weekly Shipment Note |
| **Investigate Field 3** | South of Ghost 3 / Ground Zero | Protective Oversight Agreement |
| **Investigate Field 4** | South of Lima 2 (YBL-1 Area) | Harvest Discipline Notice |

To successfully complete the **harvest moon gzw** task, you do not necessarily need to visit them in a specific order, but following a north-to-south route is generally the most fuel-efficient way to use the helicopter transport system.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check your surrounding treeline before approaching the center of a poppy field. The bright flowers offer very little concealment, making you an easy target for AI snipers.

## Detailed Location Walkthrough

Each location in the **harvest moon gzw** questline features a specific document that must be "read" or "translated" using the interact key (default 'F'). Simply standing in the field is not enough to update the task.

### 1. Hidden Poppy Field #1 (Coordinates: 192, 154)
The first field is located to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield. If you are inserting via the Nomad LZ, you will head southeast. This field is relatively open, but there are small structures nearby where the workers manage the harvest.
*   **Target Item:** Labor Allocation Request.
*   **Exact Spot:** Look for a wooden table or crate near the center of the farming area. The paper is sitting out in the open.

### 2. Hidden Poppy Field #2 (Coordinates: 178, 152)
Move west-southwest from the first field toward the area directly south of the main Airfield. This location is often more heavily guarded due to its proximity to the military installations.
*   **Target Item:** Weekly Shipment Note.
*   **Exact Spot:** Locate the open-sided building (a shed or basic pavilion). The note is placed on a surface inside this structure.

### 3. Hidden Poppy Field #3 (Coordinates: 172, 132)
This field is a significant trek to the south, positioned near the Ground Zero area and south of the Ghost 3 landmark. For Mithras players, this is a somewhat shorter walk, but for others, it represents a deep excursion into the jungle.
*   **Target Item:** Protective Oversight Agreement.
*   **Exact Spot:** Look for a green folder. It is typically found on a crate or a makeshift desk within the field's perimeter. You must select "Translate" and then "Close" for the task to register.

### 4. Hidden Poppy Field #4 (Coordinates: 148, 117)
The final location for the **harvest moon gzw** mission is far to the south, below the Lima 2 Landing Zone and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. This area is notorious for high AI density and potential PvP encounters.
*   **Target Item:** Harvest Discipline Notice.
*   **Exact Spot:** Approach the small house/shack at the edge of the field. The document is a book or ledger located on the front porch.

## Combat Strategy and Preparation

Navigating between these four points in [Gray Zone Warfare](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2477940/Gray_Zone_Warfare/) is a test of endurance. Because the fields are spread across the map, you should plan your extraction points (LZs) carefully.

| Field | Recommended LZ | Enemy Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field 1 & 2** | Nomad 1 or Hotel 1 | **Medium** (Standard Insurgents) |
| **Field 3** | Ghost 3 or Kilo 1 | **High** (Well-armed patrols) |
| **Field 4** | Lima 2 | **Very High** (Military-grade gear) |

When approaching the southern fields (3 and 4), expect AI to be wearing body armor and using optics. A suppressed weapon is highly recommended to avoid drawing the entire zone's attention while you are searching for the documents.

> **Warning:** Some of the buildings near these poppy fields contain locked rooms. While not required for the **harvest moon gzw** task, these rooms often contain high-value loot. If you find a key for the Airfield or YBL areas, keep it in your secure container.

## Navigating the Terrain

The journey for the **harvest moon gzw** task covers diverse biomes. The northern fields are flatter with more industrial debris, while the southern fields are tucked into dense foliage and hilly terrain. 

1.  **Stamina Management:** Do not sprint across the open fields. Use the "walk" pace to keep your stamina high in case you need to dive for cover.
2.  **Visual Landmarks:** Use the "Balloon Crash Site" as a waypoint when moving between the northern fields. It serves as a great north-star for orientation.
3.  **Team Play:** If playing in a squad, have one person pull security on the perimeter while the other interacts with the documents. The animation for reading the files leaves you vulnerable for several seconds.

## Rewards and Completion

Once you have interacted with all four documents, your task list will update to "Report findings to Banshee." You do not need to carry physical items back; the intel is recorded digitally upon interaction. Return to your base camp and speak with Banshee to receive your rewards.

Typical rewards for this tier of mission include:
*   Increased Reputation with Banshee.
*   Significant Experience Points (XP).
*   Currency (USD) to fund future deployments.
*   Access to new tactical gear or weapon parts in the shop.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to visit all four poppy fields in a single raid to complete harvest moon gzw?**

A: No, you can progress the task over multiple raids. The game tracks which documents you have already interacted with. However, completing them in one go is more efficient if you have the supplies.

**Q: I found the location but the document isn't appearing. What should I do?**

A: Ensure you are at the correct coordinates. Some fields have multiple small shacks; the document is always in a specific one. If it's definitely missing, try restarting your session or checking if a teammate has already interacted with it, which sometimes triggers the update for the whole squad.

**Q: Is there a specific faction requirement for the harvest moon gzw task?**

A: This task is available to all factions (Mithras, Crimson Shield, and Lamang Recovery Initiative) as it is assigned by Banshee, a neutral vendor. The only difference will be your starting Landing Zones and the distance you have to travel.

**Q: Can I complete this task while in a squad?**

A: Yes, squad play is highly recommended for this mission due to the high AI count in the southern poppy fields. As long as each member interacts with the document, everyone will receive credit for the objective.
