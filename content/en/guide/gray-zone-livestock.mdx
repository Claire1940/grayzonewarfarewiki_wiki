The 0.4 Spearhead update for Gray Zone Warfare has introduced a series of challenging new tasks that push players into the more dangerous territories of Lamang. Among these is the **gray zone livestock** task, a mission assigned by Banshee that requires tactical precision and keen observation. This task sends operators to the Spielberg area to investigate grim reports of human trafficking centered around an abandoned car repair shop. Completing the **gray zone livestock** mission is essential for players looking to increase their reputation with Banshee and unlock higher-tier equipment in the 2026 meta.

Navigating the outskirts of Nam Theun can be treacherous, especially with the increased AI density introduced in the latest patch. This guide provides a step-by-step breakdown of the objectives, including the exact coordinates for the evidence, the location of the randomized office code, and the best extraction routes to ensure you make it back to base with the documents in hand.

## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out to the field, it is vital to understand the scope of the mission. Banshee is specifically looking for hard evidence of illegal activities. You won't just be clearing out hostiles; you will be acting as an investigator in a high-threat environment.

| Task Element | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vendor** | **Banshee** |
| **Location** | **Spielberg (Nam Theun area)** |
| **Primary Objective** | Investigate car repair shop for trafficking evidence |
| **Secondary Objective** | Find office code and retrieve documents |
| **Recommended LZ** | **November 1 or November 2** |
| **Threat Level** | **Medium-High** |

> **Warning:** The Spielberg area is notorious for AI snipers hidden in the surrounding foliage. Ensure your helmet has a high ballistic rating and carry at least two surgical kits.

## Step 1: Locating the Car Repair Shop

The first phase of the **gray zone livestock** task requires you to fly into the Spielberg region. Most players find that landing at **LZ November 2** provides the most direct approach, though **November 1** offers a more stealthy flanking route through the woods.

Once you arrive, head toward the abandoned car repair shop. This structure serves as the central hub for the mission. Your first objective is to find physical proof of human trafficking. To trigger this objective completion, you must locate the outdoor cages.

### Finding the Evidence
The cages are located in a small wooden shack/structure at the bottom left corner (southwest) of the main car repair shop building. You do not necessarily need to "interact" with a specific item here; usually, getting close to or touching the metal bars of the cages will trigger the sub-task completion.

| Objective Location | Coordinates / Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Trafficking Cages** | Southwest corner of the repair shop compound |
| **Main Office** | Located at the rear of the repair shop building |
| **Code House** | North of the repair shop at **182 127** |

## Step 2: Finding the Car Office Code

After you have confirmed the presence of the cages, the **gray zone livestock** task updates, requiring you to enter the locked office at the back of the shop. This door is secured with a digital padlock, and you cannot breach it with explosives or lockpicks. You must find the specific code left behind by the operators.

Do not waste time searching the repair shop for the code. Instead, head north from the repair shop toward the residential cluster. You are looking for a specific house located roughly at coordinates **182 127**.

1.  Move North from the car repair shop.
2.  Enter the house directly in front of the television set.
3.  Look for a small note or scrap of paper resting on a table or near the TV.
4.  **Note:** This code is randomized for players. Common codes seen in the field include **2896** or **2381**, but you must check the note yourself to ensure you have the correct one for your session.

## Step 3: Retrieving the Documents

With the code memorized (or written down), return to the car repair shop. Head to the back of the building where you will find a door with a keypad. 

### Entering the Office
Interact with the padlock and enter the 4-digit code you found in the northern house. Once inside, the office is relatively small. Look for a stack of papers or a folder—this is the evidence Banshee requires. 

> **💡 Tip:** Once you pick up the documents, they occupy a slot in your quest inventory. If you are killed before extracting, you may lose the item depending on your current faction's persistence rules in the 2026 build. It is highly recommended to head straight to the LZ once the papers are in your possession.

| Item to Retrieve | Visual Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Trafficking Ledger** | A yellow/tan folder on the desk |
| **Employee Records** | Often found near the filing cabinet |
| **The Key Document** | Specifically labeled for the "Livestock" task |

## Tactical Advice for Spielberg

The **gray zone livestock** mission takes place in an area with long sightlines and plenty of concealment for AI. Spielberg is not a location where you want to engage in a prolonged shootout.

### Recommended Loadout
*   **Weapon:** An assault rifle with a 4x or 6x optic is ideal. The distances between the code house and the repair shop are just long enough to make red dots feel inadequate.
*   **Ammo:** Use armor-piercing rounds (AP). The local militia in the 0.4 update often wear Level II or III soft armor.
*   **Utility:** Smoke grenades are incredibly useful for crossing the road between the repair shop and the northern houses, as AI often tracks players across the open asphalt.

### Extraction Strategy
The closest extraction point is usually the one you arrived at (November 2). However, if you hear heavy gunfire or see high player activity at N2, consider trekking East toward the river and looping around to a secondary LZ. The documents are the priority; avoid unnecessary engagements once the objective is complete.

## Summary of Steps

To ensure a successful run of the **gray zone livestock** task, follow this condensed checklist:

1.  Accept the task from **Banshee** at Base Camp.
2.  Infil at **LZ November 2**.
3.  Proceed to the **Car Repair Shop** and walk up to the cages in the wooden shack.
4.  Travel North to the house at **182 127** and read the code by the TV.
5.  Return to the shop, enter the code into the office padlock.
6.  Grab the documents from the desk.
7.  Extract safely and hand the documents to Banshee.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the office code the same for everyone in the gray zone livestock task?**

A: No. The code is randomized. While some players report codes like 2896 or 2381, you must visit the house at coordinates 182 127 to find the specific code generated for your session.

**Q: I reached the cages but the task didn't update. What should I do?**

A: The trigger area for the cages can be narrow. Try walking inside the small shack and standing directly against the cage doors. Ensure you are at the wooden structure southwest of the main repair building.

**Q: Can I complete this task in a squad?**

A: Yes, but every member of the squad needs to pick up their own set of documents from the office. The code, however, should be the same for all members of the same squad in that instance.

**Q: What happens if I die after getting the code but before getting the documents?**

A: You usually do not need to find the code again if you remember it or wrote it down. You can head straight back to the office padlock on your next deployment. However, if you die after picking up the documents, you will likely need to retrieve them again.
