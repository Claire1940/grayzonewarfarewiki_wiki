In the **harvest moon grayzone** task, players are contracted by the enigmatic vendor Banshee to investigate the illicit agricultural operations fueling the island's underground economy. This mission is a multi-stage investigation that takes operators across several dangerous sectors, specifically focusing on hidden poppy fields that are often guarded by local militia forces. Completing the **harvest moon grayzone** objective is essential for players looking to increase their reputation with Banshee and unlock higher-tier equipment suited for stealth and reconnaissance.

## Task Overview: Harvest Moon

The Harvest Moon task is primarily an exploration and intelligence-gathering mission. Banshee is interested in the logistics of the poppy harvests, requiring you to find and "translate" or read specific documents left behind by the overseers. Unlike some combat-heavy missions, your primary goal here is interaction, though the presence of AI at several locations makes a "loud" approach almost inevitable.

### Objective Summary
*   Locate the first poppy field and investigate the labor records.
*   Locate the second poppy field and find the weekly shipment notes.
*   Locate the third poppy field and find the oversight agreement.
*   Locate the fourth poppy field and investigate the discipline notice.
*   Report all findings to Banshee at Base Camp.

> **Warning:** These locations are spread across a wide area. Ensure you have sufficient hydration and food supplies, as the trek between coordinates 172 132 and 149 117 is particularly grueling on foot.

## Location 1: The Eastern Airfield Field (192 154)

The first stop in the **harvest moon grayzone** journey is located to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield. This area is relatively open, making you vulnerable to long-range fire from any AI patrolling the outskirts of the airfield or the nearby Nomad settlement.

To find the document, head to the center of the poppy field. You are looking for a small makeshift workspace or a table. On this table, you will find the **Labor Allocation Request**. Approach the paper and use the interaction key (default 'F') to read/translate the document. This will trigger the task update in your UI.

### Tactical Notes for 192 154
*   **Approach:** Move from the southeast to avoid the heavy patrols at the main airfield gates.
*   **Cover:** The poppies provide visual concealment but zero ballistic protection. If engaged, move toward the nearby treeline immediately.

## Location 2: The South Falcon Field (178 152)

The second poppy field is situated south of the Falcon area (F.A.L.N.G.). This location is characterized by a few small outbuildings and storage sheds used by the workers. 

Search for an open-faced building or a shed near the edge of the field. Inside, you will find the **Weekly Shipment Note**. Reading this note is crucial for understanding the volume of product moving through this sector. 

| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Landmark** | Open-sided wooden structure |
| **Document Type** | Weekly Shipment Note |
| **AI Threat Level** | Low to Moderate |
| **Nearby LZ** | Check your faction's closest LZ south of the Airfield |

## Location 3: The Highland Oversight Field (172 132)

This is arguably the most difficult stage of the **harvest moon grayzone** task. Located at coordinates 172 132, this field is near the "Balloon Crash Site" and is significantly further inland than the first two locations. For Mithras players, this is a shorter walk, but for others, it represents a significant investment in time.

The area is heavily guarded. Expect to encounter between 5 to 10 AI combatants patrolling the perimeter and the center of the field. Once the area is clear, look for a green or yellow folder titled **Protective Oversight Agreement**. It is typically found resting on a crate or a small table in the middle of the agricultural zone.

> **Tip:** Use a suppressed weapon here. The echoes in the highlands can draw in nearby patrols from Ground Zero, turning a simple investigation into a protracted firefight.

## Location 4: The Southern YBL Field (149 117)

The final location takes you to the far south, below the Lima 2 extraction point and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. This area is dense with foliage and features a small residential-style house used by the field overseers.

Instead of looking in the fields, head directly to the house. On the front porch, you will find a book or a set of papers titled **Harvest Discipline Notice**. Translate this final document to complete the investigation phase of the mission.

### Final Document Details
The Harvest Discipline Notice outlines the harsh penalties for workers who fail to meet quotas. This piece of intelligence is what Banshee needs to finalize his report on the local drug trade's hierarchy.

## Survival Strategies for Lamang Island

Completing the **harvest moon grayzone** task is as much about navigation as it is about combat. Here are several strategies to ensure you make it back to base with the intel:

1.  **LZ Hopping:** Don't try to walk to all four locations in one go unless you are heavily supplied. Use your faction's Landing Zones to return to base, restock, and fly back out to the next closest point.
2.  **High Ground Advantage:** The poppy fields are often in depressions or flat areas. Always scout the field from a nearby hill or ridge before entering the center to interact with documents.
3.  **Locked Rooms:** As noted by veteran operators, two of these poppy field locations contain locked rooms. While not required for the task, these rooms often contain high-tier loot. Keep an eye out for keys labeled for "F.A.L.N.G. Storage" or "Poppy Overseer."
4.  **Stamina Management:** In the 2026 build of Gray Zone Warfare, stamina drain is significant when carrying heavy armor. Consider dropping your bag in a safe bush before clearing the poppy field to maintain maximum mobility during the fight.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order for the Harvest Moon Grayzone task?**

A: While the task lists them numerically, you can generally visit the fields in any order that suits your flight path. However, the task UI usually updates sequentially, so following the order in this guide (192 154 to 149 117) is the most reliable way to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I die after finding three of the four documents?**

A: Your progress on the individual objectives is saved. You do not need to revisit the first three fields. Simply head to the final coordinate you missed, interact with the document, and then survive long enough to extract or report back to Banshee.

**Q: Are the documents physical items I need to carry in my inventory?**

A: No, the documents in the **harvest moon grayzone** task are "interaction items." You simply need to stand near them and hold the interaction key to "read" or "translate" them. Once the task checkbox ticks in your menu, you are done with that specific location.

**Q: Can I complete this task in a squad?**

A: Yes, and it is highly recommended. Squad members can cover the perimeter while one player interacts with the document. Note that every player in the squad must individually interact with the document to receive credit for their own task log.
