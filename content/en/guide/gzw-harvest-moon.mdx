## GZW Harvest Moon Task Overview

Before heading out, ensure you have accepted the task from Banshee. The primary objective is to investigate the "hidden poppy fields" and report your findings. Each location contains a specific piece of intel—usually a folder, note, or book—that you must interact with (default key 'F') to progress the quest.

| Objective | Requirement | Location Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field 1 Intel** | Read Labor Allocation Request | East of Airfield (192 154) |
| **Field 2 Intel** | Read Weekly Shipment Note | South of Falcon (178 152) |
| **Field 3 Intel** | Read Protective Oversight Agreement | South of Ghost 3 (172 132) |
| **Field 4 Intel** | Read Harvest Discipline Notice | South of Lima 2 (148 117) |

> **💡 Tip:** This task takes you across a wide horizontal and vertical spread of the map. It is highly recommended to bring at least three spare magazines and plenty of water, as the "haul" between fields can be exhausting for your character's hydration levels.

## Detailed Walkthrough for GZW Harvest Moon

The **gzw harvest moon** task is non-linear in some respects, but following a logical path from North to South (or vice versa) will save you significant time on helicopter rotations. Below is the breakdown of each location based on the most recent 2026 map data.

### 1. Hidden Poppy Field One (Coordinates: 192 154)
Located to the east of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and southeast of the Nomad area, this field is relatively open. You are looking for a small table or crate within the immediate vicinity of the poppy plants.
*   **Target Item:** Labor Allocation Request.
*   **Location Detail:** Check the central work area where laborers would gather. The paper is usually sitting on a makeshift table.

### 2. Hidden Poppy Field Two (Coordinates: 178 152)
This field is situated just south of the Falcon area. It features an open-sided building or shed that serves as a processing point.
*   **Target Item:** Weekly Shipment Note.
*   **Location Detail:** Approach the open building. The note is typically found inside on a desk or a stack of crates. Be wary of AI patrolling the perimeter of the Falcon outskirts.

### 3. Hidden Poppy Field Three (Coordinates: 172 132)
This is arguably the most dangerous leg of the **gzw harvest moon** journey. Located south of Ghost 3 and near Ground Zero, this area is often swarming with high-tier AI.
*   **Target Item:** Protective Oversight Agreement (Green/Yellow folder).
*   **Location Detail:** Look for a green folder on a crate or table in the open processing area. You may need to clear 7-10 AI combatants before it is safe to interact with the document.

### 4. Hidden Poppy Field Four (Coordinates: 148 117)
The final field is located at the far southern end of the map, below the Lima 2 Landing Zone and near the YBL-1/YBO2 complex. 
*   **Target Item:** Harvest Discipline Notice.
*   **Location Detail:** Find the small house adjacent to the field. The intel is a book located on the front porch. Simply "Translate" and "Close" the interaction window to finish the objective.

## Tactical Considerations and AI Threats

While progressing through **gzw harvest moon**, the environment is your greatest enemy. The poppy fields are often located in low-lying areas or depressions, making you vulnerable to snipers or AI positioned on the surrounding ridges.

### Enemy Density by Location
| Location | AI Threat Level | Recommended Gear |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field 1 (192 154)** | Low | Light Armor, Carbine |
| **Field 2 (178 152)** | Medium | Class 3 Armor, Optic |
| **Field 3 (172 132)** | **High** | Class 4+ Armor, Suppressor |
| **Field 4 (148 117)** | Medium | Semi-Auto Rifle, Frag Grenades |

If you are playing as the Mithras faction, the trek to the northern fields is significantly longer than for other factions. Conversely, Crimson Shield International members may find the southern fields more of a logistical challenge. Always check the [Gray Zone Warfare Steam Page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2477940/Gray_Zone_Warfare/) for the latest patch notes, as AI behavior and spawn rates are frequently adjusted.

> **Warning:** Two of these poppy field locations contain locked rooms. While not required for the **gzw harvest moon** task itself, these rooms often contain high-value loot, including rare weapon attachments and medical supplies. If you find a key for a "Poppy Field Storage" or "Processing Shed," keep it in your safe container.

## Loot and Rewards

Completing the task and reporting back to Banshee provides a substantial boost to your standing with him. This is essential for unlocking higher-tier equipment and better ammunition types.

*   **Experience Points:** Significant XP for character leveling.
*   **Banshee Reputation:** Unlocks advanced tactical gear.
*   **Currency:** A standard payout of Lamang Dollars to cover your operational costs.

The **gzw harvest moon** locations are spread out enough that most players will not complete the entire task in a single "life" unless they are very well-prepared or playing in a coordinated squad. If you are playing solo, consider tackling the fields in two separate deployments: the northern two in one run, and the southern two in another.

## Expert Tips for Success

1.  **Use the "Translate" Feature:** When you find a document, ensure you actually click "Translate" if the prompt appears. Simply looking at the item is sometimes insufficient to trigger the quest flag.
2.  **LZ Management:** For Field 4, use the Lima 2 LZ. For Field 3, Ghost 3 is the closest, but be prepared for immediate combat upon landing.
3.  **Check for Other Tasks:** Banshee and other vendors often have tasks in the same general vicinity (like those near the YBL-1 bunker). Try to stack your missions to maximize your efficiency per helicopter flight.
4.  **Ammo Choice:** Since many AI at these fields wear basic vests, 5.45x39mm PP or 5.56x45mm M855 ammunition is usually sufficient to clear the area without wasting expensive armor-piercing rounds.

Finishing **gzw harvest moon** provides a clear path forward in Banshee's questline, eventually leading to more lucrative missions involving the airfield and the central Ground Zero zone. Stay vigilant, watch your corners in the processing sheds, and keep your extraction plan ready at all times.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order for GZW Harvest Moon?**

A: No, you can investigate the four hidden poppy fields in any order. However, the most efficient route is usually determined by your faction's starting location and available Landing Zones.

**Q: I found the document, but the task didn't update. What should I do?**

A: Make sure you interact with the document (Press F) and select the "Translate" or "Read" option. Once the text box appears, you can close it, and the task objective should check off in your menu. If it still doesn't update, try re-logging or checking if you are at the correct set of coordinates.

**Q: Are the poppy fields guarded by boss NPCs?**

A: While there are no unique "named" bosses specifically tied to the **gzw harvest moon** task, the AI density at Field 3 (172 132) is significantly higher than usual. These enemies often carry better weapons and are more aggressive than those found in the starting towns.

**Q: Can I complete this task in a squad?**

A: Yes, and it is highly recommended for Field 3. As long as every member of the squad interacts with the documents individually, everyone will receive credit for the investigation.
