## Tactical Advantages of a Full Squad

Operating at the **gray zone warfare max squad size** provides several mechanical advantages that smaller teams or solo players simply do not have. Since there is a limited number of Little Bird helicopters available for each faction, a full squad of four takes up only one "flight slot." Four solo players flying to the same location would require four separate helicopters, often leading to long wait times at the Base of Operations. By maximizing the **gray zone warfare max squad size**, you ensure efficient deployment and extraction for your entire faction.

## Communication Strategies for 4-Man Teams

With the **gray zone warfare max squad size** being four, communication must be concise. Lamang Island is a persistent world where sound travels realistically. Using the in-game proximity VOIP can alert nearby enemies to your position, so many competitive squads in 2026 utilize external comms or the squad-only radio channel.

*   **Short, Clear Calls:** Instead of saying "He's over there," use compass bearings (e.g., "Contact, 285, behind the blue truck").
*   **Sector Scanning:** Assign each squad member a 90-degree arc to watch while moving through the jungle.
*   **LZ Security:** When waiting for a helicopter, form a 360-degree perimeter. One player watches the air, while the other three watch the surroundings.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when using proximity chat near enemy faction Base of Operations. They can hear your tactical plans if you are within 20-30 meters.
