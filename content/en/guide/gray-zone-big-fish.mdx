Navigating the treacherous terrain of the Midnight Sapphire resort requires more than just firepower; it requires tactical intelligence. In the latest 0.4 Spearhead update, players are tasked with the **gray zone big fish** mission, a multi-stage objective provided by Handshake. This mission involves infiltrating high-security villas, retrieving encrypted hardware, and uncovering the whereabouts of President Narith Sayavong.

The **gray zone big fish** task is notoriously difficult due to the high density of elite AI and the requirement for specific keys that may not be easily accessible. Success in this mission rewards players with significant currency and reputation, but failure carries a heavy penalty. This guide provides a comprehensive breakdown of every objective, from the initial laptop discovery to the final secret room extraction, ensuring you can navigate the Midnight Sapphire hotel and its surrounding villas with confidence.

## Task Objectives and Requirements

Before heading into the field, you must be prepared for a long-haul mission. The "Big Fish" task is not a simple "fetch and extract" job; it requires visiting multiple locations within the Midnight Sapphire region, often forcing you to backtrack between the hotel and the outlying villas.

| Objective | Location | Key Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Locate the Laptop** | Villa Luisa (North of Hotel) | Villa Luisa Key |
| **Find Hardware Key** | Midnight Sapphire Hotel (1st Floor) | None (Quest Item) |
| **Transfer Data** | Villa Luisa | Villa Luisa Key |
| **Retrieve Intel 1** | Hotel (4th Floor) | None |
| **Retrieve Intel 2** | Hotel (4th Floor Secret Room) | None (Button Trigger) |

> **Warning:** This task has a unique failure penalty. If you die while the task is active, you will lose 50 Reputation with Handshake and must wait 20 minutes before the task becomes available again.

## Step 1: Locating the Villa Luisa Laptop

The first phase of the **gray zone big fish** mission takes you to the northern side of the lake, away from the main hotel structure. You are looking for Villa Luisa, a luxury residence that houses the first piece of the puzzle.

To enter the villa, you must possess the **Villa Luisa Key**. Once inside, clear any immediate threats and head toward the bedroom area located behind the bar. On the desk, you will find a government laptop. However, upon interacting with it, you will realize the data is encrypted and requires a physical hardware key to proceed.

## Step 2: Finding the LAF Hardware Key

With the laptop located, your next destination is the first floor of the Midnight Sapphire Hotel (sometimes referred to in-game files as the Vinewood Fair Hotel). This is a high-traffic area, so approach with extreme caution.

1.  **Enter the Main Lobby:** Head to the reception desk on the first floor.
2.  **Locate the Offices:** Behind the reception area, there is a corridor leading to administrative offices.
3.  **Search the Office:** You are looking for the office situated next to the "Midnight Sapphire Hotel Office Key" room.
4.  **Retrieve the Key:** The **LAF Hardware Key** is located on one of the desks in this office. It occupies a 1x1 slot in your inventory.

## Step 3: Data Transfer and Moving to the Fourth Floor

Once you have the hardware key, return to Villa Luisa. Interact with the laptop again to initiate the data transfer. This process takes a moment, so ensure the perimeter is secure before committing to the interaction. After the transfer is complete, your objectives will update, pointing you back to the main hotel—specifically the fourth floor, which serves as the presidential suite level.

### The Fourth Floor Intel Locations

The fourth floor is the most dangerous part of the **gray zone big fish** quest line. The hallways are narrow, and the AI defenders are heavily armored.

| Intel Item | Exact Location | Interaction Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Embassy Message** | 4th Floor, near large meeting room | Pick up from table |
| **Sayavong's Escape Plan** | 4th Floor Secret Room | Press hidden button under desk |

To find the **Embassy Message**, look for the large meeting room on the fourth floor. Just to the right of this room is a private office. The message is sitting clearly on the desk.

To find the **second piece of intel (Escape Plan)**, you must locate the primary office desk in the presidential suite. Look underneath the desk for a hidden button. Pressing "F" on this button will slide a bookshelf or wall panel, revealing a secret room. Inside this hidden compartment, you will find the final documents needed to satisfy Handshake's requirements.

## Tactical Recommendations for Midnight Sapphire

The Midnight Sapphire resort is one of the most challenging POIs in the game. When attempting the **gray zone big fish** task, consider the following gear and strategy:

*   **Ammunition:** Use high-penetration rounds (e.g., M855A1 or 7.62x39 BP) as the guards here often wear Tier 3 or Tier 4 body armor.
*   **Suppressors:** Stealth is your friend. Aggroing the entire hotel staff will lead to a swift trip back to base and a reputation penalty.
*   **Team Play:** It is highly recommended to perform this task with at least one teammate. One player can handle the laptop interaction while the other pulls security on the villa entrance.
*   **Extraction:** The closest extraction points are often contested. Consider clearing the path to the LZ before picking up the final intel.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the hardware key for the Gray Zone Big Fish task?**

A: The LAF Hardware Key is located on the first floor of the Midnight Sapphire Hotel. Go behind the main reception desk into the administrative offices. It is on a desk in the office adjacent to the locked Hotel Office room.

**Q: Can I complete Big Fish without the Villa Luisa Key?**

A: No. The Villa Luisa Key is required to enter the villa where the laptop is located. You can find this key by looting AI in the Midnight Sapphire area or trading with other players.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the intel?**

A: If you die while the task is active, you will fail the mission. You will lose 50 reputation points with Handshake and must wait 20 minutes for the task to reset so you can attempt it again.

**Q: How do I open the secret room on the fourth floor?**

A: Go to the main office on the fourth floor of the hotel. Look under the large wooden desk for a prompt to "Press Button." This will open the secret door containing the final piece of intel.
