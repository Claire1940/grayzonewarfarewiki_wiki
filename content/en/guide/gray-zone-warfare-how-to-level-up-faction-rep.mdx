## Understanding the Reputation and Vendor System

In Gray Zone Warfare, your progress is gated by "Vendor Tiers." To reach Tier 2, Tier 3, and beyond, you must meet two specific criteria for each vendor: a minimum Reputation (Rep) score and a minimum amount of Money Spent (Buy/Sell volume). While spending money is relatively straightforward, earning reputation is the primary bottleneck for most players.

Reputation is earned exclusively through the completion of Tasks (missions) assigned by the various vendors at your Base of Operations. Each vendor—Handshake, Gunny, Lab Rat, Artisan, Turncoat, and Banshee—has their own track of missions that often overlap or conflict.

| Vendor | Primary Goods | How to Increase Rep |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Handshake** | Basic Gear, Storage, M4s | Tactical missions and outpost clearing. |
| **Gunny** | Weapons, Ammo, Weapon Parts | Combat-focused tasks and supply retrieval. |
| **Lab Rat** | Medical Supplies, Stimulants | Scientific recovery and survivor rescue. |
| **Artisan** | Specialized Gear, AK-series | Engineering tasks and technical loot recovery. |
| **Turncoat** | High-tier Military Gear | Intelligence gathering and clandestine ops. |
| **Banshee** | Clandestine/Rare Items | High-risk PvP and deep-territory scouting. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check multiple vendors before heading out. Often, you can complete three or four tasks in a single deployment if they are located in the same Point of Interest (POI).

## Completing Tasks: The Fastest Path to Rep

The absolute fastest way to solve the puzzle of **gray zone warfare how to level up faction rep** is to prioritize the "Main Story" tasks. Early missions in the starting town (such as Nam Thaven or Mithras' equivalent) provide small amounts of rep but unlock the larger, more lucrative chains in high-tier areas like Midnight Sapphire and Fort Narith.

### Task Efficiency Strategies
1.  **Squad Coordination:** If you are playing with friends, ensure everyone is on the same task. This allows you to share key access and clear AI-controlled factions more effectively.
2.  **Key Farming:** Many tasks are locked behind specific doors. Farming keys from AI guards in areas like the Blue Lagoon or Sawmill is essential.
3.  **Night Operations:** With the 0.4 update, AI vision is slightly reduced at night. Use this to your advantage to slip into high-risk areas like the Pha Lang Airfield to complete "plant" or "retrieve" tasks without engaging the entire Viper Tactical Services garrison.

## Maximizing Vendor Spend with Item Preferences

Even if you have the reputation required, you won't level up your vendor if you haven't "spent" enough money with them. The 0.3 Winds of War update introduced **Vendor Preferences**, meaning certain vendors pay significantly more for specific items. To optimize your **gray zone warfare how to level up faction rep** journey, you must sell your loot to the right person.

| Item Type | Best Vendor to Sell | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Guns & Ammo** | Gunny | Highest buyback rates for firearms. |
| **Phones & Electronics** | Lab Rat | Pays a premium for communication devices. |
| **Trackers & Cameras** | Handshake / Banshee | Essential for their specific operational needs. |

## High-Tier Looting: Midnight Sapphire Guide

To afford the gear required for late-game tasks, you need a reliable source of income. Midnight Sapphire remains the gold standard for high-value looting in 2026. By targeting specific villas, you can find items that satisfy the spending requirements for multiple vendors at once.

### The Villa Loot Route
-   **Villa Louisa:** Requires the Louisa Key. This location is packed with jewelry boxes, safes, and luxury watches. It also contains a military gear crate that frequently spawns Tier 3+ armor.
-   **Villa Monica:** Focus on the garage for military firearms and high-tier attachments like the AK-19 suppressor or Razor scopes.
-   **Hotel Pyram:** A high-density AI area, but the loot inside the hotel rooms includes rare passports and laptops that Lab Rat and Artisan value highly.

When looting these areas, remember that **gray zone warfare how to level up faction rep** is often about survival. If you die before reaching the extraction point (LZ), you lose the loot and the potential spend progress.

## Combat Stats and Survival for Rep Farming

You cannot gain reputation if you are constantly stuck in the "respawn" loop. The 2026 meta emphasizes three specific stats that every operator must monitor to ensure successful task completion.

1.  **MOA (Minute of Angle):** This determines your weapon's mechanical accuracy. For long-range tasks in the jungle, ensure your weapon has a low MOA (under 1.5) to avoid missing critical headshots on armored AI.
2.  **Arm Stamina Drain:** This dictates how long you can hold your Aim Down Sights (ADS). In high-pressure areas like Tiger Bay, high arm stamina allows you to hold angles longer against pushing AI.
3.  **Rain Protection:** A new stat in recent updates. Keeping your character dry prevents stamina penalties and "shaking" during precision shots in the tropical storms of Lamang.

## Advanced Tips for Faction Progression

-   **Stacking Trackers:** You can stack the basic trackers found throughout the world to save inventory space while maximizing the "spend" value when selling to Handshake.
-   **Blood Tracking:** If you wound an enemy PMC or a high-value AI boss, look for blood trails on the floor. In indoor environments like the YBL-1 bunker, this is the most reliable way to find a hiding enemy.
-   **Bush Stealth:** Moving your body in a bush creates significant noise. However, using the "Free Look" key (default: Alt) allows you to scan your surroundings with significantly less acoustic signature.
-   **River Currents:** When crossing water to reach an LZ, always move with the current. Moving against the current drains stamina faster and makes you a louder, slower target for snipers.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I level up reputation by just killing AI?**

A: No. While killing AI is necessary for survival and some specific "kill count" tasks, the only way to gain reputation points is by successfully completing and turning in tasks to vendors at the base.

**Q: What happens to my faction rep if I wipe my character?**

A: Wiping your character resets all progress, including your level, inventory, and all vendor reputation. You will start back at Tier 1 with zero rep. Note that you must wait at least 7 days after creating a character before you are allowed to perform a wipe.

**Q: Why isn't my vendor leveling up even though I have enough rep?**

A: You likely haven't met the "Money Spent" requirement. Check the vendor screen to see how much more you need to buy or sell. Remember to use the vendor preference list to reach this goal faster by selling high-value items to the correct NPC.

**Q: Do all vendors share the same reputation?**

A: No, each vendor has an independent reputation score. Completing a task for Lab Rat will only increase your rep with her, not with Gunny or Handshake. However, some late-game tasks may reward reputation for multiple vendors simultaneously.
