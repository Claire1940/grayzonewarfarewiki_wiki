Completing the **gzw livestock** task is a crucial step for players looking to increase their reputation with Banshee in the latest 0.4 update of Gray Zone Warfare. This mission requires a mix of reconnaissance, exploration, and tactical extraction as you navigate a hostile village to uncover dark secrets regarding local activity. Like many tasks in the Spearhead update, the **gzw livestock** mission rewards players who pay close attention to environmental cues and maintain a high level of situational awareness.

In this guide, we will break down every objective of the mission, from the initial discovery of the holding cages to the retrieval of the encrypted car office code. Whether you are playing solo or with a squad, understanding the layout of the car repair shop and the surrounding residential houses is vital for survival. Follow these steps carefully to ensure you secure the necessary intel and make it back to base in one piece.

## GZW Livestock: Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out into the field, it is important to understand what Banshee expects from you. This task is centered around investigating reports of illegal "livestock" transport, which turns out to be far more sinister than simple animal husbandry. You will be operating in a high-traffic area, so coming prepared with the right gear is non-negotiable.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vendor** | **Banshee** |
| **Location** | Southwest of Car Repair Shop / Northern House |
| **Objectives** | Locate cages, find office code, retrieve intel |
| **Recommended Level** | 15+ (Spearhead Update) |
| **Key Item Needed** | Randomized Code (found in-mission) |

> **💡 Tip:** Ensure you have at least two empty inventory slots (2x1) for the intel document you need to retrieve at the end of the mission.

## Step 1: Locate the Cages and Wooden Shack

The first phase of the **gzw livestock** mission begins at the southwestern edge of the designated objective circle. You are looking for a specific wooden shack and a series of cages that indicate where the "livestock" was being held.

1.  **Approach the Car Repair Shop**: Head toward the industrial zone in the village.
2.  **Navigate Southwest**: Move to the bottom-left corner of the car repair shop area.
3.  **Identify the Shack**: Look for a small, weathered wooden structure surrounded by cages.
4.  **Trigger the Objective**: Simply walking up to these cages should trigger the first sub-task completion in your menu.

This area is often patrolled by 2-3 AI combatants. It is recommended to clear the perimeter before standing out in the open near the cages, as the shack provides very little hard cover from incoming fire.

## Step 2: Finding the Car Office Code

Once you have confirmed the location of the cages, you will find that the main office building nearby is locked with a keypad or padlock that requires a specific code. Unlike some keys in Gray Zone Warfare that can be looted from any AI, the **gzw livestock** code is located in a fixed residential building to the north.

| Objective | Location Description | Key Item |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Search the House** | North of the mission circle | **Note with Code** |
| **Identify Room** | Living room area near the television | **Car Office Code** |
| **Code Variation** | Unique per instance (e.g., 2896) | **Intel Access** |

Head north from the cages toward the residential houses. You are looking for a house that appears relatively intact compared to the surrounding ruins. Enter the building and locate the television set. On a small table or stand near the TV, you will find a note. Interacting with this note will reveal your specific code. 

> **Warning:** The code is randomized for different players or instances. Do not rely on codes found in old forum posts; you must physically interact with the note in your current session to progress.

## Step 3: Entering the Office and Extracting Intel

With the code in hand, return to the original objective site near the cages. The office entrance is typically located at the back of the building adjacent to the wooden shack. 

Follow these steps to finish the **gzw livestock** task:
*   **Locate the Padlock**: Approach the back door of the office building.
*   **Enter the Code**: Use the code you found at the northern house to unlock the door.
*   **Grab the Documents**: The intel is usually sitting on a desk or a filing cabinet inside the small office space.
*   **Secure the Item**: Place the document in your secure container (if it fits) or your backpack.

Once the document is in your possession, your priority shifts to extraction. The village is a notorious "hot zone" for player vs. player encounters, especially near mission-critical buildings.

## Common Hazards and Tips

The area surrounding the **gzw livestock** objectives is deceptively dangerous. While the AI may seem sparse, they often use the dense foliage and dilapidated walls to set up ambushes.

*   **Check the Roofs**: The car repair shop has several elevated positions where AI can spawn with SKS rifles. Always scan the skyline before approaching the cages.
*   **The "Invisible" Code**: If the note doesn't appear near the TV, try relogging or checking the kitchen counter. In rare cases, the prop may glitch through the furniture.
*   **Squad Coordination**: If playing in a group, have one player stay at the office while the other two fetch the code. This prevents the area from being re-occupied by enemy patrols while you are away.
*   **Extraction Pathing**: Don't take the main road back to the LZ. Use the forest line to the west of the car repair shop to avoid being spotted by squads arriving to start their own missions.

## Mission Rewards

Upon successful extraction and handing the intel over to Banshee, you will receive a significant boost to your standing. This is essential for unlocking higher-tier suppressors and specialized ammunition.

*   **Experience Points**: 1,200 XP
*   **Vendor Reputation**: +150 (Banshee)
*   **Currency**: $4,500 (approximate)
*   **Equipment**: Usually includes a set of rare medical supplies or ammunition crates.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I find the gzw livestock code before visiting the cages?**

A: Yes, you can technically head straight to the house to get the code, but the mission log may not update correctly until you have visited the southwest cages first. It is safer to follow the objectives in order.

**Q: Does the code change if I die and come back?**

A: No, the code is typically tied to your character's specific mission instance. However, if you join a different server or a different friend's squad, you should double-check the note again to ensure the code hasn't changed.

**Q: Is the office key/code used for any other missions?**

A: Currently, the code found in the **gzw livestock** task is specific to this objective. However, the office itself contains minor loot spawns that can be useful for scavenging in later raids.

**Q: What happens if I lose the intel document?**

A: If you die before extracting or placing the document in your secure container, you will need to return to the office. If the door is already unlocked, you can simply walk in and grab it again. If the door has reset, you may need to re-verify the code at the northern house.
