## Muddy Buddy Task Overview

The **Muddy Buddy** task is one of the more elusive early-game missions because it relies on environmental clues rather than a fixed waypoint on your map. Handshake tasks you with retrieving a package hidden in a barrel near a "confluence"—a point where two bodies of water meet. Because your starting location depends on your chosen faction (Mithras, Crimson Shield, or Lamang Recovery), the specific riverbank you need to scout will vary.

### Mission Objectives
*   Locate the package hidden in a barrel on the riverbank.
*   The location is always south of your faction's starter town.
*   Search near a confluence (where the river splits or joins).
*   Extract the package and return it to Handshake at Base Camp.

| Faction | Search Area | Clue |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crimson Shield** | South of Kiu Vongsa | Look for the "Y" split in the river. |
| **Mithras** | South of Nam Thaven | Follow the river downstream to the confluence. |
| **Lamang Recovery** | South of Pha Lang | Check the riverbank downstream from the junction. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always put quest items like the Muddy Buddy package in your **Safe Pocket** (Kester) immediately. If you are killed during extraction, the item remains with you, saving you a return trip to the dangerous riverbank.

## Step-by-Step Guide for Muddy Buddy

To complete this mission in 2026, you need to identify the blue barrels scattered along the riverbank. There are typically three barrels in the vicinity of the confluence. The package does not always spawn in the same barrel for every player, so you may need to check all three.

1.  **Deploy to the nearest LZ:** Choose the Landing Zone closest to the southern edge of your starter town.
2.  **Navigate to the Water:** Move toward the river and follow it south until you see where the water branches.
3.  **Identify the Barrels:** Look for bright blue plastic barrels sitting in the mud or near the tall grass.
4.  **Loot the Package:** Interact with the barrels. The package is a small, rectangular box.
5.  **Extract Safely:** Once the package is secured, head to the nearest extraction point. Avoid the main roads, as players often camp these routes.

## Related "Buddy" and Squad Missions

While "Muddy Buddy" is the primary task associated with the name, several other missions in Gray Zone Warfare require you to find "buddies" or missing squad members. These are often high-risk operations because they take you into areas heavily populated by AI or rival PMCs.

### The Stand & Leave No Man Behind
These tasks are issued by Gunny and involve locating fallen operatives. In **The Stand**, you must find out what happened to a PMC squad that went missing in the jungle or near urban centers like Ban Pa.

### Finding the PMC Squad in Ban Pa
Ban Pa is a notorious hotspot for hostile activity. When performing the squad-related tasks here, you are looking for specific environmental markers. In many cases, the "squad" is found near the main road leading into the village or near the hidden docks.

1.  **Scout the Perimeter:** Use a suppressed weapon to clear the outskirts of Ban Pa.
2.  **Check the Roadside:** Many missing squad members are found near crashed vehicles or temporary checkpoints.
3.  **Interact with Bodies:** You must physically stand over the bodies and interact with them to "confirm" the status of the squad.

## Essential Gear for Buddy Missions

Buddy missions often require long treks through the jungle or deep into enemy-controlled villages. You should prepare your loadout specifically for endurance and stealth.

*   **Suppressed Weapons:** Essential for avoiding attracting the entire AI population of a town while you search for barrels or bodies.
*   **Hydration and Nutrition:** Searching for barrels near confluences can take longer than expected. Bring at least two bottles of water and a food item.
*   **Medical Supplies:** Bring at least two **Combat Tourniquets** and a **SurKit**. The riverbanks are often open areas where you are vulnerable to snipers.
*   **Safe Pocket Storage:** Ensure your Kester or equivalent safe container is empty before you leave, so you have room for the task items.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The confluence areas are often "kill zones" where snipers from other factions wait for players to interact with the barrels. Never approach a barrel without scanning the surrounding ridgeline first.

## FAQ

**Q: I found the barrels for Muddy Buddy, but there is no package. What should I do?**

A: There are usually three barrels at the confluence. Check all of them. If none contain the package, it is possible another player has recently taken it. You may need to restart your session or wait for the loot to respawn.

**Q: Do I need a specific key for the buddy missions in Ban Pa?**

A: Most "find the squad" missions do not require keys. However, related tasks in Ban Pa, like "The Shadow Over Ban Pa," might require you to enter locked buildings. For the basic squad discovery, you only need to find the bodies in the open world.

**Q: Can I complete Muddy Buddy with a group?**

A: Yes, but only one person can pick up the package per instance. If your entire squad needs the mission, you will either need to wait for a respawn or run the mission multiple times in different instances.

**Q: Where is the confluence for the Crimson Shield faction?**

A: It is located directly south of Kiu Vongsa. Look for where the main river splits into a small "Y" shape. The barrels are usually positioned right at the edge of the muddy bank near the water's edge. Use this **gray zone warfare buddy missions guide** to pinpoint the exact riverbank before you deploy.
