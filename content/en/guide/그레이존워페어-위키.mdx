## Who is Vulture? The Secret Scavenger of Lamang

Vulture is a unique NPC vendor who specializes in "recovered" items. In the lore of Gray Zone Warfare, he follows the trails of carnage left behind by PMCs, gathering the weapons, armor, and backpacks that were left on the battlefield. Because his inventory is sourced from dead players, the items he sells are often pre-modified with high-end attachments, making him an incredible resource for low-level players who haven't yet unlocked Level 4 loyalty with standard vendors.

In the 2026 meta, Vulture serves two primary purposes:
1.  **Gear Accessibility:** Providing high-tier weapons (like the M14 or suppressed M4s) to players regardless of their level.
2.  **Stash Management:** Offering the Small and Large Safes, which are mandatory for organized high-value loot storage.

> **💡 Tip:** Vulture's inventory is server-dependent. If you don't like what he is selling on one server, switching to a different region or session might reveal better gear salvaged from different player skirmishes.

## How to Unlock the Vulture Shop

Even if you arrive at the correct COP where Vulture is located, you cannot simply walk up and trade with him. Vulture only does business with the faction that currently controls the territory. This adds a layer of PvPvE tension to every shopping trip.

### Steps to Capture a COP:
1.  **Clear the Area:** Eliminate all AI defenders and any rival PMCs within the perimeter of the Command Post.
2.  **Locate the Terminal:** Every major COP has a central computer terminal.
3.  **Check the Status:** If the terminal light is **Gray**, there are still enemies nearby. If it is **Yellow**, the area is clear and ready for capture.
4.  **Interact:** Once captured, the COP belongs to your faction, and Vulture will immediately become accessible in your "Vendors" menu tab as long as you remain within the zone.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Capturing a COP broadcasts your faction's presence. Be prepared for counter-attacks from rival factions who may want to claim the vendor for themselves.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I access Vulture in PvE mode?**

A: Yes, Vulture is available in both PvP and PvE modes. In PvE, you still need to capture the COP from AI, but you won't have to worry about rival PMCs stealing the terminal.

**Q: Does Vulture's location change mid-week?**

A: No, the rotation is strictly weekly. Once he is found at a location like Harrison or Titan, he will remain there until the weekly reset.

**Q: Why is Vulture locked even though I am at the COP?**

A: You must ensure your faction has captured the COP. Check the terminal; if it isn't yellow, the shop won't open. Also, ensure you are checking the **그레이존워페어 위키** to confirm he is actually scheduled for that location this week.

**Q: Is it better to sell high-value loot to Lab Rat or Vulture?**

A: If you need money, Lab Rat often pays more. However, if you need the Large Safe, you must sell to Vulture to gain the necessary reputation, even if his buy prices are slightly lower (often 50% of standard value).
