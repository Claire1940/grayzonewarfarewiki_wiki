## Starting Town Stash Locations

Every faction begins in a mirrored starting town located just outside their respective base camps. While the orientation of the towns differs, the layouts and loot spawns remain consistent. These areas are perfect for early-game farming, especially if you are looking for the M700 sniper rifle or basic plate carriers.

The primary point of interest in the starting town is **The Hideout**. Inside this building, you will find a UN Firearms box and two additional equipment boxes. Tactical data suggests that the UN Firearms box has approximately a 50% chance to spawn an M700, making it a priority for players who prefer long-range engagements.

### Starting Town Loot Overview

| Location | Requirement | Primary Loot |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Hideout** | None | M700, UN Firearms Box, Equipment |
| **Market Roof Room** | None | Medical Box, UN Firearms Box, Floor Spawns |
| **Water Tower Room** | UNL A Key | UN Firearms Box, Equipment Chest |
| **Lumber Yard Office** | Maintenance Key | Firearms Box, High-Value Gear |

For those without keys, the **Market Area** offers a hidden gem. By clearing the central building and ascending the rear stairs, you can access the roof. Following the pathway around the building leads to a back door containing a dedicated loot room. This room is frequently overlooked and contains a medical box alongside weapon spawns.

> **💡 Tip:** Check the floor in the Market Roof Room; high-value attachments often spawn outside of the crates themselves.

## The Sawmill: A Mid-Game Goldmine

The Sawmill is one of the most efficient **gray zone warfare stash locations** due to its high density of boxes in a relatively small, open area. Because the enemies are spread out, a player with a scoped rifle can clear the perimeter and loot up to five boxes without needing a single key.

Start your run at the **Office Building**. Here, you will find a firearms box and various loose weapons on the floor. Moving north to the **Warehouse** provides a medical box for restocking supplies. The western side of the Sawmill features a large warehouse containing a firearms box that frequently drops CQS, Mosins, and AK-series rifles.

1.  **Office Building:** Main hub for firearms and floor attachments.
2.  **North Warehouse:** Primarily medical supplies.
3.  **West Warehouse:** High-tier rifle spawns (AKs, Mosins).
4.  **Truck Spawns:** Two trucks located south of the West Warehouse contain a firearms box and an equipment box (plate vests).
5.  **Storage Shed:** Requires the **Storage Shed Key** for an extra firearms and equipment box combo.

## Coastal Looting in Ban Pa

Ban Pa is a coastal village that offers a mix of open-access stashes and highly valuable locked rooms. It is a favorite for players looking to flip gear quickly due to the proximity of the loot to the extraction points.

The **Docks** are the most reliable open spot in Ban Pa. By clearing the town and heading to the water's edge, you can find a room containing both a UN Firearms box and an equipment box. If you are lucky enough to find or trade for the **Fishing Hut Key**, you can unlock a small shack on the coast that provides a significant boost to your weapon and gear reserves.

> **Warning:** Ban Pa is a high-traffic area. AI enemies are manageable, but player encounters are frequent near the docks.

## YBL-1: The Hidden Bunker Entrance

While most players attempt to fight through the main entrance of the YBL-1 bunker, savvy operators use the northern "backdoor." Following a dirt path outside the main wall leads to a square hole in the ground. This drop-in point is one of the most tactical **gray zone warfare stash locations** because it places you directly next to a firearms box.

If you possess the **Storage Key**, you can open the door on the right side of this corridor. This room contains an equipment box and a second firearms box. Furthermore, a hole in the floor of this room allows you to bypass the main bunker defenses, giving you a stealthy advantage over players coming from the front.

## Tiger Bay: High-Tier Endgame Farming

Tiger Bay is currently the most dangerous POI in the game, but the rewards match the risk. The AI here is aggressive and well-equipped, so it is highly recommended to enter this zone with a squad.

The **Mall** is the centerpiece of Tiger Bay. It is massive, featuring dozens of cash registers that can net you thousands of dollars in a single run. The south side of the mall contains equipment boxes on the lower floor, while the upper floor (accessible via escalator) houses a firearms box that can contain fully kitted M4s.

### Tiger Bay Key Stashes

| Location | Key Required | Notable Loot |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Refugee Camp Truck** | None | UN Firearms Box (Trunk) |
| **Refugee Camp Corner** | None | Equipment Chest |
| **Pier Locked Room** | **Pier Key** | Kitted M4s, Voodoo Sights, Tier 3+ Armor |

The **Pier** is perhaps the single best loot room in Gray Zone Warfare. Requiring the Pier Key, this room is famous for spawning M4A1s already equipped with Voodoo sights—attachments that are otherwise locked behind Level 3 vendors or extremely rare world drops. The gear chest here also provides Level 3+ rated armor, which is essential for surviving late-game missions.

## Essential Keys for Stash Access

While many **gray zone warfare stash locations** are accessible for free, the highest quality loot is often behind locked doors. Collecting these keys should be a priority for any player looking to maximize their farming efficiency.

*   **UNL A Key:** Unlocks the Water Tower in the starting town.
*   **Lumber Yard Maintenance Key:** Grants access to the high-value office in the Sawmill area.
*   **Storage Shed Key:** Essential for completionists farming the Sawmill.
*   **Fishing Hut Key:** Provides a safe, isolated loot drop in Ban Pa.
*   **Pier Key:** The holy grail for Tiger Bay runs, providing endgame weapons and armor.

> **💡 Tip:** Keys can often be found on the bodies of AI enemies within the same POI. If you are missing a key, spend some time clearing the local guards before leaving the area.

## FAQ

**Q: Do the stashes in Gray Zone Warfare respawn?**

A: Yes, stashes and loot boxes respawn over time. If a box is empty, it means another player has recently looted it. You may need to wait or join a different server instance to find the crates refreshed.

**Q: Where can I find the M700 sniper rifle early?**

A: The best **gray zone warfare stash locations** for the M700 are in the starting town Hideout and the UN boxes located in the Water Tower (requires UNL A Key). There is roughly a 50% spawn rate in these specific crates.

**Q: Is it worth farming Tiger Bay solo?**

A: Farming Tiger Bay solo is extremely difficult due to the high density of elite AI. While the loot (like Voodoo sights and Tier 3 armor) is incredible, it is much safer and more efficient to go with at least one or two teammates to cover your flanks.

**Q: Can I find Voodoo scopes in the starting town?**

A: No, Voodoo scopes are generally reserved for high-tier areas like Tiger Bay or the Pier. In the starting town, you are more likely to find basic red dots or low-magnification optics. For the best scopes, you must venture into the more dangerous southern regions of the map.
