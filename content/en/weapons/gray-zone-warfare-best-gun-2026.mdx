2. **Infiltration:** Fly to Delta 3 and head to the mound overlooking the training course (Coordinates 140 31).
3. **Clear the Perimeter:** Use your sniper to pick off the guards on the roof and the general patrolling the courtyard.
4. **Enter the Barracks:** Move cautiously toward the barracks building. Use a close-quarters weapon (like a scavenged AK-12) for the interior.

7. **Extraction:** Exit through the runway area where guard density is lower and head to Savannah to secure your loot.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute gray zone warfare best gun 2026 for solo players?**

A: The DDM4 Phantom Lance is the best choice for solo players due to its integrated suppressor and high-capacity magazines. It allows you to eliminate targets stealthily and handle multiple AI attackers without constant reloading.

**Q: Can I use FMJ ammo in high-tier areas like Midnight Sapphire?**

A: Yes. While AP ammo is better for torso shots against armored targets, FMJ ammo is highly effective for headshots. In the 2026 meta, precision is rewarded more than spray-and-pray tactics.

**Q: How do I get the new M1A EBR?**

A: The M1A is one of the eight new guns added in the Spearhead update. You can purchase the base M14 from vendors after increasing your reputation or find the EBR chassis parts as high-value loot in military armories and boss crates.

**Q: Are grenades and throwables effective in the 2026 update?**

A: Absolutely. Grenades have been completely reworked with realistic fragmentation physics. They are now essential for clearing rooms in POIs and can ricochet off surfaces to hit enemies behind cover.
