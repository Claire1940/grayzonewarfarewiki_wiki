## The Secret Vendor: Locating Vulture

One of the most significant additions to the tactical landscape is the vendor known as Vulture. Unlike standard vendors like Gunny or Handshake, Vulture does not stay at your base. He is a scavenger who roams the map, appearing only at captured Major Control Points (COPs). 

To interact with Vulture, you must first locate which COP he is currently occupying. He operates on a weekly rotation, moving between two specific Major COPs at any given time. These locations are marked on your map with three black dots rather than the standard single dot.

| Major COP Name | Region | Tactical Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Westmore** | Western Sector | High-traffic PvP zone |
| **Stalwart** | Northern Border | Excellent for military loot |
| **Crusader** | Central Lamang | Centralized for fast extraction |
| **Bronco** | Southern Coast | High AI density |
| **Titan** | Eastern Jungle | Difficult terrain, high reward |
| **Harrison** | Industrial District | Close to Midnight Sapphire |
| **Winchester** | Rural Outskirts | Good for beginner farming |
| **Nomad** | Desert Fringe | Long sightlines for snipers |

### How to Capture a COP for Vulture
If you arrive at a Major COP and the terminal is gray, it means the area is still contested by AI or rival PMCs. You must clear the area entirely until the terminal turns yellow. Once captured, Vulture will appear in your vendor list, allowing you to buy scavenged PMC gear and, more importantly, high-tier storage blueprints.

> **💡 Tip:** Vulture sells fully repaired weapons (95-100% durability) that he has "scavenged" from fallen players. This is an excellent way for low-level players to acquire high-tier guns like the M14 before leveling up their base vendors.

## Unlocking the M700 Chameleon Blueprint

For snipers, the M700 Chameleon is a must-have variant. This weapon isn't just a cosmetic upgrade; it represents a successful infiltration of one of the most dangerous zones in the game. To unlock it, you need to find a specific magazine/manual located in the hotel near Louisa.

### The Hotel Infiltration Steps
1. **LZ Selection:** Land at Hotel Tower 1 and approach the villas with caution.
2. **Clear the Perimeter:** There are usually three guards patrolling the entrance. Use a shotgun or suppressed SMG for close-quarters work.
3. **The Rooftop Snipers:** Before entering the main building, look up. There are typically three snipers on the roof. If you can't see them from the ground, they may be aggroed elsewhere.
4. **The Boss Fight:** A boss often spawns in the upper suites. Clearing him may grant you an engraved weapon, but your main goal is the desk in the corner office.
5. **The Blueprint Manual:** Locate the magazine on the desk. Picking it up will trigger a notification from Banshee.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Target Item** | M700 Camo Manual |
| **Location** | Hotel near Louisa (Top Floor) |
| **Unlock Condition** | Hand in to Banshee at base |
| **Reward** | Ability to purchase M700 Chameleon |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The "Death Squad"—a 4-5 man patrol—often roams the path between the villas and the hotel. If you spot them, use the high ground and the SVDs scavenged from roof snipers to pick them off from a distance.

## Stash Management: Small and Large Safes

A core part of any **gray zone warfare blueprints guide** is stash optimization. Vulture is the only vendor who sells specialized safes. These are essential for players who are tired of managing dozens of wallets or losing high-value loot to inventory clutter.

### Safe Tiers and Benefits
*   **Level 3 Vulture:** Unlocks the **Small Safe**.
*   **Level 4 Vulture:** Unlocks the **Large Safe** (108 slots).

These safes allow you to store massive amounts of currency and high-value items (gold bars, watches, rare documents) in a single inventory block. To reach Level 4 with Vulture, you cannot simply do missions. You must sell him specific military equipment and high-tier valuables.

### Best Items to Sell Vulture for Reputation
Selling standard weapons to Vulture provides minimal XP. To level him quickly, focus on farming the following areas:
*   **Midnight Sapphire:** Look for "Military Equipment" items on guards.
*   **Boss Loot:** Rare items found near bosses (like the 60k value military item at the top of Midnight Sapphire) provide the largest reputation boosts.
*   **High-Value Loot:** Items normally sold to Lab Rat can often be sold to Vulture for half the price but significantly more reputation XP.

## Tactical Advice for Blueprint Hunting

When following this **gray zone warfare blueprints guide**, remember that Lamang is an unforgiving environment. Blueprint runs often take you into high-risk areas like Midnight Sapphire or the Louisa Hotel. 

*   **Bring Grenades:** When clearing the hotel, AI often hides in corners or stairwells. Tossing a grenade up the stairs is safer than a blind peek.
*   **Check Durability:** Scavenged SVDs from snipers are often at 60% durability. They are fine for a single run, but do not rely on them for long-term operations without repairs.
*   **Inventory Space:** If you are hunting for the 60k military item, ensure you have a large backpack. These items often take up a 3x3 or 4x4 slot and cannot be placed in small tactical rigs.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I find Vulture on PvE servers?**

A: Yes, Vulture appears in the exact same locations on both PvP and PvE servers. The only difference is the competition you might face from other players when trying to capture the Major COP.

**Q: Does the M700 Chameleon manual respawn?**

A: Yes, the manual is a world-spawn item. If another player has already taken it, you may need to wait for the instance to reset or join a different server to find it on the desk.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level Vulture in this gray zone warfare blueprints guide?**

A: The most efficient method is farming Midnight Sapphire for high-value military loot. A single successful run can net you over 70,000 worth of items, which translates to roughly 700 reputation XP when sold to Vulture.

**Q: Do I need to unlock Banshee before I can get the M700 Chameleon?**

A: You can pick up the blueprint manual at any level, but you will not be able to purchase the camo version of the M700 until you have officially unlocked Banshee through his standard questline. Once unlocked, the Chameleon variant will be available in his shop immediately if you have handed in the manual.
