## Overview of the Standard Traders

There are six primary vendors available to players at the base camp or any captured Command Outpost (COP). These traders follow a standard progression from Level 1 to Level 4. To level them up, you must increase your reputation through tasks, and meet specific financial requirements by buying from or selling to them.

| Trader | Primary Specialization | Key Level 4 Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Handshake** | Armor, Rigging, & Logistics | RPC Armor, Large Containers |
| **Gunny** | Western Firearms & Ammo | M4A1, DDM4, Firearms Crate |
| **Lab Rat** | Medical Supplies & Provisions | EMT Case, Advanced Stems |
| **Artisan** | Eastern Firearms & Tools | SVD, Advanced Key Case |
| **Turncoat** | AK Variants & Large Bags | Delta Backpack, Grenade Box |
| **Banshee** | Special Ops & Scopes | MP7, MCX, Razer Scope |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not focus on "power leveling" Handshake by selling him items. He has the most missions in the game, and you will naturally hit Level 4 just by completing his questline.

## The Secret Vendor: Vulture

Unlike the standard six, Vulture is a "Secret Vendor" who does not stay at your base. He is a scavenger who collects gear left behind by fallen PMCs and resells it at a discount. In 2026, finding Vulture is a weekly ritual for high-level players looking for the massive storage safes.

### How to Find Vulture
Vulture rotates weekly between two "Major COPs" on the map. These are the locations marked with three black dots rather than one. Common locations include:
*   Westmore
*   Stalwart
*   Crusader
*   Bronco
*   Titan
*   Harrison
*   Winchester
*   Nomad

To access Vulture, your faction must capture the COP where he is currently residing. Once the main computer at the COP turns **Yellow**, the zone is cleared, and Vulture will appear in your vendor menu.

### Vulture Progression & Rewards
Vulture is unique because he does not offer many missions. To level him up, you must sell him specific "Military Equipment" found in high-tier POIs like Midnight Sapphire.

| Reputation Level | Unlockable Rewards |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Salvaged PMC Weapons & Armor |
| **Level 3** | **Small Safe** (Storage for money/valuables) |
| **Level 4** | **Large Safe** (108 slots for money/high-value loot) |

## Gunny: The Weapon Specialist

Gunny remains one of the most important entries on the **gray zone warfare all traders list**. He provides the backbone of the Western arsenal, including the M4 platform which remains a top-tier choice for both PvE and PvP.

### Critical Level Unlocks
*   **Level 2:** Unlocks the base M4A1 and low-tier Mortar rounds. Mortars are essential for clearing out entrenched AI or suppressing enemy PMCs during COP captures.
*   **Level 3:** This is the "sweet spot" for Gunny. You unlock the DDM4, weapon repair kits, and most importantly, **M855 Ammunition**. This round is versatile enough to handle almost any threat in the game.
*   **Level 4:** Grants access to the Firearms Crate, allowing you to clean up your stash by storing multiple primary weapons in a single container.

## Banshee: The Close Quarters King

Banshee has seen a significant rise in popularity in 2026 due to the addition of the MP7 and MCX. While Gunny handles the mid-range meta, Banshee provides the tools for aggressive, close-quarters combat.

### Why Prioritize Banshee?
Banshee has fewer tasks than Handshake or Gunny, meaning you should focus on selling your high-value loot (watches, necklaces, and rare electronics) to him to bridge the reputation gap. At Level 4, he sells the **Razer Scope**, widely considered the clearest and most effective optic for long-range engagements. He also provides unlimited APSX 4.6x30mm rounds for the MP7, which can shred through Level 3+ body armor in seconds.

## Logistics and Storage: Turncoat & Handshake

Managing your inventory is a constant struggle. Turncoat and Handshake provide the containers and bags necessary to keep your operations running smoothly.

### Backpacks and Cases
Turncoat is the only vendor who sells the **Delta Backpack** at Level 4. With a 36-slot capacity, it is the largest bag in the game, making it mandatory for serious loot runs. Handshake, on the other hand, focuses on protection. His Level 4 RPC armor offers some of the highest protection-to-weight ratios available for purchase.

> **Warning:** High-tier armor like the RPC is expensive. Always ensure you have a steady stream of income from high-value loot before making it your standard kit.

## Leveling Strategy for 2026

To efficiently navigate the **gray zone warfare all traders list**, follow this prioritization strategy:

1.  **Do Missions First:** Tasks are the primary source of reputation. Always have an active task for the trader you are trying to level.
2.  **Targeted Selling:** Sell armor to Handshake, guns to Gunny, and high-value "junk" to Banshee or Artisan.
3.  **The Vulture Loop:** When farming high-tier areas like Midnight Sapphire, save "Military Equipment" items. These are the only things Vulture buys at a reasonable rate to increase his XP.
4.  **Buy to Level:** If you have the cash but lack the "Money Spent" requirement, buy expensive suppressors or optics and sell them back. You lose some money, but it’s the fastest way to hit the financial threshold for Level 4.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find high-value loot for traders?**

A: Areas like Midnight Sapphire, Fort Narith, and Tiger Bay contain the highest density of safes and rare item spawns. Bosses in these areas often carry items worth tens of thousands of dollars.

**Q: Can I access Vulture if another faction owns the COP?**

A: No. Your faction must actively control the Major COP for Vulture to appear in your menu. If the location is gray or red on your map, you must clear the area and capture the terminal first.

**Q: What is the benefit of the Large Safe from Vulture?**

A: The Large Safe provides 108 slots of storage specifically for money and high-value loot. This is significantly more efficient than using multiple wallets and helps keep your stash organized as you reach the endgame of the **gray zone warfare all traders list**.

**Q: Does reputation reset?**

A: Reputation only resets if you choose to wipe your character or if a major seasonal wipe occurs. In 2026, most major content updates include a progression reset to keep the economy balanced.
