## Mithras Earth Council: Starting Area Overview

When you deploy as a Mithras operative, your base of operations serves as the hub for all your tactical needs. Unlike other factions that start in different corners of the map, Mithras players focus their early efforts on the western side of the Lamang region. The primary starter town for this faction is Nam Thaven.

The layout of Nam Thaven is designed to teach you the basics of urban combat, verticality, and extraction. Most of your initial tasks will revolve around three main vendors. Understanding their requirements is the first step in your progression.

| Vendor | Primary Focus | Initial Task |

## Nam Thaven: The Gray Zone Warfare Mithras Map Guide to POIs

Nam Thaven is a complex environment filled with AI combatants and potential rival players. To navigate effectively, you must learn the key landmarks that serve as both task objectives and navigational anchors. Following a **gray zone warfare mithras map guide** ensures you don't wander aimlessly into high-threat zones.

### Key Points of Interest (POIs)

1.  **The Water Tower:** Located in the northern sector of town, this is the most visible landmark. It serves as the primary marker for Lab Rat's "Meds Wanted" task.
2.  **The Lumber Yard:** Situated in the southeast, this open area is filled with stacks of wood and small sheds. It is a high-traffic area for Gunny's "Cash Retrieval" task.
3.  **The Gas Station:** Found at the southern edge of town, this is a dangerous area often guarded by 3-4 AI enemies. It is a required stop for Handshake's "First Recon."
4.  **The Warehouse:** A large industrial building in the western part of town, north of the water tower.

## Essential Starter Task Walkthrough

To progress quickly in 2026, you should aim to complete multiple tasks in a single deployment. This **gray zone warfare mithras map guide** recommends a specific rotation to minimize travel time between Landing Zones (LZs).

### Phase 1: Recon and Retrieval
Start by flying into **LZ Charlie 2**. This puts you in close proximity to the northern entrance of Nam Thaven.

*   **Meds Wanted:** Head directly to the medical site under the Water Tower. Look for white tents with blue logos. Clear the AI, enter the building in the northwest corner, and grab the white medical box from the shelves.
*   **Cash Retrieval:** Move southeast to the Lumber Yard. Locate the small shed on the east side. Interact with the red-topped crate to place your tracker. 
*   **First Recon:** Visit the Convenience Store (north of the lumber yard), the Demolished Building (marked by a yellow excavator), and finally the Gas Station.

### Phase 2: The Crashed Little Bird
After completing the initial set, you will unlock **LZ Charlie 3**. This is located south of the gas station and is the primary extraction point for Mithras players.

*   **Little Bird Down:** Head slightly north of LZ Charlie 3 into the treeline. You will find a burnt-out helicopter. After "discovering" the crash site, head to the nearby house to the north. Clear the enemies and locate the bodies of the crew inside to finish the objective.
*   **Lost and Found:** Enter the construction shop north of the lumber yard. Inside, you will find the body of a clerk. You must retrieve the necklace lying between his hands.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you die while carrying a quest item (like the clerk's necklace or the medical box), it will despawn. Always place these items in your **Safe Lockbox** immediately upon pickup.

## Tactical Combat and Survival Tips

Consulting a **gray zone warfare mithras map guide** for coordinates is only half the battle; you must also survive the trek. In 2026, the AI has been tuned to be more reactive to sound and movement.

### Weapon Configuration
For the Mithras starter area, an M4 configured with an ACOG 4x scope is highly recommended. You can purchase the ACOG and the necessary mount from Gunny once you reach level 2. This allows you to pick off AI guards at the Gas Station and Water Tower from a safe distance. If you are low on funds, looting AKs and SKS rifles from downed enemies is a viable strategy, as 7.62mm ammo is abundant in Nam Thaven.

### Health and Stamina Management
Gray Zone Warfare uses a complex medical system. Always carry at least two bandages, a splint, and painkillers. 
*   **Blood Loss:** If you are bleeding, your blood levels (HP) will drop rapidly. Bandage immediately.
*   **Hydration:** Running in the tropical heat of Lamang drains your energy and hydration. Loot soda and food from the Convenience Store POI to stay topped off.
*   **Stealth:** Use the "C" key to crouch and control + scroll wheel to adjust your height. This is vital when sneaking through the Alleyways near the Town Hall.

## Advanced Map Navigation: Unlocking LZs

By walking to the fields south of the Gas Station, you trigger the unlock for Charlie 3. Having multiple LZs allows you to choose the safest extraction point if one is being "camped" by rival faction players or heavy AI presence.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the Mithras starter town in Gray Zone Warfare?**

A: The starter town for the Mithras Earth Council is Nam Thaven. It is located on the western side of the map and contains all the initial POIs for Handshake, Gunny, and Lab Rat tasks.

**Q: How do I find the KV Dock Key?**

A: The KV Dock Key is a random drop from AI enemies within Nam Thaven. You will need this key for the "Medical Detective" quest. Use this **gray zone warfare mithras map guide** to identify high-density AI areas like the Gas Station to farm for this drop.

**Q: What happens if I die during a task?**

A: If you die, you lose all gear on your person. However, your body remains on the map for a limited time. You can redeploy and run back to your corpse to retrieve your gear. Note that if you die a second time before reaching your first body, the original loot will despawn forever.

**Q: Can I change my faction to Mithras later?**

A: Currently, faction choice is permanent for the duration of the "wipe" or season. If you wish to join Mithras to use this map guide, you must select them at the initial character creation screen.
