## Essential Mission Walkthroughs for Nam Thaven

As you progress through the southern region, the tasks become more specific, requiring you to visit exact coordinates within the town. Two of the most common hurdles for Mithras players involve the "Tools of the Trade" and "Radio Silence" missions.

### Tools of the Trade
This mission is assigned by Artisan and requires you to retrieve a specific toolbox. For the Mithras faction, this objective is located near the construction site.

*   **Location:** Construction site at the corner of **170 117** and **170 118**.
*   **Execution:** Look for the large, unfinished building. To the right of this structure, there is a smaller house. Enter this house and check the room on the right. The briefcase containing the Artisan's ToolBox is located inside.

Handshake requires you to disrupt local communications by neutralizing specific electronic equipment.

*   **Location:** Coordinate **171 116**.

## Tactical Survival in South Mithras Territory

Surviving in the southern sector of Lamang Island as a Mithras operative requires more than just knowing where to go; you must know how to move. The AI in Nam Thaven often patrols in groups of three to five, and they are surprisingly accurate with iron sights.

1.  **Utilize High Ground:** The water tower and certain rooftops in the construction site offer excellent sightlines. Clearing an area from a distance before moving in for a task objective is always the safer play.
2.  **Suppressors are Mandatory:** To avoid pulling the entire town's population toward your position, use suppressed weapons whenever possible. This is especially important for tasks like **Rat's Nest**, where you must enter two separate hideouts.
3.  **Faction Identification:** Be aware that other Mithras players will be in the area. Check your map frequently to identify "blue" friendly markers. If you see an operator without a marker, they are likely from the Lamang or Crimson Shield factions and should be engaged with caution.

> **Warning:** The "First Hit" mission requires you to neutralize a criminal leader in Nam Thaven. He is usually protected by better-armored guards. Do not attempt this task with basic pistol gear; ensure you have at least a Class II vest and a rifle with a magnifying optic.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find the Doctor's Office Key for the Medical Detective task?**

A: In Nam Thaven, keys are most commonly found on the bodies of neutralized AI. Farming the enemies near the Town Hall or the local clinics is the most reliable way to find the Doctor's Office Key.

**Q: I can't find the briefcase for Native Negotiations. Where is it?**

A: Unlike most mission items, the briefcase for Native Negotiations must be picked up at your **Base Camp** first. Check the command tent or your messenger rewards before heading to the Nam Thaven Auto Shop.

**Q: Does this gray zone warfare south mithras guide apply to the other factions?**

A: No. While the mission names (like First Recon) are the same across factions, the locations are different. Mithras operates in the south (Nam Thaven), while Lamang is in the east (Pha Lang) and Crimson Shield is in the north (Kiu Vongsa).

**Q: What should I do if a mission item like the Thug's Smartphone doesn't spawn?**

A: If a quest item is missing, it may have been recently picked up by another player in your session. Try waiting a few minutes for the loot to respawn, or extract and join a different server instance to reset the world state.
