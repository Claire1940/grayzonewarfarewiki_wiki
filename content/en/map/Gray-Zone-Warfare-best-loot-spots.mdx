### Fort Narith: Military Grade Loot

Fort Narith is the island's largest military site, making it a prime target for military-grade weapons, armor, and attachments. It's a Tier 3 zone with LAF Soldiers as locals, indicating tough resistance.

| Loot Type           | Key Items                                | Risk Level | Notable Locations          |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------|------------|----------------------------|
| Weapons & Attachments | Assault Rifles, DMRs, high-tier optics   | High       | Armory, Barracks, Command Center |
| Armor & Gear        | Plate carriers, helmets, tactical rigs   | High       | Supply Depots, Admin Buildings |
| Valuables           | Intel documents, rare electronics        | Medium     | Offices, Secure Rooms      |

<Callout type="warning" title="High-Risk Zone">
Fort Narith is heavily guarded and frequently patrolled by both AI and other players. Ensure you are well-equipped and consider bringing a squad for these runs. Keys for Fort Narith are often tied to tasks and can unlock highly valuable rooms.
</Callout>

### Tiger Bay: Urban Warfare and Diverse Loot

Tiger Bay is a sprawling city zone offering a diverse range of loot, from military supplies to high-value civilian items. It's a Tier 3 zone with both Rebels and LAF Soldiers, making it unpredictable.

| Loot Type           | Key Items                                | Risk Level | Notable Locations          |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------|------------|----------------------------|
| Weapons & Ammo      | ARs, SMGs, various ammunition calibers   | High       | Central Armory, Police Station |
| Medical Supplies    | Advanced medkits, pain killers, stims    | Medium     | Clinics, UNLRA distribution points |
| Valuables           | Jewelry, electronics, quest items        | High       | Mall, Residential buildings, Offices |

### What to Prioritize When Looting

Not all loot is created equal. Focus on items that offer high value-to-slot ratio or are critical for tasks and vendor progression.

| Item Category   | Priority | Examples                                 | Notes                                        |
|-----------------|----------|------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Valuables**   | High     | Gold bars, jewelry, rare electronics     | Always pick up; high sell value to any vendor |
| **Firearms Boxes** | High    | CSI/UN/Military Firearms Boxes           | Can contain full weapons, magazines, parts   |
| **Task Items**  | High     | Quest intel, specific components         | Essential for progression; always secure     |
| **Medical Items** | Medium   | IFAKs, surgical kits, combat tourniquets | Replenish your stock; sell excess to Lab Rat |
| **Weapon Parts** | Medium   | High-tier scopes, suppressors, grips     | Can significantly improve weapon builds      |
| **Ammunition**  | Medium   | High-penetration rounds                  | Critical for combat; sell excess to Gunny    |
| **Armor**       | Low      | Damaged vests, common helmets            | Only if needed or high-tier; heavy and bulky |

<Callout type="success" title="Smart Selling">
Always sell valuables to the vendor who offers the highest price. While any vendor will buy them for 50% less, checking the game wiki for the "best buyer" ensures maximum return on your efforts.
</Callout>
