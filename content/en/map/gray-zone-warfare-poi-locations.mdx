## Starter Town POIs: Early Game Essentials

Every faction begins in a mirrored starting town located just outside their respective main bases. While these areas are designed for training, they contain some of the most consistent early-game loot in the game. If you are looking for a "zero-to-hero" run, these **gray zone warfare poi locations** should be your first stop.

### The Hideout and Market Area
The Hideout is a premier spot for obtaining the M700 sniper rifle. Located centrally within the town, this building houses a UN Firearms box and two equipment boxes. Statistics from recent deployments suggest a nearly 50% spawn rate for the M700 in the firearms box, making it a mandatory stop for marksmen.

The Market area features a central building where local bosses often spawn. To maximize loot here, clear the ground floor and take the rear stairs to the roof. By looping around the exterior walkway to the back of the building, you can access a hidden loot room containing a UN Firearms box, a medical crate, and loose weapon spawns on the floor.

| Location | Key Required | Primary Loot | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Hideout** | None | M700, UN Gear, Ammo | Low |
| **Market Rooftop** | None | Medical Supplies, Weapon Parts | Medium |
| **Water Tower** | UNL A Key | UN Firearms Box, Plate Vests | Low |
| **Lumber Yard** | Maintenance Key | Equipment Boxes, Industrial Loot | Medium |

> **💡 Tip:** Even if you don't need the M700, the gear from the Hideout equipment boxes sells for significant currency at vendors, providing an easy way to fund your early-game kits.

## Mid-Game Farming: Sawmill and Ban Pa

Once you move beyond the starter areas, the Sawmill and Ban Pa become the primary focus for mid-tier progression. These **gray zone warfare poi locations** offer a high density of weapon boxes without requiring an excessive number of keys.

### The Sawmill Layout
The Sawmill is an open-air POI that favors players with magnified optics. Because the enemies are spread out with ample cover between them, you can systematically clear the area to access five weapon and equipment boxes that do not require keys.

1.  **Office Building:** Located on the eastern side, this contains a firearms box and loose attachments.
2.  **North Warehouse:** Primarily a medical hub; check here if you are low on bandages or splints.
3.  **West Warehouse:** Contains a firearms box frequently spawning AK-series rifles and CQS Mosins.
4.  **Truck Spawns:** Two trucks located south of the warehouses contain an additional firearms box and an equipment box (ideal for plate vests).

### Ban Pa Coastal Loot
Ban Pa is a fishing village that provides excellent loot for those willing to brave its narrow corridors. The docks are the highlight here, featuring a room with both a UN Firearms box and an equipment box. If you manage to find the **Fishing Hut Key**, you can unlock a coastal shack that adds another set of high-tier boxes to your run.

## Tactical Entry: YBL-1 and the Hidden Bunker

YBL-1 is a notorious POI due to its cramped underground corridors and aggressive AI. However, savvy players utilize a hidden secondary entrance to bypass the main gate meat-grinder. 

To find this entrance, follow the dirt path on the North side of the POI wall. You will encounter a square hole in the ground that leads directly into the bunker system. 
*   **Entrance Loot:** A firearms box sits right at the bottom of the drop.
*   **Storage Room:** If you possess the **Storage Key**, you can open a room in this corridor for an additional equipment and firearms box.
*   **Tactical Advantage:** This path allows you to flank the main bunker area or extract quickly if the surface becomes too hot with enemy player activity.

## High-Tier Zones: Tiger Bay and Midnight Sapphire

As of the 2026 Spearhead update, the **gray zone warfare poi locations** in Tiger Bay and Midnight Sapphire have been overhauled to provide the highest-rated armor and weapons in the game. These areas are extremely dangerous and often require a coordinated squad.

### Tiger Bay Mall and Refugee Camp
The Mall is a massive multi-story complex. While the ground floor is filled with cash registers for currency farming, the upper floors are where the real gear resides.
*   **South Side Escalators:** Head up to find a firearms box that can contain fully kitted M4s and Mosins.
*   **Refugee Camp:** Located on the outskirts, look for a truck with a UN Firearms box in the trunk and an equipment chest nearby.
*   **The Peer Room:** Requires the **Peer Key**. This is arguably the best loot room in Tiger Bay, offering 3+ rated armor and M4s equipped with Voodoo sights.

### Midnight Sapphire Expansion
The luxury hotel at Midnight Sapphire is no longer just a backdrop. In 2026, the roof has been opened for exploration. It is "chalk-full" of secrets, including high-value loot and unique puzzles. Some areas now require specific sequences—such as turning on power in a specific order—to unlock reinforced armories.

## The 2026 Spearhead Update: New POIs and Mechanics

The recent Spearhead update introduced 25 new **gray zone warfare poi locations**, significantly expanding the playable area of Lamang. Beyond just new buildings, the way you interact with these locations has changed.

1.  **Map Integration:** Tasks are now physically marked on your map. You can click these icons to read objectives and track progress without diving into deep menus.
2.  **Regional Reputation:** The map is now divided into regions. You must earn enough reputation within a specific region to unlock the most lucrative quests and POI access codes.
3.  **Dynamic AI Patrols:** No longer are AI confined to the centers of POIs. You may encounter fireteams or even full platoons patrolling the roads between major locations.
4.  **Server-Specific Codes:** Some locked rooms no longer use physical keys. Instead, you must find codes scattered throughout the POI. These codes are server-specific, meaning you cannot simply look them up on a wiki; you must find the intel yourself during that session.

> **Warning:** The 2026 update added tripwires to many jungle paths surrounding major POIs. Listen for a distinct "click"—this indicates a pin has been pulled on a smoke, flare, or frag grenade.

## FAQ

**Q: Where are the best gray zone warfare poi locations for solo players?**

A: Solo players should focus on the **Sawmill** and **Ban Pa**. These areas offer significant loot (up to 5 boxes in Sawmill without keys) and have enough cover to allow for stealthy approaches against AI. Avoid the center of Tiger Bay or the YBL-1 main entrance unless you are heavily geared.

**Q: How do the new server-specific codes work at POIs?**

A: In the 2026 update, certain high-value rooms require a 4-digit code found on notes or computers within that same POI. These codes change every time a server restarts, encouraging players to actually explore the environment rather than just rushing to a door with a pre-known password.

**Q: Can I find high-tier scopes like the Voodoo in starter towns?**

A: While rare, it is possible to find mid-tier optics in the starter town's Market or Hideout. However, for guaranteed high-tier attachments like the Voodoo scope, you must venture into **Tiger Bay** or the **Midnight Sapphire** hotel roof.

**Q: What is the benefit of the new Combat Outposts?**

A: Combat Outposts act as mini-POIs where you can turn in quests and restock basic supplies without returning to your main faction base. Capturing these outposts from enemy factions is also a primary way to trigger organic PvP encounters in the 2026 warfare mode.
