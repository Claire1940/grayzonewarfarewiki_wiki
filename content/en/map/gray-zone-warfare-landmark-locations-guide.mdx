### Faction Home Base Proximity
Each faction (Mithras, Lamang, and Crimson) has a home base that can be raided for high-level equipment. To do this effectively, you must land at specific LZs and move toward the "raiding houses" located on the periphery of these bases.

*   **Mithras Raiders:** Land at **Jefferson** and move to the house on the right side of the base entrance.
*   **Lamang Raiders:** Land at **Vanguard** and target the house at the top right of the compound.
*   **Crimson Raiders:** Land at **Walker** and approach the house on the left.

## Navigating the YBL-1 Bunker and Underground Landmarks

The underground sections of Lamang are some of the most dangerous landmarks in the game. The **YBL-1 Bunker** is a multi-level complex that serves as a massive gear check for players. Unlike surface landmarks, the bunker limits your movement and forces close-quarters engagements where the AI's new "flinch" mechanics are most visible.

When exploring the bunker, players should look for the following sub-landmarks:
1.  **The Infirmary:** Located on the middle level, this is the best spot for finding high-end surgical kits and blood bags.
2.  **The Weapons Room:** Requires a specific key but contains some of the best weapon spawns in the region, including the new Alien and L43A1 firearms.
3.  **The Generator Room:** A dark, narrow corridor that often holds patrols. It is a prime location for setting up ambushes against other players.

> **⚠️ Warning:** In the 2026 update, dying during a main story task at a landmark like YBL-1 will reset your task progression. You will be forced to return to your main base to re-accept the contract, so always bring a medic.

## Survival Tips for Landmark Exploration

As this **gray zone warfare landmark locations guide** emphasizes, the environment is just as deadly as the enemy players. The 2026 weather and lighting overhaul means that landmarks can become nearly invisible during tropical storms.

*   **Use the New Sprint System:** Use regular sprint (Shift) to move between landmarks to preserve stamina. Save your tactical sprint (Double-tap Shift) for dashing between cover once you are inside a landmark's "aggro zone."
*   **Watch for Traps:** Many rebuilt landmarks now feature grenade traps and tripwires. You can interact with these to diffuse them, though they cannot currently be picked up and reused.
*   **AI Injury Cues:** Watch for AI flinching or limping. The 2026 update added realistic damage reactions, letting you know when a target is suppressed or severely wounded.
*   **Check the Map:** Tasks are now visible on your map, and you can accept new contracts while in the field. Use this to chain multiple landmark visits in a single deployment.

## FAQ

**Q: How many new landmark locations were added in the 2026 updates?**

A: The 0.4 Spearhead update added 25 new locations across the map and completely rebuilt several existing ones to improve tactical gameplay and visual fidelity.

**Q: Where can I find the best armor and weapons in this gray zone warfare landmark locations guide?**

**Q: Can I share task progress with my squad at these locations?**
