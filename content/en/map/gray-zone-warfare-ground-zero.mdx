  <Step num="2" title="Cache Retrieval (Gunny)">
    **Objectives:** Find and mark a cache in your town's lumberyard.
    **Known Locations:** Warehouse (140 163), Lumber Yard (141 161).
    **Rewards:** 400 EXP, +100 Gunny Rep, $1,700 USD, 1x Scorpion OCP Convoy Pack.
    **Why it matters:** An early weapon progression task that builds crucial Gunny reputation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Meds Wanted (Lab Rat)">
    **Objectives:** Locate UNLRA medical supplies near the town's water tower.
    **Known Locations:** Medical Supplies (140 161).

  </Step>

    **Objectives:** Retrieve and deliver intel from the restaurant attic to Handshake.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Essentials: Staying Alive on Lamang Island">
Gray Zone Warfare is an extraction shooter, meaning every raid carries the risk of losing your gear. Understanding and managing basic survival mechanics is critical, especially when starting out on Ground Zero. Pay close attention to your health, hydration, and energy levels to avoid preventable deaths.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Hydration & Energy">
    - **Thirsty:** Below 50% hydration, no blood regeneration.
    - **Dehydrated:** At 1% hydration, hazy vision.
    - **Hungry:** Below 50% energy, no blood regeneration.
    - **Starving:** At 1% energy, hazy vision.
    - **Always carry water and food.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Injury & Coma">
    - **Bleeding:** Light, medium, and severe bleeding cause continuous blood loss. Treat immediately.
    - **Coma:** Can occur if blood drops below 50% or from destroyed organs (lungs/liver).
    - **Revival Window:** Squadmates have ~180 seconds to revive a player in a coma.
    - **Medical items** like IFAKs and bandages are essential.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The secure lockbox is your best friend on Ground Zero. Any items placed inside it are protected from loss upon death. This is particularly important for quest items or high-value loot you cannot afford to lose.

<Callout type="warning" title="Secure Your Valuables">
Always place quest items and high-value loot you intend to keep into your secure lockbox. Losing mission-critical items can significantly impede your progression and force you to restart tasks.
</Callout>
