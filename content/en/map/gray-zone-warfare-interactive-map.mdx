## Navigating YBL-1: A Case Study in Map Utility

One of the most notorious locations on Lamang Island is the YBL-1 bunker. Without a **gray zone warfare interactive map**, players often find themselves trapped in "one-way" sections or missing vital intel.

For the "Mystery Machines" mission assigned by Lab Rat, precision is key. Follow these tactical steps to secure the intel:

1.  **Locate the Secret Entrance:** Instead of charging the main gate, use the interactive map to find the secret entrance located in the hills. This allows for a stealthier approach.
2.  **Descend to the Bottom Floor:** The objective is located on the lowest level. Be prepared for close-quarters combat as AI guards frequently patrol the stairwells.
3.  **Find the Communication Room:** Navigate to the Eastern side of the bottom floor. Inside a room filled with server racks and computers, look for a specific terminal with a desk lamp.
4.  **Extract the Floppy Disk:** Access the data on the computer to receive the floppy disk. This item must be placed in your "Safe Container" or back pocket to ensure it isn't lost upon death.

> **💡 Tip:** YBL-1 is a high-traffic area for rival PMCs. Always check the interactive map for nearby extraction points before entering the bunker to plan your exit strategy.
