## Maximizing Your Cache Runs

Finding a cache is only half the battle; successfully extracting its contents requires careful planning and execution. Here are some tips to maximize your cache runs in *Gray Zone Warfare*:

*   **Plan Your Route**: Before deploying, mark multiple cache locations on your map. Plan a route that minimizes exposure to high-threat areas or allows you to approach them cautiously.
*   **Stealth is Key**: Many caches are in quieter, less trafficked areas. Approach with caution, using cover and checking for enemy patrols or other players. A suppressed weapon can be invaluable.
*   **Inventory Management**: Buried caches can yield significant loot. Ensure you have enough space in your backpack and secure containers. Consider bringing a larger backpack if your primary goal is a dedicated cache run.
*   **Team Up**: Running with a squad provides extra eyes for spotting caches, cover during excavation, and increased firepower if you encounter resistance.
*   **Know Your Exfils**: Always have a clear extraction plan. Caches can be heavy, so consider the distance to your nearest exfil point.
*   **Listen for Sounds**: Pay attention to ambient sounds. Digging a cache often involves a distinct sound cue that could alert nearby players or AI.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize high-value items like rare weapons, attachments, and valuable trade goods. If space is limited, leave behind common provisions unless you desperately need them. Remember, survival and successful extraction are paramount.
