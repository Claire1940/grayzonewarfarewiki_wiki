## Troubleshooting Connection and Login Errors

If the official **gray zone warfare server status** indicates that everything is operational, but you are still unable to join a match, the issue likely resides with your local client or network configuration.

1.  **Verify Game Files:** Right-click Gray Zone Warfare in Steam > Properties > Installed Files > Verify Integrity of Game Files. This fixes corrupted data after a large update.
2.  **Clear Shader Cache:** Large updates like 0.4 often require a fresh shader compilation. If you experience "stuttering" that feels like lag, reset your cache in the graphics settings.
3.  **Check Regional Selection:** Sometimes specific regional servers (like US East or Europe West) may experience localized outages. Try switching to a neighboring region to see if the connection stabilizes.
4.  **Firewall Exceptions:** Ensure the game's executable is whitelisted in your Windows Firewall, especially after a major version change (e.g., moving from 0.3 to 0.4).

## FAQ

**Q: Why does the gray zone warfare server status say "Online" but I can't find a match?**

A: This usually occurs during "soft maintenance" where login servers are active, but individual world instances are being capped to prevent overcrowding. Try waiting in the queue for at least 5 minutes or restarting your client to refresh the instance list.

**Q: Do I lose my gear if the server crashes?**

A: In most cases, the game has a "rollback" protection system. If the server itself crashes, your character state usually reverts to the last successful save (often when you last left the base). However, if your individual connection drops while the server stays live, the game may treat it as a "disconnection," and you could lose items if you are in a combat zone.

**Q: How often is scheduled maintenance performed in 2026?**

A: Typically, MADFINGER Games schedules maintenance on Tuesday or Wednesday mornings (UTC). However, following major releases like the 0.4 Spearhead update, "rolling restarts" may occur daily to clear memory leaks and update AI behavior.

**Q: Can I check the status of specific regions like US West or Asia?**

A: The official Discord has a "Status" channel that often breaks down issues by region. If you see a surge of players from a specific region reporting "Lag Spikes," it is a clear indicator that the local cluster is under heavy load.
