## The 0.4 Update: A New Era of Stash Management

The 2026 update 0.4 has completely transformed how players interact with their inventory. Previously, players struggled with limited space, often forced to sell valuable gear just to make room for basic ammunition. The introduction of "Cases" solves this by allowing players to compress items into specialized containers. These cases are sold by various vendors and unlocked as you progress through your reputation ranks.

### Gunny’s Weapon and Part Storage

Gunny is your primary source for all things ballistic. His new inventory cases focus on organizing your armory, from individual magazines to entire weapon systems.

| Case Type | Vendor Rank | Capacity | Primary Use | Approximate Price |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Magazine Case** | Rank 2 | 36 Slots | Storing various rifle and pistol mags | $69,000 |
| **Weapon Parts Case** | Rank 2 | 42 Slots | Scopes, suppressors, and grips | $75,000 |
| **Hard Case (Small)** | Rank 3 | 60 Slots | Compact weapon storage | $77,000 |
| **Mortar Round Case** | Rank 3 | 5 Slots | Specialized explosive storage | $75,000 |

To maximize efficiency with Gunny's cases, remember that disassembling weapons is the key to saving space. Removing the pistol grip and magazine from a rifle often reduces its vertical profile to a single slot, allowing you to fit significantly more firearms into a single Large Weapon Case.

## Medical Utility with Lab Rat

Lab Rat’s new cases aren't just for your stash; many of them are designed for "in-raid" utility. These "Utility Cases" allow you to carry more medical supplies in your rig or pockets than would normally be possible.

| Case Type | Capacity | Slot Cost | Utility Feature |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hard Case First Aid** | 2 Slots | 1 Slot | Fits in pockets; allows hotkeying 2 items |
| **Mini First Aid Kit** | 3 Slots | 1 Slot | Triple the med density for your belt/rig |
| **QR First Aid Kit** | 6 Slots | 2 Slots | Ideal for field medics in secure containers |
| **EMT Pouch** | 9 Slots | 2 Slots | Maximum medical density for long deployments |
| **Injector Case** | 16 Slots | 1 Slot | Stores various combat stimulants (Stems) |

The **gray zone warfare discord** community frequently discusses the "Field Medic" meta, where one player carries an EMT Pouch in their secure container. This allows the squad to stay supplied with blood bags and surgery kits without risking expensive medical gear if they are ambushed.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Injector Case currently does not allow for "quick use" of stimulants from the hotkey menu. You must manually open the case to use the stems inside.

## Artisan’s General Utility and Lore Storage

Artisan provides the widest variety of cases, ranging from simple wallets to massive gear crates. She also introduces a unique "Admin Desk" for players interested in the game's deep lore.

### Key and Document Management
Finding keys for locked rooms in Fort Narith or the Blue Lagoon is essential for high-tier loot. Artisan’s cases ensure you never lose these valuable items.

*   **Leather Key Holder:** A basic 6-slot holder for early-game keys.
*   **Admin Pouch:** A 2-slot item that provides 6 slots of storage for a mix of keys, money, and documents.
*   **Combat Folder:** A 2x2 case (16 slots) that acts as a high-capacity loot container for secure storage.
*   **Large Key Case:** The ultimate endgame storage for those who have collected every key in Lamang.

### Gear and Stash Expansion
For your armor and rigs, Artisan offers the Trunk Case and the MM24 Case. These function similarly to "Thick Cases" in other tactical shooters, allowing you to store full kits (helmets, vests, and backpacks) in a condensed space.

| Case Type | Total Slots | Restrictions | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Trunk Case** | 63 | No Weapons | Storing spare armor and helmets |
| **MM24 Case** | 120 | No Weapons | Organizing full "ready-to-go" kits |
| **Cooler** | 20 | Food/Drink only | Managing hydration and nutrition |
| **Admin Desk** | 210 | Paperwork only | Storing lore items and newspapers |

The Admin Desk is particularly interesting for 2026. It provides a staggering 210 slots but only accepts "paperwork" items like propaganda flyers and newspapers. While it may seem niche, veteran players in the **gray zone warfare discord** speculate that these lore items will be required for high-level faction missions in future updates.

## Ammunition and Explosives with Turncoat

Turncoat handles the grittier side of logistics. His cases are straightforward but essential for players who prefer aggressive, high-explosive gameplay.

1.  **Metal Ammo Box:** A 20-slot starter case for loose rounds.
2.  **MM Ammo Box:** A much larger 42-slot box for bulk ammunition storage.
3.  **Grenade Case:** A specialized container for fragmentation grenades, smokes, and flashes. It keeps your stash from becoming cluttered with individual explosive icons.

## Advanced Inventory Tactics

To truly master the 0.4 update, you must use these cases synergistically. For example, you can place a loaded rig inside a backpack, and then place that backpack inside an MM24 Case. This "nesting" technique allows you to store a complete combat loadout in a fraction of the space.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I join the official Gray Zone Warfare Discord?**

A: You can find the link on the game's official website or through the social media links on the Steam store page. Once you join, make sure to read the rules and select your faction role to unlock the relevant channels.

**Q: Can I lose my cases if I die in a raid?**

A: If the case is in your stash or your "Secure Container" (Safe Lock, Shnook, etc.), it is safe. However, if you carry a case like an EMT Pouch or a Wallet in your standard rig or backpack and die, other players can loot it from your body.

**Q: Why can't I put my rifle in the Trunk Case?**

A: The Trunk Case and MM24 Case are specifically designed for "Gear" (armor, rigs, helmets, and backpacks). Weapons must be stored in the Hard Case or Large Weapon Case provided by Gunny.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many cases I can own?**

A: There is no hard limit other than the physical dimensions of your stash. As you upgrade your edition of the game or progress through the 2026 content, your stash size will increase, allowing for more cases.

**Q: Does the gray zone warfare discord offer technical support?**

A: Yes, there is a dedicated "Bug Reports" and "Technical Support" section where community managers and developers track ongoing issues and provide workarounds for common performance problems.
