Introduced in late 2024 and refined throughout 2025, the day/night cycle is now a core part of the meta. Players can now shoot out lights, disable generators, and plunge buildings into darkness to gain a tactical advantage.

### Combat Outposts (Winds of War)
Added in May 2025, Combat Outposts are contentious locations that factions can capture and hold. These outposts provide:
*   Forward operating bases closer to high-tier loot.
