## Quest Overview and Objectives

Before heading out into the field, it is vital to understand what Gunny expects from you. The "A Single Drop" mission is focused on reconnaissance and resource tracking. You aren't necessarily required to engage in heavy combat, though the presence of guards makes stealth or surgical strikes necessary.

The primary goal is to locate and "check" three specific fuel tankers. These are large, industrial-sized containers used for aircraft refueling. Two are clustered together, while one is isolated in a different section of the airfield.

| Objective | Location | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Locate Tanker 1** | Remote Hangar (South of Runway) | Approach and interact/check |
| **Locate Tanker 2** | Main Compound (North Section) | Approach and interact/check |
| **Locate Tanker 3** | Main Compound (Near Tanker 2) | Approach and interact/check |
| **Extract Safely** | Any viable LZ | Survival and return to Gunny |

Many players start with only limited LZs, such as Fox Drop 1. If you find yourself without direct access to the airfield's immediate vicinity, you will need to perform a "leapfrog" maneuver across the map. A common route involves moving from the southern Hotel LZs up toward the Golf LZs.

> **Warning:** The airfield is an open area with very little overhead cover. When moving between LZs and the tankers, stay low and utilize the tall grass or perimeter fences to avoid being spotted by long-range AI snipers.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Finding the Tankers

Once you have landed and secured your immediate surroundings, your first priority should be the isolated tanker. This prevents you from getting pinned down in the main compound before completing the first stage of the **gray zone warfare a single drop quest**.

### Finding Tanker #1: The Remote Hangar
The first tanker is located south of the main runway. Look for a structure labeled as the "Remote Hangar" on your tactical map. This area is slightly detached from the main cluster of buildings, making it a bit quieter but also more exposed. 

Follow these steps to secure the first objective:
1. Approach the airfield from the southern perimeter.
2. Locate the large hangar sitting away from the main terminal buildings.
3. The tanker is parked just outside or partially inside the hangar bay.
4. Move close enough to the vehicle until the quest notification updates on your UI.

### Finding Tankers #2 and #3: The Main Compound
The remaining two tankers are located within the main compound of the airfield. These are positioned much closer together, often appearing as small white marks or rectangular shapes on the map's overhead view.

1. Move north from the Remote Hangar toward the main cluster of buildings.
2. Be cautious of AI patrols near the terminal and the fuel depot area.
3. The two tankers are usually parked side-by-side or in close proximity within the fenced fuel storage area.
4. "Boop" or check both tankers to complete the tracking requirement.

## Tactical Advice for the Airfield

The airfield is one of the more treacherous locations in the early-to-mid game. Because the **gray zone warfare a single drop quest** requires you to visit two distinct areas of the facility, you are likely to encounter multiple waves of enemies.

### Recommended Loadout
Since the engagement distances at the airfield can vary from close-quarters inside hangars to long-range across the runway, a versatile weapon is required.

| Gear Category | Recommendation | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Weapon** | Assault Rifle (e.g., M4A1 or AK-74) | Balance of fire rate and range. |
| **Optic** | 1x/4x Variable Zoom | Essential for spotting AI in the distance. |
| **Armor** | Level III or higher | Protects against common AI ammunition. |
| **Medical** | Extra Tourniquets & Splints | High chance of limb damage in open fields. |

### Stealth vs. Aggression
While it is possible to "run and gun" through this quest, it is rarely the most efficient method. The AI in Gray Zone Warfare is known for its ability to track movement across open ground. 

*   **The Flanking Strategy:** Instead of running down the center of the runway, stick to the tree line on the eastern or western edges. 
*   **Suppressors:** Using a suppressed weapon is highly recommended for the **gray zone warfare a single drop quest**. It allows you to pick off guards near the tankers without alerting the entire compound.
*   **Time of Day:** If possible, attempt this mission during dawn or dusk. The shifting light can make it slightly harder for AI to spot you at extreme ranges, though you should be wary of your own visibility.

> **Tip:** If you are playing in a squad, have one player act as an overwatch sniper from the hills overlooking the airfield while the others move in to check the tankers.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to bring any special items like a tracker or a GPS for the gray zone warfare a single drop quest?**

A: No, you do not need to bring any specific tools. Your character simply needs to be in close physical proximity to the tankers for the objective to trigger. However, always ensure your tactical map is functioning so you can pinpoint the "Remote Hangar" and "Main Compound" locations.

**Q: Can I complete this quest in a single deployment?**

A: Yes, it is highly recommended to do all three tankers in one go. Start with the Remote Hangar to the south and then move north to the main compound where the final two tankers are located. This path typically aligns with the safest extraction routes.

**Q: What should I do if the tankers aren't registering as "checked"?**

A: Sometimes the interaction zone can be finicky. Try walking around the entire perimeter of the tanker or standing directly next to the driver's cabin. Ensure you see the UI notification pop up in the top corner of your screen before moving to the next location.

**Q: Is the airfield a PVP zone?**

A: In the standard "Warfare" mode, the airfield is a high-traffic area where you are very likely to encounter players from opposing factions. If you are struggling with the **gray zone warfare a single drop quest** due to player interference, consider attempting it during off-peak hours or joining a larger squad for protection.
