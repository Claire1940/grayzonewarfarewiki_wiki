<Section icon={Swords} title="PvE vs. PvP Hide and Seek Strategies">
While the core principles of hide and seek remain, specific strategies vary depending on whether you're facing AI (PvE) or other players (PvP).

### PvE Hide and Seek
AI enemies often follow predictable patrol patterns and have specific detection ranges.

- **Exploit AI Pathing**: Learn common patrol routes and avoid them. Use buildings or dense foliage to break line of sight.
- **Sound Traps**: AI can be lured by sound. Throw a grenade or fire an unsuppressed shot to draw them away from your objective.
- **Patience is Key**: AI will often investigate suspicious noises. Remain hidden and wait for them to clear the area before proceeding.

### PvP Hide and Seek
Player-controlled enemies are far less predictable and often employ similar stealth tactics.

- **Anticipate Player Movement**: Think like a PMC. Where would you go? What routes would you take? Use this to predict enemy positions.
- **Counter-Stealth**: Listen for subtle sounds like rustling bushes, distant footsteps, or quiet reloads. Use thermal scopes or NVGs to spot hidden players.
- **Ambush Points**: Utilize corners, choke points, and elevated positions to set up ambushes. Don't engage immediately; wait for the perfect moment.
- **Avoid Obvious Spots**: Highly trafficked areas or easily visible cover are often checked first by savvy players. Find less obvious, harder-to-spot locations.
