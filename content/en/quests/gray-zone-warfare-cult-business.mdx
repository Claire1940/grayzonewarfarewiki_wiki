<Section icon={Map} title="Phase 1: The Elder's House and Chemical Production">

The first objective in **gray zone warfare cult business** takes you to the Elder's House in Ban Pa. This building serves as the initial hub for the drug ring's operations. To complete this step, you must find a chemical production workbench located on the first floor. However, the room is locked and requires the **Ban Pa Elder's House Key**.

Finding this key is often the most time-consuming part of the task. It can spawn in multiple locations within the village, or you may find it on the corpses of local AI defenders.

| Key Spawn Point | Specific Location | Coordinates / Detail |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Warehouse Area** | Inside the main Ban Pa warehouse | Check shelves and toolboxes |
| **Stilt House Table** | Outside on a wooden table | Near the northern stilt houses |
| **AI Drop** | Looted from dead enemies | Low random chance |

Once you have the key, head to the Elder's House (often marked by its more prominent structure compared to the shacks). Go downstairs to the locked door. Unlock it, enter the room, and your task will update once you "report findings" by interacting with the chemical equipment.

<Callout type="warning" title="Key Persistence">
If you die after finding the key but before using it, you will lose the key unless it is in your **Safe Container**. Always move quest-critical keys to your secure slot immediately upon pickup.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Phase 2: Locating the Warehouse Intel">

After reporting the chemical production, Lab Rat will task you with finding intel left by the drug producers. This intel is locked inside a warehouse protected by a keypad. Unlike other tasks where codes are static, the code for **gray zone warfare cult business** is randomized for each server instance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Stilt House">
    Head to the northern section of Ban Pa where the houses are built on stilts. You are looking for a specific house that often contains the "Angry Message" document.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find the Angry Message">
    Inside the stilt house, look for a table with a rotten pear and an empty bottle. Next to them is a document titled **Angry Message**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Translate and Record the Code">
    Interact with the note and select "Translate." A four-digit code (e.g., 5644) will appear. Write this down or memorize it, as you will need it for the warehouse door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Infiltrate the Warehouse">
    Move back toward the warehouse area. Locate the door with the padlock. Enter the code you just found to unlock the door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Retrieve the Drug Operation Message">
    Inside the locked warehouse room, find the note titled **Drug Operation Message**. Reading this will update your objective.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stealth Approach">
The warehouse area is heavily patrolled. If you are playing solo, try to clear the AI from the outskirts using a suppressed weapon before attempting to enter the code. The animation for entering the code leaves you vulnerable for a few seconds.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Phase 3: The Shaman's Diary and Villager Intel">

The final piece of the puzzle involves discovering what happened to the villagers. You must find the **Shaman's Diary**, which contains the final narrative elements of the "cult" cover story. Interestingly, this item is located back near the stilt houses where you found the warehouse code.

The Shaman's Diary provides the context needed to understand Sada's motivations—specifically his attempt to evacuate followers while claiming "Phi Pa Mum’s revenge" was coming to the state. This intel is vital for Lab Rat's final report.

| Intel Item | Location | Interaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Shaman's Diary** | Stilt house (near code location) | Pick up / Put in Safe Container |
| **Scared Pirate's Diary** | Docks area | Optional lore (not required) |
| **Drug Operation Note** | Locked Warehouse | Required for Phase 2 |
