## Quest Overview and Requirements

The Harvest Moon quest is typically assigned by Handshake or Gunny, depending on your faction's current standing and progression path. The mission centers on gathering evidence from poppy fields scattered across the southern regions of the map. With the 0.4 update, the environment has become significantly more reactive, meaning you cannot simply sprint into these areas without a plan.

| Requirement | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Minimum Level** | Level 2-4 (Early Game Progression) |
| **Primary Objective** | Locate and read four specific documents/folders |
| **Primary Locations** | South of Lima 2, YBO2, Ghost 3, and Ground Zero |
| **Vendor** | Handshake / Gunny |
| **Recommended Gear** | M4A1 or AK-74 (Suppressed), Level III Armor |

> **💡 Tip:** Always ensure you have at least two surgical kits and splints. The 0.4 update introduced more realistic injury mechanics where leg wounds can severely hamper your extraction speed.

## Step-by-Step Guide to the GZW Harvest Moon Quest

To successfully complete the **gzw harvest moon quest**, you must visit four distinct locations. These areas are often guarded by local insurgents who, while not as heavily geared as the soldiers in Tiger Bay, are highly accurate and will use the new 0.4 foliage to flank your position.

### 1. The First Poppy Field (South of Lima 2)
Your first objective is located in a poppy field south of the Lima 2 landing zone, specifically near the YBO2 landmark. 
*   **Location:** Look for a small cluster of buildings on the edge of the field.
*   **The Document:** Enter the house on the left side of the path. You will find a paper sitting on a wooden table. 
*   **Action:** Approach the paper and press the interact key (default 'F') to "Read" the document.

### 2. The Middle Field Objective
The second document is often found in the central area of the larger poppy plantations. 
*   **Location:** Navigate to the center of the field where a makeshift workstation has been set up.
*   **The Document:** There is a paper resting on a table under a small canopy. 
*   **Action:** Ensure the immediate area is clear of AI before interacting, as this table offers very little cover.

### 3. The Southern Field (Near Ghost 3)
The third objective takes you further south, near the Ghost 3 area and the outskirts of Ground Zero.
*   **Location:** Locate the table positioned near the edge of the poppy rows.
*   **The Document:** Look for a distinct **yellow folder**. This is often harder to spot if the lighting is low or if there is heavy rain.
*   **Action:** Read the folder to update your quest progress.

### 4. The Hidden Field (South of Ground Zero)
The final objective is tucked away in a hidden field south of Ground Zero.
*   **Location:** Look for a small clearing containing several green supply crates.
*   **The Document:** The document is placed directly on top of a large green crate.
*   **Action:** Once you read this final document, your objective will shift to extraction.

## Tactical Considerations for 0.4 Spearhead

The **gzw harvest moon quest** has changed significantly with the 2026 Spearhead update. The developers at MADFINGER Games have overhauled the AI behavior and environmental interaction.

### AI Reactions and Combat
In previous versions, AI would often stand in the open. In 0.4, enemies at the poppy fields will now dive for cover in the bamboo biomes and attempt to ambush you while you are reading the documents. 
*   **Suppressors are Mandatory:** Using unsuppressed fire will draw every insurgent within 300 meters to your exact location.
*   **Visual Impacts:** When you hit an enemy, they now react based on the caliber and location. A limb shot will cause them to retreat or limp, giving you a window to secure the quest item.

### Performance and UI Improvements
The 0.4 update has introduced massive optimizations. Players on mid-range hardware are now seeing frame rates upwards of 160 FPS, which makes clearing the tight interiors of the farmhouses much smoother. Additionally, the quest area is now highlighted with a subtle yellow circle on your map, reducing the need to constantly alt-tab to external maps.

## Progression: Unlocking the Secret Vendor Vulture

Completing the **gzw harvest moon quest** is a prerequisite for the high-level progression that leads to **Vulture**, the scavenger vendor. Vulture is unique because he does not stay at your home base; instead, he rotates weekly between "Major COPs" (Command Posts) marked with three black dots on the map.

### Why You Need Vulture
Vulture sells gear scavenged from fallen PMCs. If a player dies and their gear is not recovered, Vulture eventually lists it in his shop. This allows lower-level players to purchase high-tier M14s or specialized armor before they have leveled up the standard vendors like Banshee.

### The Path to the Large Safe
The ultimate goal for many contractors is the **Large Safe**, which offers a staggering 108 slots of secure storage for money and high-value loot.

| Vulture Level | Unlockable Item | Storage Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Scavenged PMC Kits | Access to Tier 4 gear early |
| **Level 3** | Small Safe | Secure money storage |
| **Level 4** | **Large Safe** | 108-slot high-value container |

To level up Vulture, you must sell him "Military Equipment" found in high-tier zones like Midnight Sapphire. However, you cannot interact with his shop unless your faction has captured the COP where he is currently located (the computer terminal must be yellow).

## Recommended Loadout for Harvest Moon

Since the poppy fields are open areas with scattered buildings, a versatile mid-range build is best.

1.  **Primary Weapon:** M4A1 with a 1-4x variable optic. This allows you to clear the fields from a distance and still be effective inside the small houses.
2.  **Ammunition:** Use M855 or better. The 0.4 update improved armor values for certain AI leaders, and basic FMJ rounds may struggle to penetrate.
3.  **Armor:** Level IIIA or Level III plates. You want to remain mobile enough to move through the poppy rows quickly.
4.  **Tactical:** Smoke grenades are surprisingly effective in 0.4 for masking your interaction with the quest documents if you are under fire.

> **Warning:** Do not linger at the poppy fields after securing the documents. The sound of combat often attracts "Third-Party" players in PvP servers who are looking to catch questing contractors off-guard.

## FAQ

**Q: I found the paper for the GZW Harvest Moon quest but I can't interact with it. Why?**

A: Ensure you have the quest active in your task menu. If it's active and still not working, try clearing any nearby AI, as being in an active "combat state" can sometimes delay interaction prompts in the 0.4 build.

**Q: Do I need to survive and extract to complete the Harvest Moon quest?**

A: Yes. While reading the documents updates your sub-objectives, the quest is only officially "completed" once you return to base and hand it in to the vendor. If you die before extracting, you may need to revisit the locations to re-verify the intel.

**Q: Where is Vulture located this week?**

A: Vulture rotates between Major COPs like Harrison, Titan, and Westmore. Check your map for the three-dot icons. If your faction owns the COP (yellow icon), Vulture will be accessible in the main building.

**Q: Can I complete the GZW Harvest Moon quest in a squad?**

A: Absolutely. Squad play is highly recommended for this mission, as one player can provide overwatch while the other interacts with the documents on the exposed tables. Progress is shared as long as all squad members are within the quest radius.
