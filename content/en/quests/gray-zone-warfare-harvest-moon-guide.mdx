Navigating the dense jungles and contested territories of Lamang Island requires a keen eye for detail and a steady trigger finger. One of the more complex reconnaissance missions you will encounter is the **Harvest Moon** task, issued by the vendor Banshee. This mission sends players deep into the outskirts of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield and surrounding regions to investigate illicit operations. Using this **gray zone warfare harvest moon guide**, you will be able to pinpoint the exact coordinates of four hidden poppy fields and recover the necessary intelligence to satisfy Banshee's requirements.

Whether you are playing as Mithras, Crimson Shield, or Lamang Recovery Initiative, the **gray zone warfare harvest moon guide** remains a vital resource for navigating these high-risk areas. The task is not merely about visiting a location; it involves finding specific documents—ranging from labor requests to oversight agreements—often guarded by aggressive AI units. Proper preparation is key, as these fields are spread across a wide geographical area, requiring significant travel time and tactical awareness.

## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out, it is important to understand the scope of the Harvest Moon mission. Banshee is interested in the logistics behind the local poppy production. You are tasked with finding four distinct "Hidden Poppy Fields" and interacting with a specific piece of intel at each site. 

| Objective | Location | Intel Item |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Field 1** | 192, 154 (East of Airfield) | Labor Allocation Request |
| **Investigate Field 2** | 178, 152 (South of Falcon) | Weekly Shipment Note |
| **Investigate Field 3** | 172, 132 (South of Ghost 3) | Protective Oversight Agreement |
| **Investigate Field 4** | 149, 117 (South of Lima 2) | Harvest Discipline Notice |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Several of these locations are in close proximity to Ground Zero and high-tier loot zones. Expect to encounter well-armed AI and potential enemy players from opposing factions.

## Location 1: The Eastern Field (192, 154)

The first stop in our **gray zone warfare harvest moon guide** takes us to the eastern side of the F.A.L.N.G. Airfield. This location is situated roughly southeast of the Nomad landing zone. The poppy field is tucked away from the main runway, making it a prime spot for clandestine operations.

Upon arriving at coordinates **192, 154**, look for a small cluster of structures used for processing the harvest. You are looking for a document titled the **Labor Allocation Request**. This paper is typically found resting on a table within the central area of the field. Simply approach the document and interact with it (default key 'F') to "read" it. This will trigger the task update for your first objective.

## Location 2: The Northern Field (178, 152)

Moving west from the first location, the second poppy field is located just south of the main Falcon area. This site is often marked by distinctive visual cues, such as nearby balloon crash sites or specific agricultural equipment.

At coordinates **178, 152**, you will find an open building or a covered shed. Inside this structure, search for the **Weekly Shipment Note**. This document details the frequency of transport for the harvested materials. Reading this note is essential for the second part of the Harvest Moon quest. Unlike some other tasks, you do not need to extract with these items; simply reading them in-situ is sufficient to progress.

## Location 3: The Ghost 3 Field (172, 132)

The third location is perhaps the most dangerous part of the **gray zone warfare harvest moon guide**. Located at **172, 132**, this field sits south of the Ghost 3 landmark and is in the general vicinity of Ground Zero. Due to its proximity to high-value areas, the AI presence here is significantly higher than at the first two locations.

When approaching this field, move cautiously. It is common to encounter between seven and ten AI guards patrolling the perimeter. Once the area is clear, look for a green or yellow folder sitting on a crate or a makeshift desk. This is the **Protective Oversight Agreement**. Interact with it to translate and read the contents. This document links the poppy production to higher-level corruption on the island, which is exactly what Banshee is looking for.

## Location 4: The Southern Field (149, 117)

The final objective takes you to the far southern reaches of the map, specifically south of the Lima 2 landing zone and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. This trek can be long, so ensure you have enough hydration and stamina supplies before making the journey.

At coordinates **149, 117**, you will find a small residential-style house adjacent to the poppy plants. Instead of searching the fields or outbuildings, head directly to the front porch of this house. At the end of the porch, you will find a book or ledger titled the **Harvest Discipline Notice**. Reading this final piece of evidence completes the investigation phase of the mission.

## Tactical Recommendations for Success

Completing the Harvest Moon task requires more than just knowing where to go. The environmental hazards and enemy density mean you should gear up appropriately.

| Gear Category | Recommended Item | Reason |

| **Utility** | 4x Water / 2x MRE | The distance between fields 2 and 4 is substantial. |

### Movement Strategy
It is often more efficient to tackle these locations in a specific order based on your faction's closest landing zones. If you are operating from the northern bases, start at 192, 154 and work your way south. If you are coming from the south, reversing the order can save time on extraction. Always check the map for active "hot zones" where other players might be engaging in PvP, as these poppy fields are popular transit points for players moving toward the airfield.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find a locked room at the poppy field locations, it may contain high-tier loot or weapon parts. While not required for the Harvest Moon task, these rooms are worth checking if you have the corresponding keys.

## Finalizing the Task

Once all four documents have been read, your task list should reflect 4/4 objectives completed. You do not need to collect any physical items, which simplifies the extraction process. Simply head to the nearest Landing Zone (LZ) and call for a transport back to Base Camp.

Upon returning, speak with **Banshee** to hand in the mission. The rewards for Harvest Moon typically include a significant amount of experience points (XP), faction reputation, and a payout of in-game currency. This reputation is crucial for unlocking better gear and higher-tier weaponry from Banshee's shop in later stages of the game.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to find all four poppy fields in a single raid?**

A: No, you can investigate these locations across multiple raids. Your progress is saved each time you interact with one of the documents. However, doing them in one go is more efficient if you have the supplies.

**Q: What should I do if the document is not appearing at the coordinates?**

A: Occasionally, documents may have slight rendering issues or may have been moved slightly in a recent patch. Ensure you are at the exact coordinates listed in this **gray zone warfare harvest moon guide** and check nearby tables, crates, or porches within a 10-meter radius.

**Q: Is there a specific faction requirement for the Harvest Moon task?**

A: While the task is given by Banshee to all players regardless of faction, your starting point on the map will dictate your optimal route. The locations of the poppy fields remain the same for everyone.

**Q: Are the AI guards at the third location (172, 132) armored?**

A: Yes, the AI near the Ghost 3 area and Ground Zero often wear body armor and carry more advanced weaponry compared to the scavengers found in the starter towns. Aim for headshots or use armor-piercing (AP) ammunition for the best results.
