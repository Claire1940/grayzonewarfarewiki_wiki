<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

To complete the **Gray Zone Warfare street spotter** objective safely, you should execute a disciplined approach. Do not run straight down the middle of the main avenue, as the spotter's tower and adjacent balconies offer the AI superior height advantages.

Follow these steps to insert, secure the intel, and exfiltrate without losing your tactical gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gear Preparation">
    Equip Class 4 or higher body armor and a helmet with face protection. Pack at least three IFAKs, four Splints, and multiple Tourniquets. Set up an M4A1 or AK-15 weapon platform with armor-piercing rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Insertion at Juliet 1">
    Call in a transport helicopter to Juliet 1 LZ. Avoid Juliet 2, as it frequently places you in direct combat with no immediate cover. Upon landing, check your perimeter and move east along the treeline toward the city outskirts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Urban Approach">
    Move slowly through the western alleys. Use the buildings as defilade against snipers positioned on the rooftops. Clear each structure room-by-room to prevent rebels from flanking your squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the Lookout">
    Approach the intersection at grid 198 130. Look for a multi-story concrete building with a wooden scaffolding structure attached to the side. Climb the stairs to the top balcony to locate the spotter's spotting logbook on the table.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Secure Exfiltration">
    Place the intelligence notebook inside your Secure Lockbox immediately. Fall back using the southern marina route to reach the exfiltration point. Call the helicopter once the landing zone is cleared of active threats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Secure Lockbox Safety">
Always keep quest items in your Secure Lockbox. Even if you fall in battle during the exfiltration phase, the intelligence will remain protected, allowing you to hand in the task back at the FOB without repeating the raid.
</Callout>

</Section>
