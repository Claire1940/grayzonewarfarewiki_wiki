<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Ban Pa Navigation">

Ban Pa is situated in the eastern region of Lamang Island, just north of the Tiger Bay shopping center and marina. Approach vectors vary based on your selected faction, but all operators will utilize Landing Zone (LZ) India as their primary insertion point. The village features dense foliage, wooden stilt houses, and muddy pathways that restrict movement and line of sight.

The document containing the information is located inside the village schoolhouse, a distinct wooden building on the eastern edge of the town. The schoolhouse can be identified by the desks and benches scattered around its perimeter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Insert at LZ India">
    Call in a transport helicopter to LZ India. Upon arrival, take advantage of the 4-second invulnerability window to scan the immediate tree line. Move north toward the southern edge of Ban Pa, staying low in the high grass to avoid alerting patrols.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Eastern Schoolhouse">
    Avoid the central village pathways where rebels gather in groups. Flank around the eastern perimeter of the village near the shoreline. Follow the coordinate indicators on your tactical map until you reach grid 205 137.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the School Perimeter">
    Rebel AI frequently patrols the immediate vicinity of the schoolhouse. Clear the area using a suppressed weapon to prevent drawing reinforcements from the nearby Elder's House. Check corners and dark interior spots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Document">
    Enter the schoolhouse and locate the wooden desk in the main classroom area. The intelligence folder lies directly on top of the desk. Pick it up and immediately place it inside your Secure Lockbox to ensure it remains protected.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract via LZ India">
    Retrace your steps back to the southern tree line. Stay alert for respawning rebels along the coast. Call for a transport helicopter at LZ India, board the aircraft, and fly back to the FOB to hand the document over to Gunny.
  </Step>
</Steps>
