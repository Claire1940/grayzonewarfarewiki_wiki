<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

To successfully complete the **gray zone warfare interrupted aid** mission, follow these precise steps. Ensure your weapon is loaded with at least Tier 2 ammunition, as the AI in Ban Pa can occasionally wear light body armor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Mission">
    Speak to Lab Rat at your faction's Base of Operations (Base). Accept the "Interrupted Aid" task to mark the objective on your map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy to Ban Pa">
    Request a transport to the nearest LZ to Ban Pa. If you are coming from the north, LZ India 1 or 2 are typically your best bets. Move cautiously toward the coordinates **201 146**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Bridge">
    Navigate to the northern outskirts of Ban Pa. You are looking for a bridge that crosses a small waterway. Look for a large road sign and a pile of tires on the side of the road.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Find the PMC Body">
    Search the immediate vicinity of the sign. You will find a deceased PMC squad member leaning against the signpost. This is the "squad" Lab Rat was referring to.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Report Findings">
    Approach the body and hold the interaction key (default 'F') to "Report Findings." You should see a notification that the objective is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Exfiltrate and Turn In">
    Head to the nearest extraction point. Once back at base, talk to Lab Rat to receive your rewards and reputation boost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mission Complete">
After reporting the findings, your task status will update to "Survive and Leave the Area." Don't get greedy; if the AI presence is too heavy, extract immediately.
</Callout>

</Section>
