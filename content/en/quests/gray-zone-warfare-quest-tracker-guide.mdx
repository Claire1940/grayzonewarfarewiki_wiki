## Getting Started: How to Use the Quest Tracker

Before heading into the field, you must officially accept tasks from the vendors at your Base Camp. Unlike traditional RPGs, quests are not automatically tracked on your screen. You must manually check your progress in the menu.

1.  Press **Tab** to open your character menu.
2.  Navigate to the **Vendors** tab at the top.
3.  Select a vendor (e.g., Handshake) and click the **Tasks** button located next to the "Trade" button.
4.  Accept all available missions to maximize your efficiency per deployment.

| Vendor | Primary Focus | Key Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Handshake** | Tactical Recon & Security | Weapons, Attachments, PMC Gear |
| **Gunny** | Logistics & Supply | Ammunition, Magazines, Scopes |
| **Lab Rat** | Medical & Research | First Aid Kits, Splints, Surgery Kits |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check your messages after accepting a task from Gunny. He often sends "Trackers" via the in-game mail system that you must retrieve and place in your inventory (ideally your Lockbox) to complete specific objectives.

## Phase 1: The Initial Town Sweep

Your first deployment should focus on three primary tasks that take place in the starter town closest to your faction's base. To save time, call in a transport to the **Charlie 2 LZ**. This puts you in a prime position to hit the Water Tower, the Warehouse, and the Lumberyard in a single circuit.

### Meds Wanted (Lab Rat)
Head to the large Water Tower, which is visible from almost anywhere in the town. Directly beneath it, you will find a medical site characterized by white tents and blue logos. 
*   **Location:** Building in the Northwest area of the medical site.
*   **Objective:** Find the white medical box on the shelves in the back room.

### Cash Retrieval (Gunny)
This task requires you to place trackers on two specific crates. If you die before placing them, you must return to base to retrieve new ones from your messages.
*   **Lumberyard:** Locate the small shed on the east side of the yard. Interact with the red crate inside.
*   **Warehouse:** Move north of the Water Tower. Find the small exterior room on the warehouse building and place the tracker on the red-topped crate.

### First Recon (Handshake)
This is a simple "visit and verify" mission. You do not need to interact with items, only enter the vicinity of these three landmarks:

| Objective | Map Location | Visual Cues |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Convenience Store** | North of Lumberyard | Large storefront with glass windows |
| **Demolished Building** | North edge of town | Construction site with a yellow excavator |
| **Gas Station** | Southeast corner of town | Standard fueling station; usually guarded by 3 AI |

## Phase 2: Advanced Early Tasks & Extraction

Once you have completed the first batch, you will likely unlock the **Charlie 3 LZ** by exploring the fields south of the Gas Station. This LZ is vital for the next set of missions. After extracting and handing in your finished tasks, pick up the following:

### Little Bird Down (Handshake)
Fly into Charlie 3. The crashed helicopter is located in the tree line just north of the LZ. 
1.  Approach the burnt-out chopper to tick off the first objective.
2.  Move to the nearby house to the north. 
3.  Clear the AI and find the bodies of the crew inside to complete the quest.

### Lost and Found (Lab Rat)
This task sends you to the local construction shop, located just north of the Lumberyard. 
*   **Objective:** Find the body of the clerk on the floor.
*   **Critical Item:** Pick up the **necklace** lying between the clerk's hands. 
*   **Safety Tip:** Place the necklace in your **Lockbox** immediately. If you die with it in your pockets, it will despawn.

### No Man Left Behind (Gunny)
Head to the backyards near the Town Hall on the east side of town. You are looking for a specific body tucked away in the alleyways between sheds. Place your tracker on the body to finish the task.

## Combat Preparation: Optimizing Your Loadout

The AI in Gray Zone Warfare can be surprisingly lethal at a distance. Before heading out for the "Restoring Order" task (which requires 10 kills in the town area), visit Gunny to upgrade your M4.

*   **ACOG Mount:** Purchase the mount and the 4x ACOG scope.
*   **Installation:** Inspect your M4, remove the default iron sights or hollow site, and drag the ACOG onto the top rail. 
*   **Benefit:** This allows you to engage enemies from 100+ meters, keeping you safe from the "aim-bot" tendencies of close-quarters AI.

> **Warning:** Be wary of the **Medical Detective** quest. It requires a specific **KV Dock Key**. These keys are random drops from AI enemies in the town. If you find one, prioritize your survival and extract immediately to secure it.

## Tactical Extraction Tips

Extraction is the most dangerous part of any mission. When using this **gray zone warfare quest tracker guide**, remember that other players (from rival factions) are likely hunting near the same LZs. 

1.  **Wait for the Bird:** When you call a helicopter, stay in cover at least 50 meters away from the LZ until the bird is touching down.
2.  **Check the Map:** If an LZ icon is pulsing red, it means enemies (AI or Players) are currently in the immediate vicinity.
3.  **Conserve Stamina:** Never run all the way to the LZ. Keep at least half your stamina bar full so you can sprint to the helicopter if you come under fire.
4.  **Vendor Reputation:** Always prioritize handing in tasks over looting random gear. Higher vendor levels allow you to buy better gear than you can find in the starter zones.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find the trackers for Gunny's quests?**

A: Trackers are sent to your "Messages" tab in the vendor menu. You must click on the message from Gunny, "Accept" the items, and then move them from your base storage into your character's inventory or lockbox. This is a common point of confusion in many **gray zone warfare quest tracker guide** searches.

**Q: Do I lose quest items like the Clerk's Necklace if I die?**

A: Yes. If a quest item is in your backpack or rig and you die, it remains on your corpse. If you cannot retrieve your body, the item is lost, and you must restart the quest. Always put small quest items in your **Lockbox** (the secure container) to prevent this.

**Q: Can I complete tasks with friends?**

A: Absolutely. Most "discovery" tasks (like First Recon) will count for everyone in the squad if they are nearby. However, items that need to be picked up (like the Med Box) are usually individual, meaning each player needs to grab their own instance of the item or wait for it to respawn.

**Q: How do I unlock more Landing Zones?**

A: LZs are unlocked by physically visiting the area. Many quests are designed to lead you toward new LZs. If you are struggling to reach a far-off objective, try exploring the perimeter of the map to trigger new flight paths for your transport helicopters.
