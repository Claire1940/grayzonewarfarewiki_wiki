## Mission Overview: Livestock GZW

Before heading out into the field, it is vital to understand the parameters of the mission. Banshee is interested in specific intelligence regarding the treatment and transport of assets in the region, masked under the guise of agricultural data. The mission takes place in a localized area where enemy presence is moderate to high, depending on your faction's current influence in the sector.

| Objective | Location | Key Interaction |

> **Warning:** The area surrounding the cages is often patrolled by armored AI. Do not approach the cages without first clearing the perimeter, as the open terrain provides little cover.

## Step 1: Scouting the Southwest Cages

The first phase of the **livestock gzw** mission requires you to move to the southwest portion of the marked quest area. Upon arrival, you will notice a series of cages. These are your primary navigation markers. You do not need to interact with the cages themselves, but you must get close enough for the quest log to update.

The terrain here is notoriously difficult to defend. The cages are situated in a relatively low-lying area, making you vulnerable to snipers positioned in the treeline or nearby structures. It is recommended to approach from the west, using the natural foliage as concealment. Once the objective "Investigate the Cages" triggers as complete, do not linger. Immediately begin your rotation toward the northern part of the village.

## Step 2: Locating the Key Code

The office containing the intel is electronically locked, and unlike other doors in Gray Zone Warfare, this one cannot be breached with a standard kick or a lockpick kit. You must find the specific key code hidden in a nearby residence.

Head north from the cages toward the cluster of residential buildings. You are looking for a specific house that appears slightly more intact than the surrounding ruins. Inside this house, navigate to the living room area. You will find a television set; resting near it is a handwritten note. This note contains the randomized key code required for the office.

## Step 3: Entering the Office and Extracting Intel

With the key code in your possession, you must double back toward the southwest. The office building is located in close proximity to the cages you visited earlier. This backtrack is the most dangerous part of the **livestock gzw** mission, as AI combatants may have respawned or moved into the area during your trek to the northern house.

Approach the office door and interact with the keypad. Once the door is unlocked, enter quickly and check your corners. The intel is typically found on a desk or inside a filing cabinet within the main room. As soon as you "Pick Up Intel," it will occupy a slot in your inventory. Ensure you have at least a 1x1 space available in your secure container if you want to guarantee the item stays with you even if you are KIA during extraction.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing in a squad, only one person needs to pick up the intel to share the progress, but it is safer to have the person with the most robust armor carry the physical item.

## Tactical Considerations for Lamang Island

The environment in Gray Zone Warfare is persistent and unforgiving. When completing the **livestock gzw** mission, keep the following tactical advice in mind:

1.  **Sound Discipline**: The northern house area has wooden floors that amplify footstep noise. Crouch-walk when searching for the note to avoid alerting nearby patrols.
2.  **Extraction Routing**: Do not use the nearest LZ (Landing Zone) if it has been active recently. The smoke from arriving helicopters attracts both AI and rival PMCs. Travel at least 200 meters away from the objective site before calling for transport.
3.  **Time of Day**: If possible, attempt this mission during the early morning or late evening in-game cycles. The lighting makes it harder for AI to spot you at a distance, though you will need a reliable flashlight or NVGs.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the note for the livestock gzw mission?**

A: The note is located in the northern residential area of the quest zone. Look for a house with a television inside; the note with the key code is sitting right next to the TV set on a small table or stand.

**Q: Can I use a lockpick to get into the office instead of finding the code?**

A: No, the office for the **livestock gzw** task requires a specific electronic key code found on the note. Standard lockpicking mechanics do not apply to this specific quest door in Update 0.4.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the intel?**

A: If the intel is in your standard backpack or rig, you will lose it upon death and must restart the mission from the office phase. However, if you place the intel in your secure container (Gamma/Alpha container), it will persist through death, allowing you to turn it in even if you don't make it to the extraction helicopter.

**Q: Do I need to visit the cages every time I attempt the mission?**

A: No, once the "Investigate the Cages" sub-objective is marked as complete in your journal, it stays completed even if you die. You can head straight for the key code or the office on subsequent attempts.
