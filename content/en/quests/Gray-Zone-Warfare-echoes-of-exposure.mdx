<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Echoes of Exposure">

Completing the **Gray Zone Warfare echoes of exposure** task requires a specific sequence of actions. You cannot simply run to the marker; you must interact with environmental triggers to "expose" the data required by your vendor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Infiltrate Ground Zero">
    Deploy to the nearest LZ (Callsign: HOTEL or DELTA depending on your faction). Move cautiously toward the central crater. Use the topographic map lines to stay in low-visibility trenches to avoid sniper AI on the ridges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Research Terminal">
    Navigate to the underground bunker entrance located at **Grid 143:122**. This is the same bunker system used in the "YBL-1" missions but requires deeper access to the restricted "Yellow Zone."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve the Encrypted Drive">
    Once inside the laboratory wing, look for the terminal with the yellow hazard symbol. You will need to hold the interaction key for 15 seconds to download the data. This will trigger an AI alarm, so ensure your squad is covering the entrances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exfiltrate and Deliver">
    Once the "Task Item: Encrypted Drive" is in your inventory, move it to your **Secure Lockbox**. Even if you die during extraction, the item will remain safe. Return to the FOB and speak to Handshake to complete the mission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mission Critical">
The download process is loud. If you are playing solo, consider clearing the entire floor before starting the interaction. Use grenades to clear the corners of the server room.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Tactics for Solo and Squads">

The **Gray Zone Warfare echoes of exposure** task changes significantly depending on your group size. Solo players must rely on stealth and suppressed weapons, while squads can utilize overwhelming fire support and tactical rotations.

**Solo Strategy:**
- **Patience is Key**: Wait for AI patrols to move away from the bunker entrance. Use a suppressor on every shot.
- **Night Ops**: Consider attempting the task at night using NVGs. AI detection range is slightly reduced, allowing you to slip into the bunker with less resistance.

**Squad Strategy:**
- **Bounding Overwatch**: Have one sniper stay on the ridge overlooking the bunker entrance while two operators move inside.
- **Distraction**: One player can fire unsuppressed shots near a different POI to draw AI away from the main objective path.
