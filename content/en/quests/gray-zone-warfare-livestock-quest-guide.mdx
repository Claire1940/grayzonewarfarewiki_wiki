## Livestock Quest Overview

The Livestock task is introduced as part of the expanded lore and vendor progression in the 0.4 update. It primarily takes place around the industrial and repair sectors of the map, often bringing players into contact with high-level AI and rival PMCs. The mission is divided into three core phases: scouting the repair shop perimeter, finding the Car Office Code, and retrieving the mission-critical documents.

| Objective Phase | Primary Task | Key Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1: Scouting** | Locate the wooden shack | Southwest of the Car Repair Shop |
| **Phase 2: Intelligence** | Find the Car Office Code | North of the mission circle |
| **Phase 3: Recovery** | Retrieve the Documents | Padlocked building (Back entrance) |

> **💡 Tip:** Always clear the immediate perimeter of the Car Repair Shop before attempting to interact with the quest items. The AI in this sector is known for aggressive flanking maneuvers.

## Step 1: Locating the Wooden Shack

The first step in your journey involves finding a specific wooden shack. This structure serves as the initial "trigger" for the quest progression. You can find this building situated at the bottom-left (southwest) corner of the Car Repair Shop complex. 

When approaching this area, stay low and use the foliage for cover. The shack is relatively small and can be easily missed if you are moving too quickly. Once you reach the shack, your task list should update, signaling that the first objective is complete. You do not need to find an item here; simply being in the vicinity is enough to satisfy the requirements.

## Step 2: Finding the Car Office Code

After scouting the shack, your next goal is to acquire the access code needed to enter the secure document room. This code is not found within the repair shop itself but is located slightly to the north of the designated mission circle on your map.

Navigate to the northern edge of the quest area and look for a small desk or terminal. The code is typically static for most players in the current 0.4 build. Based on field reports, the **Car Office Code is 2896**. However, you must physically interact with the intelligence at this location for the game to register that your character "knows" the code.

| Item | Location Description | Interaction Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Car Office Code** | Northern edge of the quest zone | Read the note/terminal |
| **Access Code** | **2896** | Manual entry at padlock |

## Step 3: Retrieving the Livestock Documents

With the code in hand, return to the area near the first objective. The documents are housed inside a reinforced building. Many players struggle here because the front entrance is often barred or heavily guarded. Instead, move to the **back of the building**.

You will find a door secured with a padlock. This is where your code (2896) comes into play. Interact with the padlock to enter the code and gain access to the room. Inside, the documents will be laying on a table or inside a filing cabinet. Grab them immediately and prepare for extraction.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Picking up the documents often triggers a fresh wave of AI spawns in the immediate vicinity. Ensure your stamina is full before grabbing the item so you can sprint to a defensible position.

## Unlocking the Secret Vendor: Vulture

While the Livestock quest is a standard task, it is closely associated with the new mechanics introduced in 0.4, specifically the secret vendor known as **Vulture**. Vulture is a scavenger who resells gear lost by PMCs on the battlefield. Understanding how to find him is just as important as completing your missions.

Vulture does not stay in one place. He operates on a weekly rotation and can be found at "Major COPs" (Command Outposts). These are identified on your map by icons with **three black dots** rather than one.

### Major COP Locations for Vulture
Vulture can appear at any of the following major locations:
*   **Harrison** (Commonly active)
*   **Titan** (High-security zone)
*   **Westmore**
*   **Stalwart**
*   **Crusader**
*   **Bronco**

To see if Vulture is active at a COP, you must first "capture" the location by clearing the AI and interacting with the main computer. If the computer screen is yellow, the area is clear and Vulture's services will become available in your vendor menu.

## Why Complete the Livestock Quest?

Completing quests like Livestock is the primary way to increase your reputation with the main vendors. However, Vulture operates differently. You cannot level him up through standard contracts. Instead, you must sell him specific high-value items, primarily military equipment and rare loot found in high-tier zones like Midnight Sapphire.

| Vendor Level | Reward/Unlock | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Basic PMC Scavenged Gear | Cheap entry-level kits |
| **Level 3** | **Small Safe** | Secure high-value loot storage |
| **Level 4** | **Large Safe** | 108 slots of secure storage |

The **Large Safe** is the ultimate goal for many players in 2026. It allows you to store massive amounts of currency and rare items without cluttering your main stash with wallets. This makes the **gray zone warfare livestock quest guide** a stepping stone toward a much larger endgame loop involving Vulture and high-stakes extraction.

## Tactical Tips for the Livestock Mission

1.  **Silencers are Mandatory:** The Car Repair Shop area is a sound trap. Unsuppressed shots will draw AI from the surrounding woods, making the document retrieval nearly impossible.
2.  **Check the Back Door First:** Don't waste ammunition trying to blast through the front of the document building. The back padlock is the only intended way in.
3.  **Team Synergy:** If playing in a squad, have one player stay on the roof of the repair shop to provide overwatch while another player enters the code at the padlock.
4.  **Inventory Management:** The Livestock documents take up a 2x2 slot in your inventory. Ensure you have space in your backpack or secure container before reaching the final objective.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Car Office Code the same for everyone?**

A: In the current 2026 version of the 0.4 update, the code **2896** has been consistent for the majority of players. However, you should still visit the intelligence location to ensure the quest log updates correctly, or the padlock may remain uninteractable.

**Q: I found Vulture, but he isn't selling any safes. Why?**

A: Vulture's inventory scales with your reputation with him. To unlock the Small and Large Safes, you must sell him high-value military equipment. He does not accept standard weapons or armor that other vendors like Gunny or Handshake buy.

**Q: Can I complete the Livestock quest on a PvE server?**

A: Yes, this **gray zone warfare livestock quest guide** applies to both PvP and PvE servers. The locations, codes, and AI behaviors remain identical across both modes, though the threat of enemy players is removed in PvE.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the Livestock documents?**

A: If you die before extracting, the documents will remain on your corpse. You must return to your body to retrieve them, or restart the mission if the body despawns. It is highly recommended to place quest documents in your secure container if you have the space.
