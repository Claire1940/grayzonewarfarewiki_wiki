<Section icon={Target} title="Following Task Breadcrumbs for Progression">

Tasks are the backbone of your progression in Gray Zone Warfare. Each task provides a series of objectives that act as breadcrumbs, leading you across Lamang Island and introducing you to its dangers and rewards. Understanding how to efficiently follow these task breadcrumbs is crucial for leveling up and unlocking better gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Task">
    Approach a vendor (e.g., **Handshake**, **Gunny**, **Lab Rat**) at your Forward Operating Base (FOB) and accept available tasks. Pay close attention to the task description for initial clues about locations and objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Key Locations">
    Cross-reference the task description with your interactive map. Look for specific POIs mentioned, such as "the shack on the outskirts" or "the town's lumberyard." Mark these locations on your map for easier navigation. Early tasks often guide you through starter towns like **Kiu Vongsa**, **Nam Thaven**, or **Pha Lang**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plan Your Route">
    Consider the most efficient and safest path to your objective. This involves identifying potential threats, alternate routes, and nearby landing zones (LZs) for helicopter insertion/extraction. For example, the "First Recon" task guides you through the starter town landmark loop, teaching basic navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Adapt">
    Once deployed, follow your planned route. Be prepared to adapt to unexpected encounters with AI enemies or hostile players. Collect any required items or intel, mark locations as instructed, and always prioritize survival. The "Helping Hand" task often leads to early key-gated progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract and Report">
    After completing objectives, head to the nearest safe extraction point. Secure your loot and return to your FOB to hand in the task to the respective vendor. This completes the breadcrumb trail for that specific mission, rewarding you with EXP, reputation, and valuable items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Task Reward Rebalance">
Update 0.4 rebalanced task rewards, placing a greater emphasis on money rather than just items. This makes early task completion even more valuable for establishing a stable economy. Completing a task for the first time also grants a larger reputation boost.
</Callout>

</Section>
