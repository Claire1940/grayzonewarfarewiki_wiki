Helicopters are not merely transport vehicles; they are a critical tactical asset. Players are immune to damage while onboard, making them a safe haven during dangerous transits. Furthermore, a 4-second invulnerability window after stepping off the helicopter provides a brief but crucial opportunity for repositioning or engaging threats without immediate risk. The sound of the helicopter itself can also serve as a tactical distraction, drawing AI attention away from your immediate landing zone.

<Section icon={Target} title="Planning Your Extraction Routes">
Effective evacuation in Gray Zone Warfare requires meticulous planning. Understanding the map, identifying key points of interest (POIs), and knowing your landing zones (LZs) are all critical components. Each faction starts in a specific town, influencing early task flow and optimal extraction paths.

For instance, Crimson Shield International begins near Kiu Vongsa, Mithras Security Systems near Nam Thaven, and Lamang Recovery Initiative near Pha Lang. These starter towns not only dictate your initial tasks but also your closest and safest LZs. As you progress, you'll venture into more dangerous areas like Ban Pa, Fort Narith, and Tiger Bay, where extraction planning becomes even more complex.

<Callout type="warning" title="AFK Timer Alert">
Do not go AFK outside safe areas. The inactivity limit is 15 minutes, and if you are removed from a live deployment while AFK, you will lose all carried gear, similar to a normal death.
</Callout>

### Key Extraction Planning Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Mission Objectives and Location">
    Before deploying, understand your task's exact location and potential extraction points nearby. Use an interactive map to identify primary and secondary LZs. Consider the threat level of the area and the type of enemies you might encounter (Rebels, LAF Soldiers).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Faction Wisely">
    Your faction choice impacts your starting town and the proximity to certain LZs. For example, Lamang Recovery Initiative starts near Pha Lang Airfield, offering quick access to contested zones. Crimson Shield International near Kiu Vongsa might offer a slower, safer early progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Gear and Secure Lockbox">
    Before evacuating, ensure all high-value or quest-critical items are placed in your Secure Lockbox. This protects them from loss upon death. Melee weapons are also not lost in the same way as normal carried gear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Hydration and Energy">
    Low hydration and energy can severely impair your ability to sprint, heal, and survive. Always ensure these thresholds are managed, especially during a high-stress extraction. Dehydration can lead to hazy vision, making combat and navigation difficult.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Communicate with Squadmates">
    If playing in a squad, coordinate your extraction plan. Designate who covers which angles during disembarkation and ensure everyone is aware of the chosen LZ and potential threats. Squadmates can revive you within 180 seconds if you enter a coma, but this is impossible if you are separated during extraction.
  </Step>
</Steps>
