## Quest Overview: The Choice at Ban Pa

The quest, often referred to by players as "Cult Business" but officially titled "Shadow over Ban Pa" or "Mounds of Madness" depending on your vendor of choice, is a branching mission. You are sent to the village of Ban Pa to retrieve a specific item—a ritualistic book or "Mel"—from the heart of the cult's operations.

What makes this mission unique is the choice you must make upon returning to base. You can hand the item over to **Handshake** or **Artisan**. Choosing one will significantly boost your reputation with them while penalizing your standing with the other.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Ban Pa

Ban Pa is a high-danger zone due to the dense vegetation and the elevated huts that provide AI enemies with excellent sightlines. To complete the **gray zone warfare cult business quest**, you must reach the Elder's Hut, which is located near the central pier area.

### 1. Infiltration and Clearing
Approach Ban Pa from the outskirts, preferably from the south or west to avoid being funneled into the main road. The AI in this area are known for using "wall-bang" tactics through the wooden slats of the huts. Clear the immediate guards near the entrance before moving toward the central cluster of buildings.

### 2. Locating the Elder's Hut
The Elder's Hut is a large, central building near the right-hand side pier. You will need to find the stairs leading to the upper floor. Be prepared for a "boss" or elite guard spawn inside the building. Once the area is clear, look behind the main desk or seating area for a "weird book." This is the item required for the quest.

### 3. Extraction
Once you have the book, place it immediately into your **Safe Container** (Secure Box). This ensures that even if you are killed during the extraction process, you will not lose the quest item. Head to the nearest Landing Zone (LZ) to return to base.

## The Secret Vendor: Vulture and the 0.4 Update

In the 2026 0.4 update, a new element has been added to the regional gameplay around Lang and Ban Pa: the secret vendor known as **Vulture**. While not directly part of the **gray zone warfare cult business quest** objectives, Vulture is essential for players who struggle with gear loss during difficult missions.

Vulture is a scavenger who resells gear lost by PMCs. He does not stay in one place; instead, he rotates weekly between "Major COPs" (Command Outposts) marked with three black dots on your map.

### Why Find Vulture?
If you lose your high-tier kit while attempting the cult quest in Ban Pa, Vulture may have scavenged it. He sells weapons and armor at a premium, but they are often fully repaired (95% to 100% condition). More importantly, leveling up Vulture unlocks high-capacity storage containers.

> **💡 Tip:** To level up Vulture quickly, sell him "Military Equipment" found at high-tier locations like Midnight Sapphire. While he pays less than other vendors, the reputation gain is vital for unlocking the Large Safe.

## Advanced Tactics for Ban Pa

To successfully navigate the **gray zone warfare cult business quest** in 2026, you should consider the following tactical adjustments:

*   **Suppressors are Mandatory:** The cultists in Ban Pa respond aggressively to unsuppressed gunfire. To avoid being swarmed, use a suppressor on your primary weapon.
*   **Check the Computer:** When capturing COPs near the quest area, check the main computer. If the screen is **Yellow**, the area is clear. If it is **Gray**, enemies (AI or players) are still nearby.
*   **High-Value Loot:** While in the Elder's Hut, look for military-grade items. These can be sold to Vulture or Lab Rat to help offset the costs of your expedition.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the book located for the gray zone warfare cult business quest?**

A: The book (referred to as "Mel") is located on the upper floor of the Elder's Hut in the village of Ban Pa. It is usually found on a table or desk behind the main NPC spawn point in that building.

**Q: Can I complete both versions of the quest?**

A: No. Once you hand the item to one vendor, the quest for the other vendor will automatically fail, and you will receive a reputation penalty with the vendor you did not choose.

**Q: What happens if I lose the book before extracting?**

A: If the book is in your backpack and you die, you will have to return to Ban Pa and retrieve it again. To prevent this, always place quest items in your Safe Container (Secure Box) as soon as you pick them up.

**Q: Is the Vulture vendor related to the cult quest?**

A: Not directly, but Vulture is often found in the same region (Lang). Finding him is useful for buying back gear if you die during the difficult Ban Pa infiltration.
