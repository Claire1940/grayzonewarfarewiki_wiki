<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Completion Walkthrough">

Follow this systematic guide to complete the task efficiently. Ensure you have adequate ammunition and healing supplies before heading out, as the starter towns can feature high AI density.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Faction Key">
    Deploy to your faction's starter town. Eliminate local militia around the Breakdown Service garage and loot their bodies. Check their pockets and backpacks specifically, as keys do not spawn on the ground or in weapon boxes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Garage Perimeter">
    Approach the Breakdown Service building carefully. Usually, 2 to 4 AI guards patrol the interior bays and the exterior yard. Neutralize them before attempting to unlock the office door to prevent being shot in the back.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock the Office and Retrieve the Handgun">
    Walk up to the yellow-walled office door inside the garage. Equip your Breakdown Service Key and unlock the door. On the main wooden desk inside, you will find the customized Colt M1911 pistol. Interacting with it will place it in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Quest Item">
    Immediately open your inventory and drag the customized handgun into your Secure Lockbox. This ensures that even if you get eliminated by AI or players on your way back to the Landing Zone, you will keep the item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract and Hand In">
    Run to your nearest Landing Zone (LZ) and call in a helicopter. Return to the main Forward Operating Base. Open the Vendors menu, select Gunny, go to his Tasks tab, and hand in the customized handgun to complete the quest.
  </Step>
</Steps>
