## Who is Vulture? The Scavenger of Lamang

Vulture is a specialized trader introduced to provide a "lost and found" mechanic for the PMC community. He doesn't sit comfortably at your faction's base; instead, he occupies captured Command Outposts (COPs) out in the field. His primary gimmick is that he scavenges the gear of PMCs who have died and failed to recover their bodies. 

Because his inventory is dictated by player deaths on your specific server, his stock is highly dynamic. On a high-activity PvP server, Vulture might be stocked with fully decked-out M4s, high-tier armor, and rare backpacks. On a fresh or quiet server, his shelves might look relatively bare.

## How to Find Vulture: Weekly Rotations

One of the most common questions in any **gray zone warfare vendor quests guide** is where to actually find the traders. Vulture does not stay in one place. He appears at exactly two major COPs per week, and these locations are consistent across all regions, factions, and game modes (PvE and PvP).

If you travel to a COP where Vulture is supposed to be and he is missing, it is likely a server-side bug. Players in 2026 have reported that switching servers or waiting for a hotfix often resolves this. Since the locations change every Monday, the community usually identifies his new spots within hours of the weekly reset.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Vulture only appears at "Major" COPs. Ensure your faction has captured the location before attempting to trade, as hostile NPCs or rival factions may still be contesting the area.

## Leveling Vulture: The Reputation Grind

Unlike other traders who gain reputation through specific mission completion, Vulture’s "quest" is a continuous cycle of scavenging and selling. To level him up, you must sell him **High Value Loot**. This includes items like jewelry, watches, and other valuables found in safes or residential areas.

There is a significant trade-off when leveling Vulture. He pays approximately **50% less** for valuables compared to Turncoat. This creates a strategic dilemma for players: do you take the quick cash from Turncoat, or do you "invest" that money into Vulture to unlock his higher-tier loyalty rewards?

## Scavenging the Best Gear

Because Vulture's stock is based on PMC deaths, he often bypasses the standard level requirements of other traders. A Level 1 player who finds Vulture can potentially buy a high-level helmet or a long-barreled rifle that would otherwise require Level 4 loyalty with Gunny.

### Items to Watch For:
1.  **20" M4 Barrels:** These are not sold by standard vendors and are highly sought after for long-range builds.
2.  **60-Round Magazines:** Essential for squad play and high-intensity firefights.
3.  **Loot-Only Helmets:** Some of the best ballistic protection in the game can only be found as loot or via Vulture.
4.  **Delta Backpacks:** Large capacity bags that allow for massive loot hauls.

> **💡 Tip:** Always inspect weapons in Vulture's inventory. Players often die with rare attachments like suppressors or high-zoom optics that Vulture will sell as part of the complete firearm.

## Expert Strategies for Vulture Trading

To make the most of this vendor, you should integrate "Valuable Runs" into your standard gameplay. Locations with high concentrations of safes, such as the villas or office complexes in major cities, are prime spots for gathering the tribute needed for Vulture.

*   **The Monday Check:** Make it a habit to check community hubs every Monday to see where he has moved.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Vulture have traditional quests like Handshake or Gunny?**

A: No, Vulture does not offer standard "go here, do that" quests. His progression is entirely based on selling him high-value loot to increase your reputation level.

**Q: Why isn't Vulture at the COP the guide mentioned?**

A: Vulture rotates his location every Monday. Additionally, he is known to be occasionally buggy and may not appear on every server instance. Try relogging or checking a different server if he is missing from a confirmed location.

**Q: Is it better to sell to Vulture or Turncoat?**

A: If you need cash, sell to Turncoat as he pays nearly double. If you want to unlock the MP7 A2 or the AK-308 for direct purchase, you must sacrifice that profit and sell to Vulture to gain reputation.

**Q: Can I buy my own lost gear back from Vulture?**

A: Theoretically, yes. If you die and no one loots your body before the server "reclaims" it, your gear can appear in Vulture's inventory. However, other players on the server can also buy it, so you have to be quick.
