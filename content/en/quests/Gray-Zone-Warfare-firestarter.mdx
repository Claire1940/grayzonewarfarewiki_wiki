<Section icon={Shield} title="Securing Keys for Early Tasks">
Many Firestarter tasks, especially those involving intel retrieval or specific item collection, are gated by keys. Knowing where to find these keys and what they unlock is vital.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Key-Gated Tasks">
    Before deploying, check your task objectives for any mention of locked rooms or required keys. Tasks like "Helping Hand" explicitly require a key to progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Key Spawns">
    Most early game keys drop from enemies within the associated POI or can be found in specific loot containers. For example, Kiu Vongsa Restaurant Storage Key often drops from enemies in Kiu Vongsa.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Key Collection">
    If a task requires a key, make finding that key your primary objective for that deployment. Do not attempt the task without it, as it will lead to wasted time and potential gear loss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Understand Key Utility">
    Some keys unlock valuable loot rooms in addition to task objectives. Prioritize keys that offer both progression and economic benefits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
