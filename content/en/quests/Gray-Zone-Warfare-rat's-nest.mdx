<Section icon={Target} title="Gray Zone Warfare Rat's Nest Task Breakdown">

When deploying to Lamang Island, one of the earliest progression roadblocks players encounter is the **Gray Zone Warfare rat's nest** task. Assigned by Gunny, this contract requires operators to infiltrate their faction's respective starter town, locate two critical pieces of criminal intel, and safely return them to the Forward Operating Base (FOB). Because this mission is handed out early, completing it is vital for building vendor reputation and unlocking higher-tier ammunition, weapon attachments, and rifles.

Unlike later sandbox operations, the difficulty of this task lies not in surviving heavily armored combatants, but in navigating dense urban structures and identifying the precise spawn locations of the required papers. The criminal lists and stashed notes are small physical objects that blend into the environment, making it easy to walk past them if you do not know exactly where to look.

<Section icon={Map} title="Crimson Shield International: Kiu Vongsa Locations">

For operators representing Crimson Shield International, the search takes place in the eastern starter town of Kiu Vongsa. The two pieces of intel are hidden in the northern and central blocks of the town, areas frequently patrolled by local militia armed with pistols and basic submachine guns.

The first piece of intel, the Thug's Note, is located in a single-story residential building with cracked concrete walls. Look for a small wooden table pushed against a stained mattress. The second piece of intel, the Criminal List, is situated inside a building closer to the construction site and water tower. Search the desk surface next to broken electronics to secure the ledger.

| Intel Type | Coordinates | Landmark | Spawn Surface |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Thug's Note** | 141 164 | East side residential sector | Wooden bedside table |
| **Criminal List** | 142 165 | Near northern town perimeter | Metal desk top |

<Callout type="tip" title="CSI Operator Strategy">
Approach the 142 165 coordinate from the eastern tree line. This path minimizes your exposure to the open streets and allows you to flank the guards patrolling the construction zone.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mithras Security Systems: Nam Thaven Locations">

Mithras Security Systems operators must deploy to the western town of Nam Thaven. The urban layout here features narrow alleyways and dense vegetation, making short-barreled weapons like the AKS-74U highly effective for clearing rooms.

The first target is a small, run-down brick building located near the local marketplace coordinates. Inside, the Thug's Note sits on top of a plastic chair in the corner of a dimly lit bedroom. The second objective, the Criminal List, is located in a multi-room house near the gas station. Walk past the main entrance, enter the back room, and look for a wooden shelf unit where the documents spawn next to a radio receiver.

| Intel Type | Coordinates | Landmark | Spawn Surface |

The second objective, the Criminal List, requires navigating to the residential buildings near the water tower. The documents are placed on a desk inside a small office room on the ground floor of a two-story building.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Task Execution Guide">

To complete the mission safely and minimize the risk of losing your gear, follow this optimized deployment sequence. Earning vendor progression is smooth when you treat every raid as a systematic extraction process.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Lockbox Preparation">
    Before loading into the helicopter, open your character inventory and clear two slots inside your Secure Lockbox. This ensures that even if you are killed by AI or an enemy operator, the retrieved intel remains safe in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Infiltrate the Town Perimeter">
    Fly to your faction's starter town landing zone. Move slowly toward the coordinate locations, using bushes and fences for cover. Keep your weapon raised and listen for voice lines from nearby rebel scouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear and Retrieve the Intel">
    Infiltrate the target buildings identified for your faction. Clear all interior rooms before interacting with the documents. As soon as you pick up the Thug's Note and Criminal List, immediately move them into your Secure Lockbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Safely Extract and Turn In">
    Navigate back to the nearest landing zone and call for extraction. Once back at the FOB, open your tasks tab, select Gunny, click on the **Gray Zone Warfare rat's nest** mission, and hand in both documents to claim your rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management Danger">

</Callout>
