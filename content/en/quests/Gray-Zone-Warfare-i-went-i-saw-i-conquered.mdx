### Faction Distance Analysis
Depending on which PMC you joined at the start of your career, one of these towns will be your "home" base, while the other two will require long-distance helicopter transport or dangerous overland trekking.

| Faction | Home Town | Distance to Fort Narith | Scouting Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crimson Shield** | Kiu Vongsa | 25-30+ min walk | Moderate |
| **Mithras Security** | Nam Thaven | 15-20 min walk | High (Central) |
| **Lamang Recovery** | Pha Lang | 5-10 min walk | Low (East Access) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Helicopter Efficiency">
Do not try to walk between all three towns in one run. Use the **Landing Zones (LZs)**. Return to your Forward Operating Base (FOB) after scouting one town to reset your supplies and then fly out to the next. This minimizes the chance of losing all progress to a single dehydration or combat event.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step Scouting Walkthrough">

Follow these steps to ensure the **Gray Zone Warfare i went i saw i conquered** task registers correctly in your quest log.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Mission and Gear Up">
    Visit **Handshake** at your FOB and accept the task. Since this is a scouting mission, bring a lightweight kit. A suppressed **AK-74M** or **M4A1** with a 1x/4x optic is ideal for handling local rebels without drawing too much attention.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Home Town Trigger">
    If you haven't already, walk into your own faction's starter town. This should immediately check off one of the three requirements. Most players trigger this naturally during the **First Recon** or **Meds Wanted** tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Infiltrating Rival Territory">
    Call a helicopter to the LZ nearest to the second town on your list. Upon arrival, stay low. You do not need to enter the heart of the town; reaching the outskirts or the first major POI (like the gas station in Pha Lang) usually triggers the objective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Maintaining Sustain">
    Check your **Hydration and Energy** levels. If they drop below 50%, your blood regeneration stops. If they hit 1%, you will suffer from hazy vision, making extraction significantly harder. Carry at least two water bottles and one food item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Extraction">
    Once all three locations are marked as "Scouted" in your UI, head to the nearest extraction LZ. You must survive the trip back to the FOB to hand in the mission to Handshake.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Task Completion">
Handing in this mission provides a massive boost to your **Handshake Reputation**, which is required to unlock Rank 2 and Rank 3 gear, including better body armor and larger containers.
</Callout>

### Dealing with Injuries
If you take fire while scouting a rival town, prioritize stopping the bleeding immediately. Light, medium, and severe bleeding will drain your blood levels, eventually leading to a coma if not treated.

| Injury Type | Treatment Item | Effect of Neglect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bleeding** | Bandage / Tourniquet | Continuous blood loss, eventual coma |
| **Fracture** | Splint / Surgery Kit | Reduced movement speed or aim sway |
| **Pain** | Painkillers | Blurred vision and muffled audio |
| **Low Blood** | Blood Bag / Saline | Fainting and inability to sprint |

<Callout type="tip" title="Coma Recovery">
If you fall into a coma while solo, the run is likely over. However, in a squad, your teammates have approximately **180 seconds** to stabilize and revive you. Always try to scout in pairs when visiting rival territories.
</Callout>
