Navigating the tactical landscape of Lamang Island requires precision, patience, and a deep understanding of the mission objectives provided by your vendors. One such mission that often trips up contractors is the **gray zone warfare delayed package** task. Issued by Turncoat, this mission sends players on a multi-stage journey across the map, requiring both keen observation and the luck of the draw when it comes to finding specific keys. Whether you are a solo operative or part of a coordinated squad, completing the **gray zone warfare delayed package** mission is essential for progressing through Turncoat’s storyline and unlocking higher-tier equipment.

In this guide, we will break down every step of the process, from locating the initial 2x2 item near the coast to infiltrating one of the most dangerous landmarks in the game: the Pha Lang Airport. We will also cover the specific key requirements and tactical considerations you should keep in mind to ensure you don't lose your gear—or the package—mid-mission.

## Task Overview and Prerequisites

Before heading out into the field, it is vital to understand what the **gray zone warfare delayed package** mission entails. This is not a simple "fetch and return" quest; it involves retrieving a physical item that occupies space in your inventory and delivering it to a locked location in a high-threat area.

| Task Element | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vendor** | Turncoat |
| **Primary Objective** | Retrieve a 2x2 package and deliver it to Pha Lang Airport |
| **Required Key** | Hangar 03 Key (G3) |
| **Package Location** | Target Bay (near Juliet LZ) |
| **Delivery Location** | Pha Lang Airport Hangar 03 |
| **Item Size** | 2x2 Slots |

> **Warning:** Since the package takes up a 2x2 space, ensure you have an appropriate backpack or tactical rig before attempting to pick it up. If your inventory is full, you will be unable to secure the item.

## Locating the Package at Target Bay

The first phase of the **gray zone warfare delayed package** mission begins in the southern region of the map, specifically around Target Bay. For players aligned with the Lamang Recovery Initiative, your primary insertion point will be Juliet LZ. 

Once you touch down at Juliet LZ, you need to head toward the small cluster of structures located right along the waterfront at Target Bay. Look for a small, unassuming wooden hut situated near the pier area. 

### Steps to Retrieve the Package:
1.  **Approach Target Bay:** Move cautiously toward the coastal buildings. While this area is generally lower in threat than the airport, AI scavengers frequently patrol the outskirts.
2.  **Enter the Hut:** Locate the small shack directly in front of the bay. 
3.  **Find the Table:** Upon entering the home, turn to your right. The package is sitting clearly on a wooden table.
4.  **Secure the Item:** Interact with the package to place it in your inventory. Remember, this is a physical item; if you die before delivering it, you may need to return to this location to retrieve it again in a subsequent raid.

## Securing the Hangar 03 Key

The most significant hurdle in the **gray zone warfare delayed package** task isn't finding the package itself, but gaining access to the delivery point. The package must be placed inside Hangar 03 at the Pha Lang Airport, which is securely locked.

To enter, you need the **Hangar 03 Key** (often labeled as G3 in the game’s UI). Unlike some quest items that are guaranteed spawns, this key is a random drop from NPCs.

| NPC Type | Drop Likelihood | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Airport Guards** | High | Pha Lang Airport Perimeter |
| **Local Militia** | Medium | Nearby Towns/Villages |
| **Elite AI** | High | Inside Airport Hangars/Terminal |

To farm this key, head to the Pha Lang Airport and begin clearing the guards around the hangars. Most players report that the key drops fairly consistently from the NPCs stationed directly outside the hangar buildings. If you are playing with a squad, check with your teammates, as only one person needs the key to open the door for the entire group.

## Infiltrating Pha Lang Airport

With the package in your bag and the Hangar 03 key in your pocket (or your secure container), it is time to head north to the Pha Lang Airport. This is a high-traffic area often contested by both AI and rival players, so tactical movement is non-negotiable.

### Reaching Hangar 03
Hangar 03 is located on the western side of the airport complex. As you approach from the south or east, use the runway's foliage and peripheral buildings for cover. Avoid running across the open tarmac, as snipers—both AI and human—often watch the long sightlines of the runway.

### Planting the Package
Once you reach Hangar 03:
1.  **Unlock the Door:** Use the G3 key on the side door of the hangar.
2.  **Locate the Aircraft:** Inside the hangar, you will see a large transport plane.
3.  **Find the Front Gear:** Move to the nose of the plane. You need to "pocket" or plant the package at the front landing gear (the wheels under the cockpit).
4.  **Hold Interaction:** Stand near the front gear and hold the interaction key until the progress bar completes.

> **Tip:** Clear the interior of the hangar before attempting to plant the package. AI can sometimes clip through or hide in the corners of these large structures.

## Tactical Considerations for Turncoat's Tasks

Turncoat is known for sending players into "hot" zones. When working on the **gray zone warfare delayed package**, keep the following strategies in mind:

*   **Faction Cooperation:** If you see other players from your faction at the airport, try to coordinate. Many players are likely working on the same task or farming keys, and a larger group provides significantly better security.
*   **Secure Container Strategy:** While the package itself cannot be placed in most secure containers due to its size, you should always keep your Hangar 03 key in your secure slot. Losing the key upon death is a much bigger setback than losing the package.
*   **Patience at the Airport:** The AI at Pha Lang Airport can be aggressive. If you hear heavy gunfire, wait for the AI to settle down or for another squad to move through before you make your move toward Hangar 03.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the package for the gray zone warfare delayed package task?**

A: The package is located in Target Bay, near Juliet LZ. Look for a small wooden hut on the coast; inside, the 2x2 package is sitting on a table to the right of the entrance.

**Q: Can I complete the task if someone else has already opened Hangar 03?**

A: Yes. If the door to Hangar 03 is already unlocked by another player, you can enter and plant the package without needing the key yourself. However, it is always safer to bring your own key in case the door is closed or the instance is fresh.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the package but before delivering it?**

A: The package is a physical item in your inventory. If you die and cannot recover your body, the package is lost. You will need to return to the hut at Target Bay to pick up a new package before heading back to the airport.

**Q: Is the Hangar 03 key a guaranteed drop?**

A: No, it is a random loot item. You can find it on the bodies of NPCs at Pha Lang Airport. It may take clearing the area several times before the key drops, so be prepared for a bit of a grind or check with faction members to see if anyone can lend you the key.
