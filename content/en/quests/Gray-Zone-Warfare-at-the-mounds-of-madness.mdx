For experienced operators, the Mounds of Madness offers opportunities for advanced tactical play. This involves exploiting terrain, understanding enemy patrol patterns, and leveraging the game's mechanics for a strategic advantage.

### Utilizing Terrain and Cover

- **High Ground Advantage**: Seek elevated positions for scouting and engaging targets from a distance. This reduces the risk of being flanked.
- **Concealment vs. Cover**: Differentiate between concealment (hiding from sight) and cover (protection from bullets). Always prioritize hard cover during engagements.
- **Flanking Routes**: Use the hilly terrain and dense vegetation to bypass direct engagements or flank entrenched enemies.

### Understanding AI Behavior

AI in Gray Zone Warfare can be sophisticated. They often react to sound, visual cues, and the presence of fallen comrades.

| Behavior Aspect | Observation                                                | Counter-Tactic                                            |
| :-------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
