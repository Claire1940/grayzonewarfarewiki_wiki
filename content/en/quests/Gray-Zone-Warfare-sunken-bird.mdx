<Section icon={Target} title="Task Overview and Requirements">

The **Gray Zone Warfare sunken bird** mission is a key transition task that pushes players out of their faction's safe starter towns and into the more contested Tier 2 areas of Lamang Island. Assigned by Gunny, the task requires players to investigate a downed scout helicopter that crashed into the marshy lake area southwest of Ban Pa. 

To complete this mission, you must navigate a highly exposed swamp, locate the submerged fuselage, search the cockpit to retrieve a flight data recorder, and return the device safely to the FOB. Because the item is physical, dying after retrieving the flight data recorder means you must redeploy and grab it again.

<Section icon={Map} title="Finding the Sunken Bird Location">

The crashed helicopter is located at coordinates **202 136**, situated in the shallow swamp waters southwest of the main Ban Pa village. The crash site is visible from the western shoreline of the lake, but approaching it requires wading through deep water that slows your operator to a walking pace. 

Navigating the area is highly dangerous due to the lack of hard cover. Dense reeds obscure your vision, while AI enemies patrolling the nearby Ban Pa fishing huts can easily spot you through the brush. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Management">
Wading through water drains stamina rapidly. Keep your weight low and avoid sprinting right before entering the water so you have enough stamina to push through to the helicopter.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

Follow these steps to complete the task and return to the Forward Operating Base without losing your gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Mission and Prepare Gear">
    Interact with Gunny at the FOB to accept the task. Equipping an assault rifle chambered in 5.45x39mm or 5.56x45mm is recommended. Bring at least two bandages, a splint, and a basic surgical kit inside your secure lockbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy to Landing Zone India">
    Call in a helicopter transport to LZ India. Ensure you wait for the transport immunity window (4 seconds) to clear before moving away from the drop point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Move to the Swamp Western Shoreline">
    Travel west-northwest towards the lake perimeter. Avoid running through the center of the swamp; instead, stick to the brush along the tree line to keep the foliage between you and the Ban Pa village.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate and Interact with the Downed Helicopter">
    Wade into the water at grid coordinate 202 136. The helicopter tail is sticking out of the water. Approach the cockpit area at the front. Press your interact key to search the cockpit and retrieve the Flight Data Recorder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract and Hand in the Item">
    Retreat back to the western tree line, then navigate south to LZ India. Call for transport, return to the FOB, and interact with Gunny to hand in the recorder and claim your rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Loot Loss Risk">
If you die while carrying the flight data recorder in your backpack, it will remain on your corpse. If you cannot recover your body, you must go back to the helicopter to get another recorder. Put the item in your Secure Lockbox immediately if you have space.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Strategy and Gear Recommendations">

The swamp environment poses unique combat challenges. The water slows your movement, making it impossible to dodge incoming fire. If you are ambushed, your best option is to submerge slightly or push forward to the helicopter fuselage, which serves as the only reliable hard cover in the immediate area.

Because the local rebels at Ban Pa use assault rifles and light armor, your weapon selection needs to account for mid-range accuracy and penetration.

<Callout type="success" title="Weapon Handling Rework">
Due to weapon weight and sway changes introduced in recent updates, using a lighter carbine or an assault rifle with high ergonomics will make aiming while wading in water much easier.
</Callout>

| Gear Slot | Recommended Item | Purpose | Alternate Option |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Primary Weapon | M4A1 or AK-74N | Reliable mid-range damage | SKS with optic |
| Ammunition | 5.56 M855 / 5.45 PP | Penetrates Class 2 and 3 armor | 7.62x39mm PS |
| Body Armor | Class 3 Plate Carrier | Balances weight and protection | Class 2 Soft Armor |
| Medical Kit | IFAK / Combat Tourniquet | Treats severe bleeding in water | Bandage + Splint |

When navigating the swamp, stay mindful of your hydration and energy levels. Getting stuck in deep water with zero stamina due to dehydration will make you an easy target. Always carry a water bottle or purchase soft drinks from Lab Rat before deployment.
