<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Task Walkthrough">

This task requires systematic movement. Charging down the main asphalt road will leave you exposed to sniper fire from the surrounding hills and resort balconies. Follow this structured approach to reach the objective and secure the intel safely.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Infiltrate via LZ Hotel">
    Land at LZ Hotel, situated south of the main resort area. Immediately move northwest into the tree line to keep a low profile. Avoid sprinting across the open golf course greens, as LAF patrols frequently scan these areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Western Perimeter Road">
    Follow the western perimeter fence toward the main road leading out of the resort. Keep the resort villas on your right. Move cover-to-cover using boulders and utility poles to shield your profile from patrols.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Checkpoint Guardhouse">
    Locate the small concrete guardhouse stationed at the western exit checkpoint. Clear the immediate area of guards. One or two armored LAF soldiers typically patrol the interior of the guardhouse. Use grenades to clear the room before entry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Operational Documents">
    Enter the guardhouse and search the metal desk near the communications console. Interact with the document folder to secure the intel. Place the recovered items directly into your Secure Lockbox to prevent losing them if you are neutralized.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract Safely">
    Retrace your steps southward through the tree line back toward LZ Hotel. Do not take the main road back, as respawned AI patrols will likely block your path. Call in your helicopter before reaching the landing zone to minimize wait time on the ground.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Zone Check">
If you enter a coma state due to heavy blood loss, your squadmates have approximately 180 seconds to revive you. Never attempt this run solo in PvP mode unless you are prepared to play slow and utilize suppressed weapons.
</Callout>

</Section>
