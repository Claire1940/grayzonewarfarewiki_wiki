## Step 1: Investigating the Elder's House

Your journey to uncover the **gray zone warfare cult business location** begins at the Elder's House. This structure is a landmark within the local village and is often surrounded by mid-tier AI. 

Upon arriving at the house, head directly to the first floor. You are looking for a specific locked room that shows signs of recent cult activity. Once inside, you don't need to pick up a physical item immediately; instead, your character must "report findings" by standing in the center of the room for a few seconds until the objective updates in your task log.

## Step 2: Cracking the Code at the Steel Houses

After reporting from the Elder's House, your next destination is the Steel Houses. This area is notorious for its verticality and tight alleyways. Your goal here is to find the "Angry Message," a document left behind by a disgruntled cult member.

This message contains the numerical code required to access the next **gray zone warfare cult business location**. The note is typically found pinned to a wall or resting on a table within one of the central steel-reinforced structures. Read the note carefully; the code will be added to your intel tab automatically, but it is wise to memorize it or write it down in case of UI glitches.

| Location Feature | Tactical Note |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Steel House Exterior** | High AI density; use suppressors to avoid swarming. |
| **The Angry Message** | Usually found on the second floor of the main house. |
| **Extraction Point** | The nearest LZ is often contested; check your map before moving. |

## Step 3: The Warehouse and Paralog Room

With the code in hand, proceed to the Warehouse District. This industrial zone serves as the primary **gray zone warfare cult business location** for their logistics operations. You are looking for the "Paralog Room," which is secured by a keypad.

Enter the code retrieved from the Steel Houses to unlock the door. Inside, you will find a "White Paper" resting on a desk or crate. Interacting with this paper completes the third phase of the mission. This room often contains high-value weapon attachments, so take a moment to loot the surrounding shelves before departing.

> **Warning:** The Warehouse District is a high-traffic area for players. Expect contact with other PMCs who may be camping the Paralog Room door.

## Step 4: Retrieving the Red Book

The final stage of the mission sends you back toward the Steel Houses, but this time you are targeting the "Loot Room." This is the definitive **gray zone warfare cult business location** where the cult stores its most sacred texts.

Inside the Loot Room, look for the **Red Book**. This is a physical quest item that must be placed in your inventory. Once you have the book, your priority is survival. Head to the nearest Landing Zone (LZ) and call for extraction.

1.  **Clear the perimeter** of the Steel Houses before entering the Loot Room.
2.  **Secure the Red Book** in your backpack or secure container.
3.  **Move to the LZ** using concealed routes to avoid sniper fire.
4.  **Hand over the book** to the vendor at base to complete the task.

## The Secret Vendor: Vulture (Update 0.4)

While searching for the **gray zone warfare cult business location**, you may encounter the new secret vendor, Vulture. Introduced in the 0.4 update, Vulture is a scavenger who resells gear lost by PMCs in the field.

Vulture does not stay in one place. He rotates weekly between "Major COPs" (Command Outposts marked with three dots on your map). To interact with him, your faction must currently control the COP where he is stationed. Vulture is an excellent source for high-tier backpacks and repaired armor that might otherwise be locked behind high-level vendor requirements.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the gray zone warfare cult business location for the Elder's House?**

A: The Elder's House is located in the main residential sector of the starting village. Look for the largest two-story house with a fenced yard. The locked room is on the first floor.

**Q: What do I do if the Red Book does not spawn?**

A: If the Red Book is missing from the **gray zone warfare cult business location**, it is likely that another player has recently picked it up. You may need to wait for the loot to respawn or join a different server instance to find the item.

**Q: Can I complete Cult Business in a squad?**

A: Yes, completing this task in a squad is highly recommended due to the high AI density at the Steel Houses. However, each player must individually interact with the White Paper and the Red Book to receive credit for the mission.

**Q: How do I find Vulture if the COP is gray on the map?**

A: If a COP is gray, it means it is currently neutral or occupied by AI/hostiles. You must clear the area and interact with the main computer to capture the COP for your faction before Vulture will appear and allow you to trade.
