<Section icon={Target} title="Progression Path: Essential Early Tasks">

Progression in Gray Zone Warfare is driven by completing tasks for various vendors. These missions unlock new equipment and higher-tier ammunition. Following the core task route is the fastest way to move from a "rookie" to an elite operator.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="First Recon">
    Deploy to your faction's starter town. You must check the shack on the outskirts, the lumberyard, and the gas station. This task introduces you to the basic landmark loop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meds Wanted">
    Visit the town's water tower to locate UNLRA medical supplies. Completing this for Lab Rat is critical as it unlocks better healing items like IFAKs and tourniquets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Helping Hand">
    Retrieve intel from the restaurant attic. This is often the first "key-gated" task. You will need to find the Restaurant Storage Key on local enemies to progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Shadow Over Ban Pa">
    This major milestone pushes you out of the starter town. Scouting the northern and southern entries of Ban Pa unlocks the Turncoat vendor, expanding your gear options significantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Task Name | Vendor | Primary Reward | Why It Matters |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **First Recon** | Handshake | $1,700 / Armor | Sets the initial town landmarks |
| **Meds Wanted** | Lab Rat | $2,200 / IFAK | Unlocks essential medical sustain |
| **Cache Retrieval** | Gunny | $1,700 / Backpack | Early weapon-focused progression |
| **Helping Hand** | Handshake | $2,300 / Key Holder | First encounter with key-locked areas |

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Bonus">
Completing a task for the first time grants a significant reputation boost. Prioritize finishing new tasks over farming AI to reach Vendor Rank 2 as quickly as possible.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Economy: Vendors and Key Management">

Maintaining a healthy cash flow is vital for replacing lost kits. Money is earned through task rewards and selling looted valuables to the right vendors. Each vendor specializes in different gear categories and has specific reputation thresholds for unlocking better stock.

| Vendor | Specialty | Unlock Requirement |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Handshake** | Armor, Helmets, Gear | Available at start |
| **Gunny** | Weapons, Attachments, Ammo | Available at start |
| **Lab Rat** | Medical, Food, Water | Available at start |
| **Artisan** | Technical Weapon Parts | Complete 'Native Negotiations' |
| **Turncoat** | Broad Inventory / Armor | Complete 'Shadow Over Ban Pa' |

Keys are the most valuable items you can find in the "trenches." They unlock rooms containing high-tier loot or essential task objectives. Always store these in your Secure Lockbox to prevent loss upon death.

| Key Name | Location | Related Task | Loot Type |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Restaurant Storage Key** | Starter Town | Helping Hand | Task Intel / Meds |
| **Sawmill Office Key** | Sawmill | EOD | Weapons / First Aid |
| **Elder's Room Key** | Ban Pa | It's in the Water II | Chemistry / Task Route |
| **Improvised Armory Key** | Fort Narith | Loot Route | High-tier Military Gear |
