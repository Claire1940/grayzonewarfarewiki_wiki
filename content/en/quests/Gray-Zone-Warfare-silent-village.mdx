The wetlands of Lamang Island present a unique tactical challenge, particularly when deployed to the quiet stilt structures of Ban Pa. Known colloquially as the **Gray Zone Warfare silent village**, this location serves as a critical junction for operators transitioning from early-game deployments to mid-game progression. Navigating this area requires an understanding of waterlogged terrain, tight sightlines, and aggressive patrol routes.

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Map Positioning">

The approach from LZ India requires navigating through dense reeds and shallow water, which limits movement speed and makes you vulnerable to elevated sniper positions on stilt structures.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Key Spawns and Loot Routes">

Progression through the village is heavily gated by key locks. Farming these keys is a requirement for completing high-value tasks and accessing locked military crates. Unlike starter towns where keys drop frequently from basic enemies, the keys in the silent village drop from specific patrol leaders and inside pockets of higher-tier Rebel defenders.

<Callout type="warning" title="Key Drop Rates">
Keys are not guaranteed drops. If a key fails to spawn after clearing the village, rotate out of the area or reload the instance in Joint Operations mode to reset the enemy population.
</Callout>

The two most critical keys are the **Ban Pa Elder's Room Key** and the **Ban Pa Fishing Hut Key**. The Elder's Room Key is essential for progressing the faction task line, while the Fishing Hut Key provides access to high-quality weapon storage.

<Section icon={Target} title="Silent Village Task Walkthroughs">

Several vendors route their progression through the village, most notably Handshake and Lab Rat. Completing these tasks unlocks the Turncoat vendor, which significantly expands your access to mid-tier weapons and armor plates.

<Callout type="tip" title="Solo Progression Note">
If you are playing solo in Warfare (PvEvP) mode, wait for other factions to clear the outer patrols before moving into the stilt platforms to reduce the risk of being cornered in the water.
</Callout>

Follow this sequence to complete the primary missions located in the silent village.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Staging Area">
    Insert via LZ India. Move slow through the southern tree line. Avoid running directly through the water, as the noise will alert patrols in the southern stilt houses. Set up a secure perimeter along the tree line to scout the village entrance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scout the Village Entrances">
    For the task *The Shadow Over Ban Pa*, you must scout the northern and southern entry points. Move along the eastern edge to reach the northern bridge marker at grid **205 137**. Once marked, rotate back around the southern swamp line to trigger the second marker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Elder's House">
    Locate the large two-story building in the center of the village. Use the **Elder Room Key** to unlock the door on the second floor. Retrieve the target intelligence documents or complete the water testing kit interaction for Lab Rat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extract via LZ India">
    Do not run straight back down the main wooden walkways. Instead, drop into the shallow water underneath the stilts, using the wooden pillars as cover from high-angle gunners, and proceed back to LZ India.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Executing these steps secures critical reputation gains that allow you to upgrade your inventory options at the Forward Operating Base (FOB).

<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactics for the Gray Zone Warfare silent village">

Combat in the **Gray Zone Warfare silent village** is defined by verticality and movement penalties. The water throughout the village slows your movement by up to 50 percent, making you an easy target if you are caught out of cover.

<Callout type="success" title="Weapon Handling Strategy">
Recent updates modify weapon sway, ergonomics, and arm stamina. When holding angles from the stilt house windows, rest your weapon or drop to a crouch to minimize stamina drain and keep your aim stable.
</Callout>

Prepare your loadout specifically for mid-range skirmishes and close-quarters house clearing.
