## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out to the Nam Theun area, it is essential to understand what Banshee expects from your PMC. The task is divided into three primary objectives: investigating the evidence of human trafficking, finding a hidden access code, and retrieving sensitive documents from a locked office.

| Objective | Location | Key Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Investigate Repair Shop** | Spielberg (Nam Theun) | Approach the metal cages in the back. |
| **Locate Keypad Code** | Residential House (182 127) | Find the note near the television. |
| **Retrieve Documents** | Repair Shop Office | Enter code **2381** and grab the papers. |

> **Warning:** The Spielberg area is heavily patrolled by AI with mid-to-high tier armor. Do not attempt this mission without at least Class 3+ body armor and plenty of 5.56mm or 5.45mm ammunition.

## Preparation and Infiltration

To begin the **livestock gray zone warfare** task, you need to head toward the Spielberg district, located in the northern sector of the Nam Theun region. Your choice of Landing Zone will dictate your initial approach and the amount of resistance you face before reaching the target site.

### Choosing Your Landing Zone

There are two primary LZs available for this mission. Your choice should depend on your faction's current control of the map and your preferred tactical approach.

1.  **LZ November 1:** Provides a more direct route from the western side. It is often contested but offers quicker access to the residential outskirts where the code is hidden.
2.  **LZ November 2:** Located to the south, this LZ allows for a more stealthy approach through the treeline, giving you a vantage point over the car repair shop before you move in.

## Step 1: Investigating the Human Trafficking Evidence

Once you arrive in Spielberg, your first goal for the **livestock gray zone warfare** mission is to locate the abandoned car repair shop. This building is a large, industrial-looking structure that stands out from the smaller residential houses.

Upon entering the repair shop, move toward the rear of the building. You will find several large metal cages. To "investigate" them, you do not need to interact with a specific prompt; you simply need to get close to them or touch the bars. Once your character is within proximity, the first part of the task will trigger as completed in your UI. 

Be careful when entering the shop, as AI often hide behind rusted vehicle frames and stacks of tires. Clear the corners thoroughly before approaching the cages, as getting caught in the open near the back of the shop leaves you vulnerable to fire from the windows.

## Step 2: Finding the Keypad Code (2381)

The second phase of the **livestock gray zone warfare** quest requires you to find a four-digit code to access the locked office. While you can technically skip the search if you already know the code, the game often requires you to "find" the note to progress the sub-objective correctly.

Follow the main road leading away from the car repair shop into the residential cluster. You are looking for a specific house located at coordinates **182 127**.

### House Location Details
*   **Exterior:** A standard residential home with a small porch.
*   **Interior:** Head into the living room area.
*   **The Note:** Look directly in front of the television set. There is a small note left on a table or stand.
*   **The Code:** The note explicitly mentions the code **2381**.

> **💡 Tip:** If the house is being camped by other players, you can approach from the backyard through the fence line to avoid being spotted through the front windows.

## Step 3: Retrieving the Documents

With the code **2381** in hand, return to the car repair shop. Inside, there is a locked door leading to a small office area equipped with a digital keypad. 

1.  Interact with the keypad and enter the code: **2-3-8-1**.
2.  Once the door unlocks, enter the office quickly.
3.  The documents are usually located on the desk or inside a filing cabinet within this room.
4.  Secure the documents in your **Safe Container** immediately. This ensures that even if you are KIA during extraction, you will not lose the quest item.

The **livestock gray zone warfare** documents are the most critical part of the mission. If you are playing in a squad, only the person who needs the task needs to pick them up, but it is recommended that everyone clears the area first to ensure a safe transition to the LZ.

## Extraction and Reporting

After securing the documents, your final hurdle is getting out alive. The Spielberg area often sees "hot" extractions, meaning AI may follow you to the LZ or other PMCs may be waiting for their own transport.

*   **Check the Map:** Ensure your chosen LZ isn't currently being camped. If you see red smoke or hear heavy gunfire, consider rotating to a secondary LZ.
*   **Ammo Check:** Before leaving the repair shop, ensure you have enough ammunition for the trek back. It is easy to burn through magazines while clearing the shop and the house.
*   **Report to Banshee:** Once back at base, open your vendors' menu, select Banshee, and hand over the documents to complete the **livestock gray zone warfare** task.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the code 2381 the same for every faction in Gray Zone Warfare?**

A: Yes, as of the 0.4 Spearhead update in 2026, the code **2381** is universal for the **livestock gray zone warfare** task across the Mithras, Crimson Shield, and Lamang Recovery Initiative factions.

**Q: Do I need to find the note if I already know the code?**

A: It is highly recommended to visit the house at 182 127 and look at the note. Sometimes the task objective "Locate the documents' location" will not trigger as complete unless you physically stand in the room where the code is found.

**Q: What should I do if the documents aren't in the office?**

A: If another player has recently taken the documents, you may need to wait for the loot to respawn or join a different server instance. However, quest items in Gray Zone Warfare usually have a high respawn rate or are instanced for players on the same task.

**Q: Can I complete the livestock gray zone warfare mission solo?**

A: It is possible but difficult. The AI in Spielberg are aggressive and often travel in groups of three or four. Bringing a friend to watch your back while you enter the keypad code is the safest way to ensure success.
