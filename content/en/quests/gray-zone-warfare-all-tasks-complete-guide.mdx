## Starter Tasks: The First Recon Cycle

When you first arrive at your base camp, your immediate priority is the "First Recon" cycle. These missions are mirrored across all three factions, though the specific town names (Nam Thaven, Pha Lang, or Kiu Vongsa) will differ based on your choice.

### First Recon (Handshake)
This task requires you to scout three key points of interest in your faction's starting town. You don't need to interact with anything—simply walking near the buildings will trigger the objective completion.

1.  **The Gas Station:** Usually located on the outskirts of town. Watch for 2-3 AI guards patrolling the pumps.
2.  **The Convenience Store:** A large building often central to the town's commercial strip.
3.  **The Demolished Building:** Look for a construction site characterized by a large yellow excavator.

### Meds Wanted (Lab Rat)
Lab Rat requires you to retrieve UNLRA medical supplies. In most starting towns, these are located near the **Water Tower**.
*   **Location:** Look for white medical tents with blue logos.
*   **Objective:** Enter the small building in the northwest corner of the medical site. The supplies are in a white box on the back shelves.
*   **Warning:** If you die while carrying this box, it will despawn. Place it in your backpack and extract immediately.

### Cache Retrieval (Gunny)
This mission introduces the **Tracker** mechanic. You must place GPS trackers on two specific supply caches.
*   **Warehouse Cache:** Find the warehouse on the western edge of town. The cache is a wooden crate with a red top inside a small side room.
*   **Lumberyard Cache:** Located in the shed on the eastern side of the yard. Interact with the red-topped crate to plant the device.

## Mid-Game Transitions: The "Little Bird Down" Chain

Once the initial town is scouted, the **gray zone warfare all tasks complete guide** moves into more dangerous territory. The "Little Bird Down" quest from Handshake is a notorious bottleneck for many players.

### Little Bird Down Walkthrough
You are tasked with finding a crashed helicopter and investigating the fate of its crew. 
*   **The Crash Site:** Locate the Landing Zone (LZ) south of the main gas station. The helicopter is a burnt-out shell hidden in the tree line nearby.
*   **The Crew:** North of the crash site, you will find a small house. Clear the AI enemies outside. Inside, you will find the bodies of the crew, which completes the investigation phase.

### Leave No Man Behind (Gunny)
Directly following the crash investigation, Gunny will ask you to mark a fallen soldier near the **Town Hall**. 
*   **Strategy:** The Town Hall area is heavily guarded. Use a suppressed weapon and move through the backyards to avoid the main street. The body is located in an alleyway just south of the main government building.

## Advanced Tactical Tips for Task Completion

To successfully finish every mission in this **gray zone warfare all tasks complete guide**, you must master the mechanics of Lamang Island.

1.  **The "Tetris" Inventory:** Press **R** to rotate items. Efficient inventory management allows you to carry more quest items and loot simultaneously.
2.  **Corpse Recovery:** If you die, your loot stays on your body. However, if you die a second time before reaching your first corpse, the original loot is lost forever. 
3.  **The Safe Box:** Always place high-value quest items (like the clerk's necklace or trackers) in your safe lockbox. Items inside the lockbox persist even after death.
4.  **Landing Zone (LZ) Management:** When you complete a task, you don't have to run back to base. Open your map, click your base, and "Call Transport." This will designate the nearest unlocked LZ as your pickup point.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find the quest item even though I'm at the right location?**

A: Some quest items in Gray Zone Warfare are physical objects that must be picked up. If another player has recently taken it, you may need to wait for a world reset or check if the item has a specific spawn timer. However, most task-critical items are instanced for each player.

**Q: Do I lose my task progress if I die?**

A: It depends. If the task requires you to "scout" or "kill," your progress is saved. If the task requires you to "retrieve" a physical item (like a folder or a box) and you haven't placed it in your safe box, you will lose the item and must return to the location to pick it up again.

**Q: How do I unlock the Artisan and Turncoat vendors?**

A: These vendors are unlocked by completing specific prerequisite tasks for Lab Rat and Handshake. Specifically, completing "Native Negotiations" for Lab Rat is a key trigger for expanding your vendor list.

**Q: Can I complete tasks with friends from different factions?**

A: No. You can only squad up and complete tasks with players in your chosen faction. If you want to use this **gray zone warfare all tasks complete guide** with friends, ensure everyone selects the same faction at the character creation screen.
