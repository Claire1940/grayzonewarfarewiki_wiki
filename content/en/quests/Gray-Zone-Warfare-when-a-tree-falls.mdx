<Section icon={Map} title="Navigating the Sawmill & Finding Keys">

The Sawmill is situated in a heavily forested valley, making it a hotbed for ambushes. Unlike early-game starter towns, the AI enemies here are aggressive rebels equipped with body armor and assault rifles. Entering this area unprepared will quickly result in lost kits.

To access the target documents, you will need the Sawmill Office Key. This key spawns randomly on the local guards patrolling the yard. Clear the surrounding area systematically to farm the key if you do not already possess it in your stash.

<Callout type="warning" title="Threat Alert">
Do not attempt to rush the main office without clearing the surrounding high ground. Snipers frequently spawn on the hills overlooking the logging yard.
</Callout>

When clearing the yard, prioritize targets near the log piles. Use the machinery for cover as you advance towards the two-story office building. If the key does not drop from the first wave of defenders, retreat to a safe distance to let the area reset, or coordinate with squadmates who may already have the office door unlocked.
