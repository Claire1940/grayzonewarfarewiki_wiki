Achieving a consistent **Gray Zone Warfare one shot one kill** dynamic requires a fundamental shift in how you evaluate shooter damage. <Section icon={Shield} title="Calber & Ammunition Tiers for OSOK Operations">

Choosing the correct ammunition type is the most critical decision when configuring your loadout. A high-tier weapon firing budget rounds will fail to penetrate basic military-grade armor. The ballistics engine prioritizes bullet caliber size and material composition. For long-distance tactical operations, the 7.62x51mm NATO round serves as the gold standard for high-damage penetration, while intermediate calibers like the 5.56x45mm NATO require top-tier AP rounds to guarantee lethal penetration.

Operating as a marksman on Lamang Island requires a disciplined sequence of steps. Rushing into a tactical position without calculating your escape routes, landing zones, and windage will quickly result in a lost gear kit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select and Verify Landing Zone">

Utilize the 4-second invulnerability window immediately after offboarding the helicopter to scan your immediate tree line for campers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure High-Ground Elevation">
    Navigate around the perimeter of your target area. Avoid walking through open valleys or roads. Establish a firing position on high ground overlooking entry checkpoints or quest-objective locations. Maintain a distance of at least 150 meters to minimize the risk of AI spotting your silhouette.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assess Target Armor Profile">
    Zoom in with your high-magnification optic to identify the target's gear. Check if they are wearing military helmets (Class III+) or simple baseball caps. If the target is heavily armored, align your crosshair carefully to target the exposed face zone or the center of the chest plate with armor-piercing ammunition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Reposition">
    Control your breathing to eliminate reticle sway. Fire a single round, then instantly break your ADS stance. AI units and enemy operators will track the sound signature of your shot. Shift your position laterally by at least 20 to 30 meters along the ridge line before re-engaging to keep the enemy confused.
  </Step>
</Steps>
