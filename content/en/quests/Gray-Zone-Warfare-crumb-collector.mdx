### Maximizing Your Sales

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Know Your Vendors">
    Each vendor specializes in certain item categories. For instance, Handshake often pays better for tactical gear, while Gunny is best for weapons and ammo. Lab Rat typically offers the best prices for medical supplies and food.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Consult Price Guides">
    Use in-game information or external resources (like the Gray Zone Warfare Wiki) to identify which vendor offers the highest price for specific valuable items. Selling to the "best buyer" can yield up to 50% more cash than selling to a general vendor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Valuables">
    When inventory space is tight, always prioritize bringing back "valuables" (e.g., gold chains, rare electronics, specific quest items) that have a high sell price per slot. These are your true "crumbs."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sell Low-Value Items Locally">
    For common, low-value items that don't have a significant price difference between vendors, it's often more efficient to sell them to the closest vendor rather than wasting time traveling.
  </Step>
</Steps>
