## Quest Overview: Fire and Water

The "Fire and Water" quest focuses on two primary landmarks in your starting town. Depending on your chosen faction (Mithras Liberation Front, Lamang Recovery Initiative, or Crimson Shield International), the names of the towns will differ, but the objective remains identical: survey the local fuel supply and the water distribution center.

| Objective | Location Type | Primary Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Survey the Gas Station** | Infrastructure | Locate the fuel pumps/storage |
| **Survey the Water Tower** | Utility | Locate the pumping station/tower |
| **Report to Handshake** | Base of Operations | Hand in the task for rewards |

### Quest Rewards
Upon successful completion in 2026, players can expect the following rewards delivered to their locker:
*   **$3,200 - $4,500** (Scales with faction reputation)
*   **Experience Points (XP)** to level up your PMC
*   **Reputation +100** with Handshake
*   **Tactical Gear** (Usually a mix of ammunition and basic armor)

## Step 1: Locating the "Fire" (The Gas Station)

The first half of the **gray zone warfare fire and water quest guide** involves scouting the gas station. This area is a hotspot for AI scavengers who often patrol the perimeter and the interior convenience store.

1.  **Approach with Caution:** Use the treeline or nearby ditches to approach the gas station. Avoid running down the main road, as AI can spot you from a significant distance.
2.  **Identify the Objective:** You generally need to get within 10-15 meters of the fuel pumps for the "Survey" objective to tick over on your map.
3.  **Clear the Interior:** If you need to enter the building for loot, check the cash registers. These often contain physical cash which stacks in your inventory, providing a nice bonus to your mission payout.

> **⚠️ Warning:** AI at the gas station frequently hide behind cars. Be careful of "line of sight" bugs where they can see your feet under the vehicle while you cannot see them.

## Step 2: Locating the "Water" (The Pumping Station)

The second objective is usually located on the opposite side of the town or near a major water source like a river or a large tower.

1.  **Check your Map (M):** Look for the blue quest circle labeled "Water." 
2.  **The Pumping House:** Look for a small industrial building with large pipes leading into the ground. You may need to enter the building to trigger the completion.
3.  **High Ground:** If your town has a water tower, climb the ladder if possible. This provides an excellent vantage point to clear any remaining AI before you move in to trigger the objective.

## Essential Medical Prep for 2026

Even in the starter zones, a single lucky shot from an AI can put you in a coma if you aren't prepared. Following the medical system updates in 0.4, you should always carry a "Hard Case" in your safe pocket containing the following:

| Med Item | Function | Usage Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **CT (Tourniquet)** | Stops Heavy Bleeding | **High** - Use immediately |
| **Bandage** | Fixes Medium Wounds | Medium |
| **Splint** | Repairs Fractures | Low |
| **Blood Bag** | Restores Health Pool | **Critical** - Use if blood is below 50% |
| **Surgical Kit** | Heals Damaged Organs | Post-Combat only |

> **💡 Tip:** If you have a "Light Bleed," don't waste a bandage. In the 2026 version of Gray Zone Warfare, light bleeds often clot on their own within 2-3 minutes, saving you resources.

## Tactical Tips for Survival

*   **Mute the Heli:** The helicopter rides can be deafening. Press the **O** key to lower the volume during transport so you can still hear your squadmates in Discord or VOIP.
*   **Check Every Body:** AI in starter zones often carry keys. Even if you don't need them for the Fire and Water quest, these keys might unlock high-tier loot rooms in missions like "Restore Order."
*   **Use Cover:** Never stand in the open while reloading. The AI in GZW has become much more aggressive in 2026, often flanking your last known position.
*   **Double-Click Looting:** To speed up your looting process, double-click items in an enemy's inventory to quickly see their contents, then press Tab to exit immediately.

## Handing in the Quest

Once both objectives are marked as "Complete" in your task menu, you must safely extract. Call in a transport helicopter to your nearest Landing Zone (LZ). 

*   **Open the Map (M)**
*   **Select your LZ (e.g., Charlie 1)**
*   **Wait for the Green Icon:** Ensure you only board the green helicopter icon on your mini-map; blue helicopters belong to other squads and may not be heading to your base.

After arriving at base, go to the **Vendors** tab, select **Handshake**, and click **Complete** on the Fire and Water task. All rewards will be sent directly to your Global Locker.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the gas station for the Fire and Water quest?**

A: The location varies by faction. For Mithras, it is in the southeast of Nam Thaven. For Crimson Shield, it is located in the northern sector of Kiue Vong Can. Always check the yellow/blue circles on your tactical map (M) for the exact coordinates.

**Q: Do I need to survive the extraction to complete the quest?**

A: Yes. While the objectives "tick" as you visit them, you must successfully return to base and interact with Handshake to claim your rewards and officially finish the mission. If you die before extracting, you may need to revisit the locations.

**Q: What is the best weapon for the Fire and Water quest?**

A: As mentioned in our **gray zone warfare fire and water quest guide**, the M870 shotgun is the most cost-effective choice for 2026. It reliably drops starter AI with a single shot to the center mass, allowing you to save your expensive M4s for higher-tier POIs like Midnight Sapphire.

**Q: Can I do this quest in a squad?**

A: Absolutely. Completing objectives in a squad is highly recommended. Only one person needs to trigger the location for the whole squad to receive credit, provided everyone is within the immediate vicinity.
