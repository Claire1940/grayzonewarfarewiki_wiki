While helicopters offer immense tactical advantages, neglecting safety protocols can quickly turn a strategic advantage into a costly mistake. Adhering to best practices ensures you maximize the benefits of **Gray Zone Warfare from the sky** without unnecessary risks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Communicate LZ Intent">
