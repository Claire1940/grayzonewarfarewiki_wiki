<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Task Walkthrough">

Completing this task requires a mix of clean navigation, tactical positioning, and swift extraction. Use the steps below to prepare, execute, and complete the mission.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Gear and Secure Storage">
    Equip a reliable weapon such as an M4A1 or AK-74M. Make sure you have at least two spare magazines, a pocket full of loose ammunition, and essential medical supplies like tourniquets and splints. Clear a 2x2 space in your Secure Lockbox. This ensures that if you are eliminated by hostile players or AI after picking up the intel, the quest item remains protected in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Insert via the Closest Landing Zone">
    Call in a helicopter transport to the nearest Landing Zone (LZ) outside your faction's starter town. For Crimson operators, this is typically LZ INDIA or nearby insertion points. Upon landing, utilize the 4-second invulnerability window to scan your immediate surroundings for threat indicators. Move quickly into cover to avoid drawing early attention from nearby patrolling rebels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Approach the Crossroads Checkpoint">
    Move toward the crossroads coordinates designated for your faction. Avoid running along the center of the main roads; instead, stick to tree lines, fences, and building walls to minimize your silhouette. Listen closely for audio cues, as the AI patrols often shout warnings before engaging. Use a suppressed weapon if possible to eliminate guards quietly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Infiltrate the Main Building and Retrieve Intel">
    Locate the target structure at the crossroads. Enter the building, clear any remaining hostiles room by room, and look for a wooden desk or table. The intelligence folder will be sitting on top of the desk. Interact with the folder to pick it up, and immediately drag it from your physical backpack or rig into your Secure Lockbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract and Turn in the Task">
    Retreat toward your extraction LZ using a different path than your insertion route to avoid campers. Call for extraction, keep your weapon raised, and watch the tree lines until the helicopter arrives. Once you safely return to the Forward Operating Base (FOB), open your vendor menu, select Handshake, navigate to Tasks, and click Hand In to receive your rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Out for AI Respawn Loops">
Starter towns in Gray Zone Warfare have high player traffic, which triggers rapid AI respawn loops. If you spend too much time looting after securing the intel, you may find yourself surrounded by newly spawned guards. Secure the quest item, verify its safety in your lockbox, and prioritize immediate extraction over unnecessary engagements.
</Callout>

</Section>
