Before heading into the field, it is important to understand the parameters of the mission. The Livestock task is generally assigned by Turncoat or Handshake, depending on your faction progress, and takes place near a heavily guarded industrial sector.

## Phase 1: Locating the Wooden Shack

The first step of the **gray zone warfare livestock quest** involves scouting a specific wooden shack. This structure serves as the initial waypoint and is located near the car repair shop.

To find it, approach the car repair shop from the southwest. Look for a small, weathered wooden building situated at the bottom-left corner of the shop’s perimeter. You do not necessarily need to find a specific item inside this shack during the first phase; simply entering or standing in close proximity to the structure will usually trigger the "Objective Complete" notification for the scouting portion of the task.

### Scouting Checklist
1.  **Approach from the South:** Avoid the main road to minimize visibility.
2.  **Identify the Shack:** It is a small, standalone wooden structure.
3.  **Clear the Perimeter:** Check the tall grass nearby for prone AI combatants.
4.  **Confirm Objective:** Wait for the UI notification before moving to the next phase.

## Phase 2: Finding the Car Office Code

Once the shack has been scouted, you cannot immediately access the locked documents. You must first find the keypad code. Many players make the mistake of trying to "brute force" the lock or searching the repair shop itself, but the code is actually located further north.

Travel to the northern edge of the mission circle. You are looking for a small office or security post. Inside, you will find a note or a terminal containing the **Car Office Code**. While some codes in Gray Zone Warfare are static, others can vary by server instance or update. Based on recent intelligence, the code **2896** is frequently used, but you should always verify it at the northern location to ensure the quest log updates correctly.

| Code Location | Description | Visual Cue |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

## Phase 3: Retrieving the Documents

With the code in hand, return to the initial objective area near the wooden shack. The documents are housed in a reinforced section of the building, often referred to as the "Car Office."

The entrance to this office is typically located at the rear of the building. Look for a door secured with a digital padlock. Interact with the keypad and enter the code you retrieved in Phase 2. Once inside, the documents are usually found on a desk or inside a filing cabinet. 

> **💡 Tip:** Once you pick up the documents, they occupy a slot in your quest inventory. If you die before extracting, you may need to restart the retrieval process, though some progress checkpoints might remain active.

## Tactical Advice for Solo Players

Completing the **gray zone warfare livestock quest** as a solo operative requires a stealthier approach than squad play. The AI in the 0.4 Spearhead update has been tuned to be more reactive to sound and muzzle flashes.

1.  **Use a Suppressor:** This is non-negotiable for solo players. Reducing your acoustic footprint allows you to pick off guards around the wooden shack without alerting the entire repair shop.
2.  **High Ground Advantage:** Before entering the shack area, scout from the hills to the west. Use your rangefinder to mark enemy positions.
3.  **Patience is Key:** If the car repair shop is "hot" (lots of gunfire), wait for other players to clear the area or for the AI to reset their patrol paths.
4.  **Extraction Pathing:** Don't take the most direct route to the Landing Zone (LZ). Circle back through the woods to avoid campers who may be watching the main roads.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the wooden shack for the gray zone warfare livestock quest?**

A: The wooden shack is located at the bottom-left (southwest) corner of the Car Repair Shop area. It is a small, standalone structure that serves as the first objective point for the mission.

**Q: Do I need to find the code every time I attempt the mission?**

A: If you have already found the code and it is logged in your quest notes, you can usually proceed directly to the keypad. However, if the server instance resets or your quest progress isn't tracking, you may need to revisit the northern security post.

**Q: What should I do if the car office door won't open?**

A: Ensure you have the correct code from the northern location. In some cases, players try to use codes from older versions of the game. In the 0.4 update, always double-check the note in the northern office to confirm the digits.

**Q: Can I share the code with my teammates?**

A: Yes, if you are in a squad, one person finding the code usually allows the entire team to progress, provided they are all on the same stage of the **gray zone warfare livestock quest**. However, each player must physically pick up the documents to complete their individual task.
