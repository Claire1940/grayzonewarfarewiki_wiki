## Tactical Walkthrough: Eyes and Ears

The **gray zone warfare eyes and ears quest** typically centers around the village of Ban Pa or the outskirts of your faction's starting town (Pha Lang, Nam Thaven, or Kiu Vongsa). For most operators, the challenge lies in the high density of AI "Militia Forces" who, while poorly equipped, utilize erratic guerrilla tactics that can easily overwhelm an unprepared scout.

### Phase 1: The Infiltration
It is recommended to land at the closest available Landing Zone (LZ) and approach the town from the tree line. Avoid main roads, as these are often death traps silhouetted against the horizon. Utilize the "Slow is smooth, smooth is fast" mantra.

### Phase 2: Identifying Entry Points
The entry points are usually marked by roadblocks or discarded civilian vehicles. You do not need to clear the entire town to satisfy the objective.
1.  **Northern Entry:** Move to the northern perimeter. Once the HUD notification "Objective Updated" appears, immediately rotate to the flank.
2.  **Southern Entry:** Skirt the eastern or western edge of the town to reach the southern side. Avoid the "Fatal Funnel" of the main street.

### Phase 3: The Observation Post
The observation post is often a two-story structure or a makeshift shack with a clear line of sight over the valley. Look for buildings with sandbags or radio equipment on the roof. Approaching this area requires the highest level of stealth.

## Survival and Health Management

Lamang is unforgiving. During the **gray zone warfare eyes and ears quest**, a single stray round from a rebel AK-47 can cause severe bleeding or organ damage. Unlike arcade shooters, you cannot simply wait for your health to regenerate. You must actively treat your wounds based on their severity.

### Triage Priority List
If you take fire while scouting, seek cover immediately and follow this triage order to stay alive:

| Priority | Condition | Immediate Action | Item Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Severe Bleeding | Stop blood loss instantly | **Tourniquet** |
| **2** | Medium Bleeding | Bandage the wound | **Bandage/Gauze** |
| **3** | Organ Damage | Repair vital functions | **Surgery Kit** |
| **4** | Fractures | Stabilize limbs | **Splint** |
| **5** | Residual Pain | Manage tremors/sway | **Painkillers** |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Applying a tourniquet stops the bleeding but does not heal the wound. You must eventually use a bandage to begin the actual healing process, indicated by a green marker on your health screen.

## Recommended Loadout for Reconnaissance

For the **gray zone warfare eyes and ears quest**, mobility is your greatest asset. Overloading your character will drain your stamina faster and prevent you from jumping or vaulting during a hasty retreat.

### The "Light Scout" Setup
*   **Weapon:** M4A1 or AK-74N with a 1x/4x optic (like the Spectre) for identifying targets at range.
*   **Ammo:** At least 3 spare magazines filled with **M855** or **7N6** rounds.
*   **Armor:** IIIA or III rated vest. Level IV is often too heavy for pure recon missions.
*   **Medical:** 2x Bandages, 1x Tourniquet, 1x Splint, and 1x Small Blood Bag.

### Stamina and Hydration
Your blood regeneration speed is directly tied to your hydration and energy levels. If you are dehydrated, your character will experience blurry vision and dizziness, making it nearly impossible to spot enemies during the **gray zone warfare eyes and ears quest**.

*   **Hydration:** Always carry at least 1.0L of water.
*   **Energy:** Carry an energy bar or MRE to keep your stamina regeneration at peak levels.

## Advanced Movement Tactics

Mastering movement is essential for survival in the Gray Zone. When approaching quest objectives, use the environment to mask your silhouette.

1.  **Stay Off the Skyline:** Avoid walking along the ridges of hills. You become an easy target for AI snipers.
2.  **Crouch-Walk in High-Threat Areas:** This reduces your noise profile and improves your weapon stability.
3.  **Use the Lean Mechanic:** Never expose your entire body when checking a corner. Use 'Q' and 'E' to peek incrementally (slicing the pie).
4.  **Bounding Overwatch:** If playing in a squad, have one player move while the other provides cover.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly are the entry points for the Eyes and Ears quest?**

A: While locations can vary slightly based on your faction, they are generally located at the main road entrances to the primary mission village. Look for the "Objective Updated" notification on your HUD when you are within 15 meters of the town's perimeter fences.

**Q: Do I have to kill the enemies at the observation post?**

A: No. The **gray zone warfare eyes and ears quest** only requires you to "locate and identify" the post. You can complete this objective using stealth or by viewing the building through a scoped optic from a safe distance.

**Q: What happens if I die after completing the objectives but before extracting?**

A: In Gray Zone Warfare, quest progress for "scouting" usually persists after death, but you must still return to the FOB to "hand in" the task to Handshake. However, you will lose all gear on your body unless you can navigate back to your corpse within 60 minutes.

**Q: Can I use a helicopter to scout the locations?**

A: No. You must be on foot to trigger the location-based objectives. However, you can use the helicopter's 4-second protection window upon landing to quickly move to nearby cover and begin your approach.

**Q: Why isn't my stamina regenerating?**

A: Check your health screen ('U' key). You likely have a fracture or are suffering from "Exhaustion." If you are dehydrated or hungry, your stamina regeneration will be significantly throttled. Drink water and rest in a crouched position to recover.
