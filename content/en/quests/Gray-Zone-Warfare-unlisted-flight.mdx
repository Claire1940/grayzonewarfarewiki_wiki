The **Gray Zone Warfare unlisted flight** task is a critical early-to-midgame mission issued by Handshake. This operation requires PMCs to investigate a radar anomaly corresponding to an unregistered flight that went down on Lamang Island. Your objective is to locate the crash site, search the debris field, retrieve the aircraft's flight recorder, and extract back to the Forward Operating Base (FOB) safely.

Before deploying to the active zone, you must prepare your character for sustained forest engagement. The airfield perimeter is heavily patrolled by local militia who carry assault rifles. Attempting this run without proper armor and high-penetration ammunition will result in a rapid extraction failure.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Steps to Complete the Gray Zone Warfare Unlisted Flight">

Completing the task requires a methodical approach to searching the crash site. Follow these steps to locate the flight recorder, secure the evidence, and return to safety.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Mission and Prepare Gear">
    Interact with Handshake at the FOB menu. Accept the task and verify you have a 1x1 space open in your Secure Lockbox. Equip a weapon chambered in 5.45x39mm or 5.56x45mm NATO with at least two spare magazines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy to the Airfield Region">
    Open your map and request a helicopter transport to the nearest active LZ near Pha Lang Airfield. Board the helicopter. Remember that you are immune to damage while onboard and for 4 seconds after stepping off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Wreckage Site">
    Move toward coordinates **203 162**. Look for broken tree branches and scattered metal debris. The tail section of the fuselage is painted in a faded gray military pattern and rests partially submerged in the mud.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Flight Recorder">
    Approach the cargo compartment situated near the broken midsection of the aircraft. Search the orange casing container inside the fuselage. Take the document titled "Flight Recorder Data" and place it in your Secure Lockbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract and Turn In">
    Move away from the crash site toward your designated extraction zone. Open your map, call a helicopter, and board it immediately upon arrival. Once back at the FOB, open the vendor menu and hand the item to Handshake.
  </Step>
</Steps>
