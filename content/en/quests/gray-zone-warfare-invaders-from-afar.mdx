<Section icon={Target} title="Phase 1: The Warehouse Investigation">

Your first objective in **gray zone warfare invaders from afar** is to locate leads regarding the hostiles. Intelligence suggests they have been performing field surgeries within one of the central structures. You need to head to the large warehouse located at coordinates **139 144**.

Inside the warehouse, navigate toward the back areas. You are looking for a blue gurney or a makeshift surgery table. The area is visually distinct, often covered in blood and medical debris, indicating that the hostiles were treating significant casualties here. Interacting with this table will trigger the next stage of the task.

| Objective | Coordinate | Interaction Point |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Search for Leads** | 139 144 | Blue Surgery Gurney |
| **Enemy Presence** | High | 4-6 AI Guards inside/near |
| **Cover Availability** | Good | Use industrial crates for shielding |

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Entry">
Enter the warehouse from the northern side to avoid the main road. This provides better concealment and allows you to clear the interior rooms one by one before reaching the surgery table.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Phase 2: Recovering the Medic's Notes">

Once you have inspected the surgery site, you must find documentation that explains the medical situation of the invaders. This requires moving from the warehouse to the Sawmill Office complex, located at coordinates **139 143**.

The office is a smaller, separate building. You do not need a key to enter the main office area where the notes are located. Look for a desk inside the primary office room; the **Medic's Notes** will be sitting clearly on the tabletop. Picking these up is essential for completing the "Locate the medic's notes" objective.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Exit the Warehouse">
    Exit through the western door of the warehouse to minimize exposure to the main courtyard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Office">
    Look for the administrative building located directly adjacent to the main processing area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure the Notes">
    Enter the office, clear any lingering AI, and grab the notebook from the desk at 139 143.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note Mechanics">
The Medic's Notes are a physical quest item. If you are eliminated before extracting or completing the final objective, you may need to return to this location to retrieve them again, depending on your current quest progress state.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Phase 3: The Deserters' Fate">

The final part of the **gray zone warfare invaders from afar** task takes you away from the industrial center of the Sawmill and toward the outskirts. You must investigate what happened to the deserters who fled the main facility.

Navigate to the fishing village/hut located at coordinates **141 145**. This area is more rural and offers less hard cover than the Sawmill. Your goal is to find a specific corpse. Look for a body leaning against a stack of ammo crates near the first major hut in the village. Interacting with this corpse completes the investigation.

| Location Type | Coordinate | Key Landmark |

| **Fishing Hut** | 141 145 | Ammo Crates / First Hut |
