The **Gray Zone Warfare Freedom for All** task represents a major progression milestone for private military company (PMC) operators on Lamang Island. Issued during the mid-game transition phase, this contract demands a deep understanding of tactical maneuvering, area clearance, and extraction security. Unlike early-game introductory tasks, this deployment places you directly in conflict with well-armed local factions and hostile rebels who defend their strongholds aggressively.

Before deploying from your Forward Operating Base (FOB), understanding the logistical parameters of the operation is critical for survival. The contract requires precise navigation to specific grid coordinates, tactical engagement at close to medium ranges, and the secure retrieval or activation of mission assets.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough and Infiltration Route">

Completing the mission efficiently requires a disciplined approach to movement and combat. Navigating through dense jungle environments to reach the objective area exposes players to ambushes from local rebel forces. Utilizing the helicopter transport network correctly provides both safe insertion and tactical misdirection.

Remember that upon stepping off the helicopter at the designated landing zone, operators receive a brief 4-second invulnerability window. Use these precious seconds to scan the immediate tree lines, identify nearby cover, and coordinate your squad's moving vectors.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Insertion Note">
The sound of an arriving helicopter acts as a loud acoustic distraction for both AI combatants and enemy players. Use the ambient noise to mask your initial push into the objective perimeter.
</Callout>

Follow these sequential steps to execute the mission parameters flawlessly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Landing Zone and Approach Vectors">
    Fly into the regional LZ (such as LZ INDIA for Ban Pa operations). Upon landing, immediately drop into a prone or crouched position behind natural defilade. Avoid pushing directly down the main roads, as AI defenders frequently patrol primary asphalt routes. Instead, execute a wide flanking maneuver through the jungle brush to approach the northern or southern entry points safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Perimeter Defenders and Patrols">
    Before entering the dense urban structures or village compounds, eliminate the outer guard pickets. Use suppressed weapons to avoid alerting the entire zone simultaneously. AI combatants in this tier utilize basic body armor and will call for reinforcements if a firefight becomes prolonged. Maintain a steady pace and check your corners diligently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate and Interact with the Mission Objective">
    Navigate to the designated grid coordinates within the compound. If the objective is locked behind a secure door, you must farm local enemies to obtain the necessary regional key (such as the Ban Pa Elder's Room Key or local storage keys). Once inside the target room, interact with the physical asset or intel document to update your task log.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish a Secure Extraction Chain">
    With the objective secured, open your tactical map and call for an extraction helicopter at the nearest safe LZ. Do not run in a straight line toward the landing point; utilize low ground and dense foliage to break line of sight. Wait in deep cover near the LZ until the helicopter physically touches down to minimize your exposure to counter-attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Optimal Loadouts and Gear Selection">

Surviving high-intensity operations requires a balanced loadout tailored to the engagement distances of the mission zone. The latest tactical updates emphasize physical weapon mechanics, meaning that total weapon weight directly influences your ergonomics, weapon sway, and aim-down-sights (ADS) acceleration. Carrying an overly heavy rifle will rapidly deplete your stamina during intense firefights.

Your choice of ammunition is arguably more critical than the weapon platform itself. Standard ballistics calculations dictate that armor-piercing rounds are mandatory when dealing with armored LAF soldiers or elite rebel leaders.
