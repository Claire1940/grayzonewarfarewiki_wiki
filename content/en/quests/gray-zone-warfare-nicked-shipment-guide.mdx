Before heading into the field, you must accept the task from your vendor (usually Gunny or Handshake, depending on your faction). The mission objectives are straightforward but can be deadly if you are unprepared for the local insurgent presence.

| Objective | Description | Location |

## Locating the Nicked Shipment

The "Nicked Shipment" truck is typically located on the outskirts of the main town area, often near a gas station or a construction yard. While the exact coordinates vary by faction, look for a large civilian transport truck that looks out of place or surrounded by debris.

### Truck Locations by Faction Town

| Faction | Town | Landmarks to Look For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mithras** | Nam Thaven | Near the northern gas station/market area. |
| **Lamang** | Pha Lang | Close to the western construction site entrance. |
| **Crimson** | Kiu Vongsa | South-eastern industrial sector near the warehouses. |

To complete the **gray zone warfare nicked shipment guide** objective, you must physically stand near the rear of the truck. You will see a prompt to "Inspect" or "Search" the cargo. Once the task update notification appears on the top right of your HUD, the objective is complete.

## Clearing the Area and Extracting

The AI surrounding the shipment are usually lightly armed but can overwhelm you with numbers. Use the environment to your advantage. 2026 tactical updates suggest using "Cops" (Capture Points) as temporary safety zones. If you have captured a nearby COP, it will turn blue on your map, providing a safe haven with friendly guards.

1.  **Approach with Caution:** Use hard cover like walls and parked cars.
2.  **Center Mass Shots:** If using the recommended M870, aim for the chest. The buckshot spread is lethal at close to medium range.
3.  **Loot for Vulture:** If you find high-value military equipment on the AI, save it. You can sell these items to the secret vendor, **Vulture**, to increase your reputation and unlock large safes.
4.  **Check for Keys:** AI in these areas frequently drop mission-critical keys. Always double-click their inventory to check for yellow-highlighted items.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rush the truck. AI often hide inside nearby buildings or behind the truck itself. Clear the perimeter 360 degrees before interacting with the cargo.

## FAQ

**Q: I found the truck, but the mission won't update. What should I do?**

A: Ensure you are at the correct truck. Many towns have multiple decorative vehicles. The mission truck is usually highlighted by specific props like crates or an open back. Walk completely around the vehicle until the "Task Updated" notification appears. This **gray zone warfare nicked shipment guide** recommends checking the very back of the trailer.

**Q: Can I complete Nicked Shipment in Joint Operations (PvE)?**

A: Yes. The mission is identical in both Warfare (PvP) and Joint Operations (PvE) modes. If you are struggling with other players camping mission objectives, switching to Joint Operations is a valid strategy to finish your tasks in peace.

**Q: What are the rewards for completing Nicked Shipment in 2026?**

A: Usually, you will receive a mix of cash (approx. 3,000–4,500), reputation with the specific vendor, and sometimes a basic weapon or armor piece. These rewards are sent directly to your "Messenger" tab or your Locker at base.

**Q: Do I need a key for the truck?**

A: No, the Nicked Shipment truck itself does not require a key. However, the AI guarding it may carry keys for nearby buildings that contain high-value loot or are required for subsequent missions.
