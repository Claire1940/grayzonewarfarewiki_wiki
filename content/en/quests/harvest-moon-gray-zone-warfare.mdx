In the unforgiving jungles of Lamang, navigating the complex web of local narcotics production is essential for gaining favor with the game's more clandestine vendors. The **harvest moon gray zone warfare** task, assigned by the vendor Banshee, is a multi-stage investigation that requires players to infiltrate several high-risk areas to uncover the truth behind the region's poppy production. This mission isn't just about finding a single item; it is an endurance test that takes you across various coordinates, each guarded by hostile AI and potentially rival players.

Completing the **harvest moon gray zone warfare** mission requires a blend of tactical patience and precise navigation. You will be tasked with locating four distinct hidden poppy fields, investigating specific documents at each site, and reporting your findings back to Banshee. Because these locations are spread across the map—ranging from the F.A.L. N.G. ## Task Overview and Requirements

Before heading out into the field, it is vital to understand what Banshee expects. Unlike some tasks that simply require a "kill and confirm" approach, Harvest Moon is an intelligence-gathering mission. You are looking for proof of labor exploitation and illicit shipping agreements.

## Preparing for the Harvest Moon Mission

Success in the **harvest moon gray zone warfare** quest depends heavily on your loadout. Since you will be traveling through open fields and dense jungle, a suppressed weapon is highly recommended to avoid drawing the attention of every AI squad in the vicinity.

1.  **Weaponry:** An assault rifle with a 4x or 6x optic is ideal. The poppy fields often have long sightlines, but the buildings containing the documents require close-quarters readiness.
2.  **Medical Supplies:** Carry at least two CAT tourniquets and several bandages. The AI near the third poppy field is notoriously aggressive.
3.  **Hydration:** Bring two large water bottles. The hike between the airfield fields and the southern fields can lead to exhaustion in the 2026 humidity.

## Location 1: Eastern Poppy Field (192, 154)

The first stop on your investigation is located to the east of the F.A.L. N.G. Airfield. This area is relatively open, making you a target for snipers if you approach from the main roads. The goal here is to locate the "Labor Allocation Request."

Upon reaching the coordinates **192, 154**, look for a small cluster of structures near the edge of the poppy rows. Inside the primary work shed, you will find a piece of paper resting on a table or crate. This is the Labor Allocation Request. You must interact with it to "Read" or "Translate" the document. Once the task notification updates in the top-right corner of your HUD, you are clear to move to the next objective.

## Location 2: South Falcon Field (178, 152)

The second field is situated just south of the Falcon (F.A.L. N.G.) area. This location is often a hotbed for player activity because of its proximity to the airfield's loot-heavy zones.

At coordinates **178, 152**, you will find an open-faced building or a pavilion-style structure. Unlike the first location, the document here is the "Weekly Shipment Note." It details the logistics of how the poppy harvest is moved out of the region.

## Location 3: The Northern High-Threat Field (172, 132)

This is arguably the most difficult part of the **harvest moon gray zone warfare** task. Located at **172, 132**, this field is known as "Hidden Poppy Field 3." If you are a member of the Mithras faction, the walk is slightly shorter, but for others, it is a significant trek through dense foliage.

Expect to encounter at least 6 to 10 AI combatants here. They are better equipped than the scavengers found in the starter towns, often wearing body armor and using higher-caliber ammunition. 

**Steps to secure the document:**
1.  Approach from the high ground to the west to scout the area.
2.  Eliminate the sentries near the perimeter before entering the poppy rows.
3.  Locate the green folder titled "Protective Oversight Agreement" inside the main office area.
4.  Interact with the folder to translate the contents.

> **Warning:** Do not rush into the building. AI often hides in the corners or behind crates, waiting for players to interact with the quest item.

## Location 4: Southern YBL Field (149, 117)

The final piece of the puzzle lies at the far southern end of the map, below the Lima 2 landing zone and near the YBL-1 bunker complex. The coordinates for this final field are **149, 117**.

This location features a more residential-style house compared to the industrial sheds of the previous fields. You aren't looking for a loose paper or a folder here; instead, you are searching for a book. Walk up to the front porch of the main house. At the end of the porch, you will find the "Harvest Discipline Notice." Translate this final document to complete the investigation phase of the task.

## Finalizing the Task with Banshee

Once all four documents have been read and translated, your task list will update to "Report findings to Banshee." You do not need to extract with physical items in your inventory for this specific quest, as the "Read" action counts as the intelligence gathering. However, you must survive the extraction to turn in the quest at the base camp.

Return to your faction's headquarters and open the vendor menu for Banshee. Handing in the **harvest moon gray zone warfare** task will typically reward you with a significant amount of experience points, reputation with Banshee (which unlocks higher-tier equipment like suppressors and optics), and a cash reward to offset your deployment costs.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to visit the poppy fields in a specific order?**

A: No, you can investigate the fields in any order that suits your flight path. Most players prefer to start at the airfield (North) and work their way down to YBL (South) to minimize backtracking.

**Q: What happens if I die after reading three of the four documents?**

A: In Gray Zone Warfare, task progress for "reading" or "investigating" usually persists even if you die. You should only need to visit the remaining field(s) you haven't interacted with yet. Always check your task menu after redeploying to confirm.

**Q: Are there any locked rooms in the poppy fields?**

A: Yes, some of the buildings at these coordinates have locked rooms that contain high-tier loot, such as rare weapon parts and medical supplies. While not required for the **harvest moon gray zone warfare** task, bringing along a set of local keys can make the trip much more profitable.

**Q: Why can't I see the document at the coordinates?**

A: Ensure you are at the exact coordinates (e.g., 172, 132). Sometimes documents are tucked under other items or on low shelves. Look for the interaction prompt (usually the 'F' key) when hovering your reticle over desks and tables in the designated buildings.
